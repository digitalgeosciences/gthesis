## Key Findings Across Sources

- Java Island is one of the most seismically active regions in Indonesia due to its location near the Java Trench. ([[2015-latif|Latif, 2015, p. 32]])

- Low-magnitude earthquakes are critical for understanding the mechanisms of larger earthquakes. ([[2021-shaheen|Shaheen, 2021, p. 15]])

- Hypocenter location is a crucial prerequisite for studying the event's seismic activation. ([[2023-suleymanli|Suleymanli, 2023, p. 16]])

- West Java has a complex tectonic setting with a significant history of seismic events. ([[2025-rachmadan|Rachmadan, 2025, p. 19]])
