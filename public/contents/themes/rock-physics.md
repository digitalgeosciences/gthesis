## Key Findings Across Sources

- Model 1, 2, and 3 showed distinct variations in P-wave and S-wave velocities across interfaces. ([[2014-kola|Kola, 2014, p. 42]])

- Several models (stiff-sand, soft-sand, Greenberg & Castagna, Raymer) were evaluated for their ability to represent the clastic reservoir. ([[2016-houssain|Houssain, 2016, p. 17]])

- Synthetic logs generated by forward modeling showed less than 10% error when compared to real well logs. ([[2019-al-dawood|Al-Dawood, 2019, p. 104]])

- Rock physics is often referred to as 'velocity-porosity' science in this context. ([[2020-almumtin|Almumtin, 2020, p. 17]])

- Determined that pore shape (rounded vs. elongated) influenced the Porosity-Acoustic Ratio (PAR). ([[2024-allehyani|allehyani, 2024, p. 38]])
