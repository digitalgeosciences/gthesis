**Summary**: The process of capturing and storing CO2 to mitigate climate change.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- MOF-based systems offer superior capacity compared to traditional materials like zeolites. ([[2014-jamil|JAMIL, 2014, p. 19]])

- Limestone offers high storage potential and the ability to react with CO2 for permanent trapping. ([[2024-suleiman|Suleiman, 2024, p. 38]])

- CO2 hydrates offer a promising solution for long-term, stable carbon storage due to their high density and self-sealing properties. ([[2024-habiburrahman|HABIBURRAHMAN, 2024, p. 39]])

- Surface mineralization is an efficient technology for Saudi Arabia's goal of net-zero emissions by 2060. ([[2025-alghawi|Alghawi, 2025, p. 14]])
