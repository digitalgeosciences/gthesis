## Key Findings Across Sources

- The Sarah Formation is considered a primary reservoir target for tight gas in Saudi Arabia. ([[2014-el-deek|El-Deek, 2014, p. 19]])

- The Sarah Formation is a potential tight gas reservoir characterized by low porosity and very low permeability. ([[2016-ejaz|Ejaz, 2016, p. 13]])

- The formation is characterized by glaciogenic features and heterogeneous lithofacies. ([[2017-alqubalee|Alqubalee, 2017, p. 17]])

- Defined by Saudi Aramco standards as having porosity < 12% and permeability < 1 mD. ([[2020-islam|Islam, 2020, p. 39]])

- Ionic liquids are promising candidates for improving production in tight gas formations due to their stability. ([[2024-mohamed|Mohamed, 2024, p. 21]])
