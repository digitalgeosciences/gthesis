## Key Findings Across Sources

- Optimized production rates from individual laterals by setting downhole valves at appropriate positions. ([[2011-almubarak|ALMUBARAK, 2011, p. 17]])

- A production rate of 40 MSCF/D was identified as a balance for maximizing recovery while managing condensate dropout. ([[1999-fawumi|Fawumi, 1999, p. 100]])
