# Groundwater Quality

**Summary**: The study of the chemical and biological parameters that determine if groundwater is safe for human use.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Deep groundwater was found to be contaminated with BOD, COD, Nitrate, and Nitrite. ([[2011-alsadeq|Alsadeq, 2011, p. 127]])

- Only one well showed a good water type for drinking according to WQI. ([[2019-ahmed-2|Ahmed, 2019, p. 57]])

- Water is suitable for irrigation but requires treatment before drinking. ([[2020-mansouri|Mansouri, 2020, p. 14]])

- Both aquifers are not suitable for domestic drinking water due to high TDS, chloride, and sulfate. ([[1992-hassan|Hassan, 1992, p. 117]])
