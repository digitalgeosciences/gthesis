# Source Rock Evaluation

**Summary**: The assessment of the potential of sedimentary rocks to generate hydrocarbons.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012]] focused on source-rock-evaluation for the Hanadir member.

- [[2016-abbas|Abbas, 2016]] evaluated the source potential of the shale interbeds using TOC analysis.

- [[2017-mohammed|Mohammed, 2017]] performed source-rock-evaluation on the TQMN formation.

- [[2017-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2017]] evaluated the Abu Gabra Formation as a primary source rock.

## Methods and Approaches Used

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Hanadir is a candidate for being a main source of gas in Northwest Saudi Arabia. ([[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012, p. 15]])

- TOC was determined to acquire a generalized trend for the source rock potential of the shale interbeds. ([[2016-abbas|Abbas, 2016, p. 55]])

- TQMN was identified as a high-quality source rock with significant gas potential. ([[2017-mohammed|Mohammed, 2017, p. 130]])

- Source rock for this hydrocarbon is believed to be the lacustrine shale of the Abu Gabra Formation. ([[2017-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2017, p. 64]])
