## Key Findings Across Sources

- Identified 47 species from the Arabian Gulf and various benthic foraminifera in the Dam Formation. ([[2006-al-enezi|Al-Enezi, 2006, p. 59]])

- Numerous foraminifera species were identified, including those from genera such as Siphovalvulina and Riyadhella. ([[2016-malik|Malik, 2016, p. 70]])

- A new genus, Pseudonubeculina, was established based on unique morphological features like a slit-like aperture. ([[2017-amao|Amao, 2017, p. 40]])

- 8209 benthic foraminiferal specimens were analyzed across 50 samples. ([[2022-khamsin|Khamsin, 2022, p. 38]])

- Identified three groups of DWAF: survivor species, extinction species, and incoming species. ([[2024-hikmahtiar|Hikmahtiar, 2024, p. 16]])

- Analysis revealed a diverse assemblage of planktonic, calcareous benthic, and agglutinated foraminifera. ([[2024-alhilali|Alhilali, 2024, p. 18]])
