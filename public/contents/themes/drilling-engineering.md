## Key Findings Across Sources

- Corrosion is a major cause of drill pipe failures and damage to downhole equipment. ([[1994-shokoya|Shokoya, 1994, p. 21]])

- SOH design offers the fastest rotation time but carries significant risks regarding well control and equipment placement. ([[2011-al-amri|Al-Amri, 2011, p. 75]])

- Not stated in source ([[2012-geri|Geri, 2012]])

- Real-time data integration is essential to move from pre-run modeling to active decision making. ([[2013-khamis|Khamis, 2013, p. 24]])

- Optimized parameters (WOB, RPM, flow rate) were found to be more consistent with high-ROP conditions than actual field values. ([[2014-ahmed|Ahmed, 2014, p. 85]])

- Developed a process to produce API-compliant bentonite from local sources. ([[2014-elhag|ELHAG, 2014]])

- All three materials with varying particle sizes and concentrations exhibited improved rheology, filtration and pH. ([[2014-wajheeuddin|Wajheeuddin, 2014, p. 18]])

- Not stated in source ([[2017-al-abduljabbar|Al-AbdulJabbar, 2017]])

- Optimizing mud rheology (K and n) can significantly improve ROP and reduce DSE. ([[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017, p. 123]])

- Not stated in source ([[2019-elzenary|Elzenary, 2019]])

- Drilling fluids with different additives will chemically interact with the reservoir rock... these interactions will change the rock pore system, and consequently, geomechanical properties. ([[2021-serag|SERAG, 2021, p. 17]])

- Optimizing parameters based on MSE can lead to a 20% improvement in ROP. ([[2022-al-dahmoush|Al-Dahmoush, 2022, p. 15]])

- Yield point reached 30.4 lb/ft2 at a barite concentration of 450 gm. ([[2022-aljaberi|AlJaberi, 2022, p. 46]])

- Determined that ilmenite and Micromax are effective alternatives for high-density muds. ([[2023-basfar|Basfar, 2023, p. 32]])

- Well-C experienced a stuck pipe incident due to poor borehole cleaning (TIm = 0.56). ([[2023-murif|Murif, 2023, p. 164]])

- The study highlighted the effectiveness of an eco-friendly polymer combined with locally sourced Saudi bentonite clay in enhancing AAES foam performance. ([[2024-gowida|Gowida, 2024, p. 104]])

- Not stated in source ([[2023-saadeldin|SAADELDIN, 2023]])
