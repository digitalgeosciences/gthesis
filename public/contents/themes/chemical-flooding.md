# Chemical Flooding

**Summary**: The use of chemical agents to enhance the permeability of a reservoir rock.

---

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The waste oil acid showed superior performance over plain HCl at higher injection rates. ([[2017-ahmed-2|AHMED, 2017, p. 80]])

- EDMM and ETMM were shown to be capable of handling concentration equations in addition to saturation equations. ([[2022-adeyemi|Adeyemi, 2022, p. 104]])

- Ionic liquids (Sx1, Sx4) were found to be more effective than commercial chemicals at lower concentrations for reducing MMP and FCMP. ([[2023-al-hakami|Al-Hakami, 2023, p. 138]])

- TCFs like NaNO2 and NH4Cl are common in the industry due to low cost and high heat generation, but require careful management of their reactivity. ([[2024-abdallah|Abdallah, 2024, p. 73]])
