## Key Findings Across Sources

- Determined a partially restricted continental shelf as the depositional environment. ([[1996-matter|Matter, 1996, p. 26]])

- Five distinct microfacies were used to interpret the paleoenvironmental conditions of deposition. ([[1992-el-sahn|El-Sahn, 1992, p. 13]])

- Identified a transition from deep water lime mudstones in the Sulaiy to shallow shelf carbonates in the Shu'aiba. ([[1992-al-sulami|Al-Sulami, 1992, p. 57]])

- Identified several sea level changes, including a major regression at 110,000 y.b.p. and a Holocene transgression. ([[1988-darwish|Darwish, 1988, p. 121]])

- Identified shoaling-upward cycles and high salinity conditions in the Middle Miocene. ([[2006-al-enezi|Al-Enezi, 2006, p. 13]])

- Evidence of dunes and playa lakes indicates a shift to arid conditions in the Late Paleozoic. ([[2011-al-masrahy|AL-MASRAHY, 2011, p. 27]])

- Identified an open-marine shelf environment below storm wave base for the Hanadir member. ([[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012, p. 31]])

- Tabuk shows an increased tidal dominance compared to the Al Ula area. ([[2014-mohammedfaiz|Mohammedfaiz, 2014, p. 25]])

- Identified distinct sedimentary facies and diagenetic signatures for different platform positions. ([[2015-koeshidayatullah|Koeshidayatullah, 2015, p. 111]])

- Identified various settings including alluvial fans, fluvial channels, deltaic systems, and lacustrine basins. ([[2016-awad|Awad, 2016, p. 73]])

- The environment of deposition is considered to be subtidal with facies deposited on the middle portion of a shallow open marine ramp. ([[2016-malik|Malik, 2016, p. 124]])

- Determined that the Dam Formation was deposited in an arid subtropical, hypersaline lagoonal setting. ([[2016-septriandi|Septriandi, 2016, p. 16]])

- Findings serve as a modern analogue for ancient lagoonal sediments of Cenozoic petroleum reservoirs. ([[2017-amao|Amao, 2017, p. 132]])

- Identified five major carbonate environment types from distal-to-proximal ramp settings. ([[2017-yousif|Yousif, 2017, p. 18]])

- Reconstructed a transition from offshore to lower shoreface environments based on lithofacies and foraminifera. ([[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018, p. 53]])

- Glacial deposits are used as indicators for paleoclimate reconstruction in the Arabian Plate. ([[2021-alqarni|Alqarni, 2021, p. 18]])

- The transition from D1A to D2W shows a shift toward shallower, higher-oxygen environments. ([[2022-khamsin|Khamsin, 2022, p. 63]])

- Reconstructed a sequence of marine, lagoonal, and arid-influenced environments over the Pleistocene-Holocene. ([[2022-khan|Khan, 2022, p. 20]])

- Merapi provides a proxy for understanding fluvial, coastal, and aeolian systems on Mars. ([[2022-argadestya|Argadestya, 2022, p. 20]])

- BFAR was used as a proxy for paleo-productivity, showing higher values in Core 25 due to higher TOC levels. ([[2023-nasir|Nasir, 2023, p. 53]])

- Morphogroup analysis provided information about bathymetry and organic carbon flux. ([[2024-hikmahtiar|Hikmahtiar, 2024, p. 47]])

- Foraminiferal assemblages are used as essential tools in paleoclimatology. ([[2024-alhilali|Alhilali, 2024, p. 16]])

- Mg/Ca and Na/Ca ratios were tested to reconstruct temperature and salinity, but both showed limited reliability in high-salinity conditions. ([[2024-alsadah|Alsadah, 2024, p. 14]])

- Determined that the Dhaiqa basin transitioned from clastic-rich to clastic-poor environments, indicating a shift in depositional setting. ([[2024-malik|Malik, 2024, p. 34]])

- Identified transitions from shallow lagoonal to deeper intra-shelf basin settings during peak transgression. ([[2024-jalloh|Jalloh, 2024, p. 16]])

- Identified a transition from shallow platform to deep shelf conditions influenced by tectonic activity. ([[1989-abdou|Abdou, 1989, p. 169]])

- The sedimentary environment is interpreted as a continental margin. ([[1999-babalola|Babalola, 1999, p. 103]])

- Identified several subaerial exposure periods and a major late Pleistocene regression. ([[1988-darwish-2|Darwish, 1988, p. 19]])
