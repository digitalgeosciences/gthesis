# Uncertainty Quantification

**Summary**: The process of identifying and quantifying the range of possible outcomes in a given system.

---

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Probabilistic methodology is an important tool to quantify parameters involved in hydrocarbon volume estimates. ([[2003-al-shridi|Al-Shridi, 2003, p. 12]])

- Velocity model and acquisition geometry are the primary sources of location uncertainty. ([[2015-own|Own, 2015, p. 20]])

- Incorporating the 5% (300m) uncertainty range from magnetic data significantly improved the reliability of the depth-to-basement estimate. ([[2020-abdallah-2|Abdallah, 2020, p. 84]])

- PCE was found to be an efficient alternative to MCS for UQ purposes. ([[2024-astsauri|Astsauri, 2024, p. 13]])
