## Key Findings Across Sources

- Identified specific high-priority zones (Safaniyah, Khursaniyah-Jubail, Tarut Bay) requiring immediate protection during spill events. ([[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016, p. 14]])

- Determined LC50 values for local fish species to assess the impact of crude oil and drilling mud. ([[2018-amiruddin|Amiruddin, 2018, p. 70]])

- Heavy metals like Cr were found to pose hazardous cancer risks in some studied areas. ([[2022-al-harbi|Al-Harbi, 2022, p. 25]])

- Non-carcinogenic hazards were below acceptable bounds, but children showed higher carcinogenic risks. ([[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024, p. 19]])

- Switching fuel to natural gas resulted in significant reductions in SO2 (97.2%) and NO2 (46.9%). ([[2025-al-afif|Al-Afif, 2025, p. 12]])
