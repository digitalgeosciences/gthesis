# Hydrogeology and Hydrology

**Summary**: The study of the distribution and movement of groundwater in the soil or rocks.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Petrographic methods provide a viable shortcut for assessing hydrostratigraphic significance. ([[1995-magaji|Magaji, 1995, p. 17]])

- Assessment included recharge, movement, occurrence, discharge, and storage of the aquifers. ([[1993-moallim|Moallim, 1993, p. 49]])

- The Khobar aquifer is a significant reservoir with high transmissivity but low recharge. ([[2000-mohammad|Mohammad, 2000, p. 30]])

- Groundwater flow within the UER aquifer is directed from the southwest to the northeast toward the Arabian Gulf. ([[2012-iwalewa|Iwalewa, 2012, p. 50]])

- Evaporation rates are critical for determining the economic viability of using brine for salt production. ([[2016-armoh|Armoh, 2016, p. 15]])

- Identified UER as a highly productive but over-exploited fossil aquifer. ([[2019-prasetyo|Prasetyo, 2019, p. 23]])
