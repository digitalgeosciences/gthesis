# Hydrogeology and Hydrology

**Summary**: The study of the distribution and movement of groundwater in the soil or rocks.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Petrographic methods provide a viable shortcut for assessing hydrostratigraphic significance. ([[1995-magaji|Magaji, 1995, p. 17]])

- Assessment included recharge, movement, occurrence, discharge, and storage of the aquifers. ([[1993-moallim|Moallim, 1993, p. 49]])

- The Khobar aquifer is a significant reservoir with high transmissivity but low recharge. ([[2000-mohammad|Mohammad, 2000, p. 30]])

- Groundwater flow within the UER aquifer is directed from the southwest to the northeast toward the Arabian Gulf. ([[2012-iwalewa|Iwalewa, 2012, p. 50]])

- Evaporation rates are critical for determining the economic viability of using brine for salt production. ([[2016-armoh|Armoh, 2016, p. 15]])

- Identified UER as a highly productive but over-exploited fossil aquifer. ([[2019-prasetyo|Prasetyo, 2019, p. 23]])

- Wasia Group contains the main aquifer that supplies drinking and irrigation water to this area. ([[2019-ahmed|Ahmed, 2019, p. 12]])

- Identified two distinct zones with different hydraulic behaviors based on fracture geometry. ([[2020-mansouri|Mansouri, 2020, p. 13]])

- The area contains two primary aquifers: Saq and Tawil-Quaternary systems. ([[2022-karami|Karami, 2022, p. 23]])

- The integration of GIS, Remote Sensing, and MCDA - Weighted Overlay Analysis (WOA) methods is assumed powerful tool for delineating ASR potential sites in the semi-arid region. ([[2022-pambudi|Pambudi, 2022, p. 14]])

- Determined that thermal water is influenced by mixing and rock interaction. ([[2023-rafiq|Rafiq, 2023, p. 72]])

- Water saturation is essential for human activity in desert areas. ([[2024-tarchi|Tarchi, 2024, p. 69]])

- The eastern coastal aquifers are Neogene, Alat, and Khobar aquifers with varying hydraulic properties. ([[2024-ahmad|Ahmad, 2024, p. 20]])

- Subsurface complexity was found to control aquifer heterogeneity. ([[2024-ahmed|Ahmed, 2024, p. 115]])

- Identified optimal irrigation strategies for arid agroecosystems. ([[2024-prasetyo|Prasetyo, 2024, p. 14]])

- Mapping water content in the shallow subsurface can provide critical information for groundwater exploration. ([[2025-oyekan|Oyekan, 2025, p. 76]])

- The water level in the ponds showed a direct correlation with rainfall, indicating an aquiclude character of the sediments. ([[1994-al-guwaizani|Al-Guwaizani, 1994, p. 55]])

- High vertical leakage confirmed the interconnection and lithologic control on groundwater flow. ([[1995-hasan|Hasan, 1995, p. 28]])
