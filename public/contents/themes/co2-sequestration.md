## Key Findings Across Sources

- Carbonate rocks are good candidates for CO2 storage due to their large capacity and potential for mineral trapping. ([[2014-al-ameri|AL-AMERI, 2014, p. 34]])

- The study established that the electrical behavior of carbonate rocks under long term CO2 storage is dynamic under a static condition. ([[2015-adebayo|Adebayo, 2015, p. 17]])

- MOF-177 can capture twice the amount of CO2 compared to zeolite 13X. ([[2014-jamil|JAMIL, 2014, p. 44]])

- Storing CO2 is preferable in shallow and low temperature reservoirs due to its high affinity for carbonate rocks. ([[2016-elnaeem|Elnaeem, 2016, p. 49]])

- Several geological options exist: depleted oil/gas reservoirs, unmineable coal seams, and saline water aquifers. ([[2020-abdallah|Abdallah, 2020, p. 23]])

- Carbonate formation (CaCO3) can initially reduce permeability but subsequent leaching leads to porosity increase and strength loss. ([[2020-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2020, p. 35]])

- Saline aquifers are the most desirable option due to their large reserves, wide distribution, and high security. ([[2023-aldhafeeri|Aldhafeeri, 2023, p. 21]])

- Understanding these storage mechanisms and enhancing them effectively will help in a longer-term, safer storage of CO2. ([[2024-suleiman-3|Suleiman, 2024, p. 34]])

- Structural trapping is more prevalent in the Upper Ramp and Shoal Facies, while residual trapping is higher in Middle Ramp Facies. ([[2024-yusmananto|Yusmananto, 2024, p. 16]])

- Referenced in this thesis. ([[2024-almukainah|Almukainah, 2024]])

- Saline aquifers are widely available and have large storage capacities, making them a promising avenue for long-term carbon mitigation. ([[2024-badhafere|BADHAFERE, 2024, p. 23]])

- Submarine channels offer promising features like stability and well-defined traps for storage. ([[2024-maigana|Maigana, 2024, p. 17]])

- A minimum depth of 800 meters is required to maintain the supercritical state of CO2. ([[2025-ahmed|Ahmed, 2025, p. 28]])

- Referenced in this thesis. ([[2025-alghawi|Alghawi, 2025]])
