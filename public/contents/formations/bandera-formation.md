## Key Findings Across Sources

- Bandera cores showed a 40% enhancement in permeability when using seawater-based fluids. ([[2016-hamza|HAMZA, 2016, p. 19]])

- Bandera Brown sample BB4 showed an increase in porosity after HCl treatment due to clay dissolution. ([[2016-jad|Jad, 2016, p. 89]])

- The Bandera gray had a wettability index of +0.35 (slightly water wet). ([[2021-almarri|AlMarri, 2021, p. 51]])
