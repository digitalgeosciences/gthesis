## Key Findings Across Sources

- The Safaniya Member is characterized by the absence of major conglomeratic facies except at channel lags. Clean sandstone units are abundant... These clean sandstone units constitute the Safaniya Reservoir. ([[1997-al-qassab|Al-Qassab, 1997, p. 25]])

- The Safaniya Member includes the Lower Safaniya Stringers, the Safaniya Main Sands, and the Upper Safaniya Stringers. ([[2013-atig|atig, 2013, p. 10]])
