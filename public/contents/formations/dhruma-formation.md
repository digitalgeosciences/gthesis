## Key Findings Across Sources

- The Dhruma Formation is divided into three units: lower, middle, and upper Dhruma. ([[2010-al-mojel|Al-Mojel, 2010, p. 27]])

- The Dhruma Formation contains 7 members (D2-D7) and is of Bajocian to Bathonian age. ([[2016-malik|Malik, 2016, p. 1]])

- The Dhruma Formation contains several members (D2-D7) with varying petrophysical characteristics. ([[2017-yousif|Yousif, 2017, p. 23]])

- The formation is a good example of an ancient carbonate ramp system with three distinct members. ([[2018-ismanto|Ismanto, 2018, p. 16]])
