## Key Findings Across Sources

- The study focused on the interval from Base Khuff to Pre Unayzah Unconformity. ([[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997, p. 10]])

- The Upper Khartam Member of the Khuff Formation contains significant moldic, vuggy, and intercrystalline porosity. ([[2014-mohammed|Mohammed, 2014, p. 71]])

- The Upper Khartam Member of the Khuff Formation is a key target for gas production, showing significant vertical and lateral heterogeneities. ([[2014-sami|Sami, 2014, p. 18]])

- Porosity ranges from 0.6% to 29.1%. ([[2016-saleh|Saleh, 2016, p. 19]])

- The formation contains multiple members with varying lithofacies and diagenetic impacts on porosity/permeability. ([[2016-makkawi|Makkawi, 2016, p. 8]])

- The formation is divided into several members including Huqayl, Midhnab, and Khartam. ([[2017-adam|Adam, 2017, p. 12]])
