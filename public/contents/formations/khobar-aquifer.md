## Key Findings Across Sources

- The Khobar aquifer is characterized by fractured limestone with an 80m thickness and a depth of around 130m, with a transmissivity ranging from 0.9 x 10^-2 m^2/s to 0.29 m^2/s. ([[2024-ahmad|Ahmad, 2024, p. 20]])

- Water quality in some parts of the Khobar aquifer was found to be good, with concentrations less than 500 ppm. ([[1995-hasan|Hasan, 1995, p. 28]])

- The aquifer has a thickness of 20-70m with an average transmissivity of 2.9 x 10^-3 m^2/s. ([[1992-hassan|Hassan, 1992, p. 22]])
