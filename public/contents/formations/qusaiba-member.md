## Key Findings Across Sources

- The average thickness is approximately 300 ft in all three wells. ([[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012, p. 14]])

- The Siq Sandstone shows high-energy features, while the Quweira Sandstone exhibits significant deformation. ([[2014-mohammedfaiz|Mohammedfaiz, 2014, p. 195]])

- The formation contains three distinct lithofacies with varying mineralogical and mechanical properties. ([[2015-mustafa|Mustafa, 2015, p. 19]])

- The member exhibits distinct lithological characteristics and is a target for hydraulic fracturing. ([[2015-own|Own, 2015, p. 14]])

- Characterized as a microbialite-dominated carbonate sequence. ([[2015-koeshidayatullah|Koeshidayatullah, 2015]])

- The sample was found to be rich in quartz and clay, with negligible carbonate content. ([[2016-lawal|Lawal, 2016, p. 46]])

- The member contains five distinct lithofacies with varying mineralogical compositions (quartz, kaolinite, muscovite). ([[2017-mahgoub|Mahgoub, 2017, p. 37]])

- The formation contains diverse foraminiferal assemblages that indicate varying oxygen levels from dysoxic to oxic. ([[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018, p. 14]])
