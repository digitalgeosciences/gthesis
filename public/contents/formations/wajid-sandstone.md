## Key Findings Across Sources

- The Juwayl member of the Wajid Sandstone shows evidence of microchanneling and slumps, potentially indicating a periglacial climate. ([[2014-mohammedfaiz|Mohammedfaiz, 2014, p. 75]])

- The formation acts as a groundwater aquifer and potential hydrocarbon reservoir. ([[2017-benaafi|Benaafi, 2017, p. 1]])

- Composed of reddish-brown conglomerates within a sandstone matrix, unconformably overlying the basement. ([[2022-al-sadah|Al-Sadah, 2022, p. 38]])

- The formation contains four main facies associations: alluvial fan, braided fluvial, floodplain, and shoreface/delta front. ([[2023-butt|Butt, 2023, p. 74]])

- Zone C is located within several rock units including Wajid sandstone. ([[2023-arrofi|Arrofi, 2023, p. 83]])

- The formation consists of a Red Unit and a Grey Unit with distinct lithofacies and mineralogical signatures. ([[1999-babalola|Babalola, 1999, p. 12]])
