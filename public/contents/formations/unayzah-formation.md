## Key Findings Across Sources

- The Unayzah Formation contains both high-quality sands (Unayzah-A) and lower quality siltstones. ([[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997, p. 39]])

- The formation contains sandstones, shale, and siltstone with a variety of diagenetic features. ([[2002-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2002, p. 23]])

- The Unayzah-A is divided into several facies including dunes, interdunes, sand sheets, and playa lakes. ([[2011-al-masrahy|AL-MASRAHY, 2011, p. 26]])

- Referenced in this thesis. ([[2011-alburaikan|AlBuraikan, 2011]])

- The Unayzah-C member is a heterogeneous glacial deposit, while the Unayzah-A member consists of eolian dune sandstones. ([[2016-hassan|Hassan, 2016, p. 32]])

- The Unayzah-a member is highly heterogeneous and divided into two stratal units (Zone A and Zone B). ([[2016-houssain|Houssain, 2016, p. 21]])

- The formation contains sand-rich intervals with varying mineralogy and porosity. ([[2020-almumtin|Almumtin, 2020, p. 26]])

- The formation is characterized by complex continental clastic deposits including aeolian, fluvial, and alluvial fan facies. ([[2020-gbiyanju|Gbiyanju, 2020, p. 45]])
