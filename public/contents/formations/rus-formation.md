## Key Findings Across Sources

- The Rus Formation is represented by a succession of chalky limestones, marls and dolomites. ([[1992-el-sahn|El-Sahn, 1992, p. 43]])

- The Rus Formation has a thickness of 50 m on the Dammam Dome outcrop. ([[2018-hanif|Hanif, 2018, p. 25]])

- The Rus formation acts as an aquitard separating the Umm Er Radhuma from the Khobar aquifer. ([[1995-hasan|Hasan, 1995, p. 61]])
