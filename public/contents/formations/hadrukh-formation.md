## Key Findings Across Sources

- The target formation frequency spectrum range is between 7-42 Hz. ([[2019-al-dawood|Al-Dawood, 2019, p. 46]])

- The formation contains high organic content and varies significantly in mineralogy (calcite, illite, kaolinite). ([[2021-albensaad|Albensaad, 2021, p. 73]])

- The formation is a gas-producing carbonate source rock with high TOC (12 wt.%). ([[2021-lawal|LAWAL, 2021, p. 78]])

- The study confirms that these carbonates are equivalent to the Wadi Waqb member of the Jabal Kibrit Formation. ([[2022-al-sadah|Al-Sadah, 2022, p. 75]])

- The formation contains a variety of lithologies including carbonate clasts and quartz grains. ([[2002-al-ali|Al-Ali, 2002, p. 65]])
