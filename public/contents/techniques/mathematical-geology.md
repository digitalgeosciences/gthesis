# Mathematical and Computational Geology

**Summary**: The use of mathematical models and numerical algorithms to simulate fluid flow in porous media.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[1990-mimoune|Mimoune, 1990]] used a finite difference numerical simulator to model the behavior of gas wells with multiple fractures.

- [[2015-adeniji|Adeniji, 2015]] applied mathematical-geology techniques like GDM and various optimization algorithms.

- [[2017-khan|Khan, 2017]] used numerical modeling to analyze the flow of carbon dioxide and corresponding deformations in carbonate reservoirs.

- [[1997-al-gahtani-2|Al-Gahtani, 1997]] applied mathematical modeling for circuit design and analysis.
