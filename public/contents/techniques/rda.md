# Rda

**Summary**: A method used to study the reaction between fluids and rock surfaces by rotating a sample in a circulating fluid.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2017]] used RDA to determine the reaction rate, order of reaction, and diffusion coefficient for carbonate rocks with various chelating agents.

- [[2023-nasir|Nasir, 2023]] performed RDA to find associations between foraminiferal assemblages and pollution proxies.

## Key Results and Findings

- The reaction of 150°F GLDA/DI was surface reaction limited while 200-250°F were mass transfer limited. ([[2017-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2017, p. 20]])

- Identified correlations between specific species (e.g., Ammonia sp. 1) and pollutants like SPI and TPAH. ([[2023-nasir|Nasir, 2023, p. 78]])
