# Cokriging

**Summary**: An extension of kriging that incorporates secondary, dense data (like seismic) to improve the estimation of a primary variable.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2002-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2002]] applied cokriging to incorporate seismic information into the prediction of reservoir properties.

- [[2002-al-ali|Al-Ali, 2002]] utilized cokriging to integrate sparse uphole data with dense vibrator performance data.

## Key Results and Findings
