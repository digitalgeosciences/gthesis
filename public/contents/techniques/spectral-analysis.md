# Spectral Analysis

**Summary**: The study of the components of a signal in the frequency domain.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017]] applied spectral analysis to determine amplification as a function of soil thickness and offset.

- [[2018-hanif|Hanif, 2018]] used spectral-analysis to isolate the residual anomaly from the regional background.

- [[2020-abdallah-2|Abdallah, 2020]] used spectral analysis to determine depth from the slope of the linear segment of the log of the power spectrum.

- [[2022-al-harbi|Al-Harbi, 2022]] applied spectral unmixing to extract pure spectral signatures for waste in mixed pixels.

## Key Results and Findings

- Average amplitude spectra were used to compare Sabkha traces and sand dune traces at different offset ranges. ([[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017, p. 10]])

- Used 2D Fourier transforms to identify different spatial wavelengths in the Bouguer map. ([[2018-hanif|Hanif, 2018, p. 43]])

- This technique is used to distinguish and map top basement horizons by analyzing energy-decay curves. ([[2020-abdallah-2|Abdallah, 2020, p. 49]])

- Spectral unmixing was used to identify specific waste types by analyzing their unique spectral signatures. ([[2022-al-harbi|Al-Harbi, 2022, p. 32]])
