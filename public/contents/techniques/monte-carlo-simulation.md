# Monte Carlo Simulation

**Summary**: A mathematical technique that uses repeated random sampling to obtain numerical results, used here to model the range of possible hydrocarbon volumes.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2003-al-shridi|Al-Shridi, 2003]] used Monte Carlo simulation to generate a reservoir pore-volume distribution and quantify uncertainty in key parameters like area, thickness, and porosity.
