# Micropaleontology

**Summary**: The study of microscopic fossils, such as foraminifera, to determine geological history and paleoenvironments.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2006-al-enezi|Al-Enezi, 2006]] used micropaleontological techniques to analyze 60 samples from the Dam Formation.

- [[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012]] used micropaleontology, specifically agglutinated Foraminifera, to interpret depositional environments.

- [[2016-malik|Malik, 2016]] used micropaleontology to identify 35 species from 19 different genera.

- [[2017-adam|Adam, 2017]] used micropaleontology for biostratigraphy and paleoenvironmental reconstruction.

- [[2022-khan|Khan, 2022]] used micropaleontology to identify foraminiferal assemblages in carbonate ramp sediments.

- [[2024-alhilali|Alhilali, 2024]] used micropaleontology to identify and characterize agglutinated benthic foraminifera from Turonian to Santonian ages.

- [[2024-malik|Malik, 2024]] used micropaleontology to identify Ediacaran microfossils.

- [[2025-ferreira|Ferreira, 2025]] used super-resolution techniques to enhance the quality of micropaleontological images.

## Key Results and Findings

- Identified 47 species from the Arabian Gulf to establish a baseline for comparison with fossil assemblages. ([[2006-al-enezi|Al-Enezi, 2006, p. 99]])

- Agglutinated foraminifera were used as indicators for dysoxic conditions in well C. ([[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012, p. 13]])

- Identified 35 species belonging to 19 different genera in the D4 and D5 members. ([[2016-malik|Malik, 2016, p. 18]])

- Identified Earlandia foraminifera in specific beds (Table 4.1). ([[2017-adam|Adam, 2017, p. 10]])

- Identified diverse benthic foraminifera including Elphidium sp. and Ammonia sp. across different core samples. ([[2022-khan|Khan, 2022, p. 180]])

- Identified 29 samples containing various species of agglutinated benthic foraminifera including Ammodiscus, Bulbobaculites, and Reophax. ([[2024-alhilali|Alhilali, 2024]])

- Identified vase-shaped microfossils and Trochamminid foraminifera. ([[2024-malik|Malik, 2024, p. 14]])

- The ForamViT-GAN aims to overcome challenges in micropaleontology by integrating vision transformers with GANs for synthesis and super-resolution. ([[2025-ferreira|Ferreira, 2025, p. 12]])
