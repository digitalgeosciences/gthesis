# Micropaleontology

**Summary**: The study of microscopic fossils, such as foraminifera, to determine geological history and paleoenvironments.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2006-al-enezi|Al-Enezi, 2006]] used micropaleontological techniques to analyze 60 samples from the Dam Formation.

- [[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012]] used micropaleontology, specifically agglutinated Foraminifera, to interpret depositional environments.

- [[2016-malik|Malik, 2016]] used micropaleontology to identify 35 species from 19 different genera.

- [[2017-adam|Adam, 2017]] used micropaleontology for biostratigraphy and paleoenvironmental reconstruction.

## Key Results and Findings

- Agglutinated foraminifera were used as indicators for dysoxic conditions in well C. ([[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012, p. 13]])

- Identified 35 species belonging to 19 different genera in the D4 and D5 members. ([[2016-malik|Malik, 2016, p. 18]])
