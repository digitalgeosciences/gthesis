# Finite Volume Method

**Summary**: A numerical method for solving partial differential equations by discretizing the domain into small volumes.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-jamil|JAMIL, 2014]] used finite volume method to solve mass, momentum, and energy conservation equations in porous media.

- [[2018-jamal|Jamal, 2018]] used finite volume method to solve the Brinkman's equation and advection-diffusion-adsorption equations.

- [[2022-aboluhom|Aboluhom, 2022]] used the finite volume method to discretize the flow, reaction, and thermal models.

- [[2024-alabdrabalnabi|Alabdrabalnabi, 2024]] used finite volume method (FVM) to solve flow, mass transfer, and heat transfer equations in a 2D and 3D grid structure.
