# Sem Edx

**Summary**: Scanning electron microscopy coupled with energy-dispersive X-ray spectroscopy to visualize morphology and elemental composition.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2018-adeyemi|Adeyemi, 2018]] used SEM-EDX to characterize the surface morphology and elemental composition of the synthesized SDAC.

- [[2024-alsadah|Alsadah, 2024]] used SEM-EDX to calculate the Mg/Ca ratio of foraminiferal shells and identify species through morphological features.

- [[2026-fatima|Fatima, 2026]] applied SEM-EDX to analyze grain morphology and elemental distribution in the aquifer material.

## Key Results and Findings
