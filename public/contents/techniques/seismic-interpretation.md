# Seismic Interpretation

**Summary**: Interpretation of seismic data to map subsurface structures and depths.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2000-mohammad|Mohammad, 2000]] applied seismic interpretation to refraction profiles to determine aquifer depth.

- [[2001-al-subai|Al-Subai, 2001]] applied 3-D seismic interpretation to map fault systems and reservoir compartments.

- [[2010-al-moqbel|Al-Moqbel, 2010]] focused on seismic-interpretation to identify and map subsurface horizons in Eastern Saudi Arabia.

- [[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012]] used 3D seismic data to identify and map faults in the basement reservoir.

- [[2015-adetokunbo|Adetokunbo, 2015]] focused on the theme of seismic-interpretation using advanced edge detection filters.

- [[2015-sanuade|Sanuade, 2015]] applied seismic-interpretation to identify structural elements like listric faults and sequence boundaries.

- [[2016-hassan|Hassan, 2016]] applied curvature and coherence as alternative seismic fracture detection methods for the Unayzah-C reservoir.

- [[2016-awad|Awad, 2016]] performed seismic-interpretation on a 3D volume to identify four key stratigraphic surfaces in the Rawat Basin.

- [[2017-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2017]] performed seismic interpretation to identify fault zones, horizons, and depositional systems.

- [[2018-abdulrahman|Abdulrahman, 2018]] performed seismic-interpretation tasks including gain, filtering, and deconvolution.

- [[2023-edigbue|Edigbue, 2023]] used SR to measure seismic wave velocities for joint inversion with DCR data.

- [[2024-tarchi|Tarchi, 2024]] applied seismic-interpretation to analyze P-wave velocities (VP) from both high-resolution and conventional surveys.

- [[2024-alkhunaizi|Alkhunaizi, 2024]] used seismic-interpretation to obtain P-wave velocities (VP) from high-resolution and conventional surveys.

- [[2024-maigana|Maigana, 2024]] applied seismic-interpretation to identify faults, horizons, and channel geometries in the Tui Field.

## Key Results and Findings

- Seismic interpretation indicated that the acoustic velocity for the Khobar aquifer ranges between 1800 m/sec and 3970 m/sec. ([[2000-mohammad|Mohammad, 2000, p. 72]])

- Identified several faults in all directions, confirming the isolation of Well-B in a separate fault block. ([[2001-al-subai|Al-Subai, 2001, p. 75]])

- The model-based velocity integration successfully identified seven key horizons with clearer signal characteristics. ([[2010-al-moqbel|Al-Moqbel, 2010, p. 136]])

- Faults identified from seismic data showed consistent trends with those observed in FMI logs and satellite imagery. ([[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012, p. 11]])

- The proposed method can be used as a complementary tool in seismic attribute analysis and interpretation. ([[2015-adetokunbo|Adetokunbo, 2015, p. 67]])

- Identified three major listric faults (F3, F6, and F7) impacting the reservoir. ([[2015-sanuade|Sanuade, 2015, p. 66]])

- No statistical correlation was found between AVOA, curvature, and coherence in either reservoir. ([[2016-hassan|Hassan, 2016, p. 47]])

- Identified four continuous reflectors corresponding to major sequence boundaries and transgressive surfaces. ([[2016-awad|Awad, 2016, p. 18]])

- Seismic interpretation revealed a pull-apart transtensional movement in the Sufyan Sub-basin. ([[2017-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2017, p. 23]])

- Standard processing steps like T2 gain and deconvolution were used to improve signal quality before velocity analysis. ([[2018-abdulrahman|Abdulrahman, 2018, p. 30]])

- The case study in the Dammam Dome showed that the approach was able to delineate major geological structures, including faults and fractures. ([[2023-edigbue|Edigbue, 2023, p. 17]])

- High-resolution survey used 31 geophones over a 5.4m line to capture detailed P-wave velocity profiles. ([[2024-tarchi|Tarchi, 2024, p. 43]])

- High-resolution 2D data showed P-wave velocities ranging from 48 m/s at the surface to 425 m/s at 7m depth. ([[2024-alkhunaizi|Alkhunaizi, 2024, p. 49]])

- Identified five key horizons including Upper Manganui (seal) and Moki A/B (reservoir). ([[2024-maigana|Maigana, 2024, p. 48]])
