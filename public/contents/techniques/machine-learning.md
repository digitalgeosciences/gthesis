# Machine Learning

**Summary**: The use of algorithms and statistical models to enable computers to learn from and make predictions on data.

## Key Results and Findings

- GRNN outperformed MLP in terms of accuracy, robustness, and training speed. ([[2009-sitouah|Sitouah, 2009, p. 16]])

- The SVM algorithm is able to accurately predict reservoir properties from computed seismic attributes. ([[2013-mark|Mark, 2013, p. 22]])

- The SVM-based approach successfully categorized image quality into two classes for assessment. ([[2015-mohammed|Mohammed, 2015, p. 98]])

- Both ANN and RBF were evaluated as non-linear mapping tools, with RBF showing faster learning rates. ([[2015-fuad|Fuad, 2015, p. 60]])

- Machine learning can be used to segment seismic sections based on texture and integrate data from different disciplines. ([[2020-abdallah|Abdallah, 2020, p. 31]])

- The ML model achieved R2 values of 0.83, 0.87, and 0.89 for training, validation, and testing respectively. ([[2020-gbiyanju|Gbiyanju, 2020, p. 18]])

- Machine learning allows for the identification of complex patterns (like moveout) that are difficult to program manually. ([[2021-shaheen|Shaheen, 2021, p. 27]])

- Machine learning techniques, including ANN and SVM, were successfully applied to predict drilling fluid rheology and evaluate hole-cleaning conditions. ([[2021-al-malki|Al-Malki, 2021, p. 102]])

- Used as a method to predict Total Organic Carbon (TOC) in the Jubaila Formation. ([[2022-septriandi|Septriandi, 2022, p. 36]])

- After applying SVI, 98.4% of DBSCAN pickings for synthetic data fell within ±8.33 msec. ([[2022-awais|Awais, 2022, p. 17]])

- Random Forest (RF) exceeded all other algorithms for facies prediction, including LSTM. ([[2023-kusuma|Kusuma, 2023, p. 17]])

- The ANN model was the most accurate method compared to radial basis function and general regression neural network models. ([[2023-alzayer|Alzayer, 2023, p. 11]])

- Machine learning can provide a proper solution for challenges in data processing and calculations without additional costs. ([[2023-gad|Gad, 2023, p. 17]])

- The FNO model, as a form of machine learning, successfully achieved high accuracy in detecting event locations even under extreme conditions. ([[2023-suleymanli|Suleymanli, 2023, p. 16]])

- Architecture_1 performed best, balancing model complexity with predictive accuracy. ([[2024-hassan|Hassan, 2024, p. 15]])

- The Categorical Boosting model outperformed the others across all three tectonic categories with the lowest MSE values. ([[2025-rachmadan|Rachmadan, 2025, p. 14]])

- Machine learning, especially supervised learning, has seen near-exponential growth in the last decade for tasks like image classification and object detection. ([[2025-ferreira|Ferreira, 2025, p. 17]])
