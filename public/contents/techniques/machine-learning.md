**Summary**: The use of algorithms and statistical models to enable computers to learn from and make predictions on data.

- [[2013-mark|Mark, 2013]] applied machine-learning (specifically SVM) to classify well and seismic attributes for pore pressure prediction.

## Key Results and Findings

- GRNN outperformed MLP in terms of accuracy, robustness, and training speed. ([[2009-sitouah|Sitouah, 2009, p. 16]])

- The SVM algorithm is able to accurately predict reservoir properties from computed seismic attributes. ([[2013-mark|Mark, 2013, p. 22]])

- The SVM-based approach successfully categorized image quality into two classes for assessment. ([[2015-mohammed|Mohammed, 2015, p. 98]])

- Both ANN and RBF were evaluated as non-linear mapping tools, with RBF showing faster learning rates. ([[2015-fuad|Fuad, 2015, p. 60]])
