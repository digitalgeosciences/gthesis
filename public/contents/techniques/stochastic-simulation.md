# Stochastic Simulation

**Summary**: A mathematical technique that accounts for risk and uncertainty by performing multiple iterations of a calculation with varying input parameters.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2003-al-shridi|Al-Shridi, 2003]] used Monte Carlo simulation to model the range of possible outcomes in hydrocarbon volume calculations.

- [[2013-atig|atig, 2013]] utilized sequential indicator simulation to capture thin sedimentary beds and complex geometries in the Safaniya member.

- [[2015-jabir|Jabir, 2015]] employed stochastic-simulation to capture heterogeneity and assess uncertainty in the 3D model.
