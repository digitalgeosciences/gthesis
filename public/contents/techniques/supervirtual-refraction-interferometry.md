# Supervirtual Refraction Interferometry

**Summary**: A method to enhance the signal-to-noise ratio of seismic refraction data by cross-correlating and summing trace pairs.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-edigbue|Edigbue, 2016]] implemented supervirtual-refraction-interferometry to improve first arrival detection in 3D seismic datasets.
