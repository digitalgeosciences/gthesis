# Electrical Resistivity Tomography

**Summary**: A geophysical method used to measure the variation in resistivity of subsurface layers.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2000-mohammad|Mohammad, 2000]] used vertical electrical sounding (VES) at 5 sites to estimate the quality of groundwater in the Khobar Aquifer.

- [[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017]] used electrical-resistivity-tomography to map subsurface cavities and weathered zones.

- [[2023-edigbue|Edigbue, 2023]] used DCR to provide a high-resolution image of subsurface conductivity.

- [[2023-alshaibani|AlShaibani, 2023]] applied ERT with a dipole-dipole array to identify high-resistivity anomalies in the deformation zone.

- [[2024-tarchi|Tarchi, 2024]] used electrical-resistivity-tomography (ERT) with 96 electrodes to measure conductivity in a sand dune.

- [[2024-prasetyo|Prasetyo, 2024]] used time-lapse ERT to analyze the pattern of active root water uptake zone.

- [[2026-fatima|Fatima, 2026]] used electrical-resistivity-tomography (ERT) to monitor the effectiveness of TWW-based MAR in a 2D lab-scale physical model.

- [[2003-chowdhury|CHOWDHURY, 2003]] used electrical-resitvity-tomography to measure potential drops across electrode arrays to track fluid front movement.

- [[1995-hasan|Hasan, 1995]] used electrical resistivity surveys (VES) to determine the thickness of various hydrogeologic units in the Wadi Al-Miyah area.

## Key Results and Findings

- Used to determine the depth and thickness of various lithological units. ([[2000-mohammad|Mohammad, 2000, p. 50]])

- The ERT imaging result proved precise and efficient in delineating shallow subsurface voids and soil heterogeneity. ([[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017, p. 16]])

- The joint inversion of DCR and SR data provides a more comprehensive understanding of the subsurface structure. ([[2023-edigbue|Edigbue, 2023, p. 19]])

- The ERT identified a high resistive anomaly of 76.5 ohm.m at the deformation site. ([[2023-alshaibani|AlShaibani, 2023, p. 54]])

- The ERT survey provided conductivity values ranging from approximately 7.68 mS/m to 65.63 mS/m at the sampled depths. ([[2024-tarchi|Tarchi, 2024, p. 53]])

- Captured root activity variations over seven months, highlighting its potential for precision agriculture. ([[2024-prasetyo|Prasetyo, 2024, p. 14]])

- ERT is sensitive to salinity and porosity changes, making it effective for monitoring recharge plumes. ([[2026-fatima|Fatima, 2026, p. 31]])

- The system could detect a fluid front up to twice the span of the electrode array. ([[2003-chowdhury|CHOWDHURY, 2003, p. 63]])

- The survey identified a probable boundary of a collapsing structure in the Rus formation. ([[1995-hasan|Hasan, 1995, p. 28]])
