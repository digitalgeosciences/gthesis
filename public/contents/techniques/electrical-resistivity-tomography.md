# Electrical Resistivity Tomography

**Summary**: A geophysical method to measure the subsurface resistivity of rocks.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2000-mohammad|Mohammad, 2000]] used vertical electrical sounding (VES) with a Schlumberger array to determine the resistivity of the Khobar aquifer.

- [[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017]] used ERT to map soil heterogeneity and detect cavities along the Red Sea coast.

## Key Results and Findings

- The survey identified low resistivity areas (< 3.0 Ohm.m) corresponding to sediment-filled voids. ([[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017, p. 16]])
