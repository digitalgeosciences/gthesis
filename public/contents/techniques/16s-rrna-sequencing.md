# 16S Rrna Sequencing

**Summary**: A method used to identify and characterize bacterial species based on the genetic sequence of the 16S ribosomal RNA gene.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2011-salamah|Salamah, 2011]] used 16s-rrna-sequencing to identify two indigenous strains, Pseudoxanthomonas sp. and Bacillus sp., from contaminated soil.

- [[2015-oyehan|Oyehan, 2015]] performed 16S rRNA sequencing to identify the isolated hydrocarbon-degrading strains.

- [[2017-budiyanto|Budiyanto, 2017]] used 16S rRNA sequencing to identify three halophilic strains (Idiomarina sp., Halomonas shengliensis, and Halomonas smyrnensis).
