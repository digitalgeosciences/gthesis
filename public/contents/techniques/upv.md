# Upv

**Summary**: A non-destructive test that measures the speed of ultrasonic waves through a rock to estimate mechanical properties.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2022-samarkin|Samarkin, 2022]] used UPV to predict UCS and static Young's modulus of carbonate rocks after chemical treatment.
