# Petrography

**Summary**: The study of rocks using optical microscopy to identify mineral composition and texture.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[1996-matter|Matter, 1996]] used petrography to characterize the microfacies and mineralogy of phosphorite samples.

- [[2014-mohammed|Mohammed, 2014]] used thin-section-petrography to identify visible grains, porosity, cement, and matrix in the Khartam Member.

- [[2015-abdlmutalib|Abdlmutalib, 2015]] used petrography to characterize the 60 samples for porosity and permeability measurements.

## Key Results and Findings

- Twelve distinct microfacies types were identified, including calcareous and siliceous phosphorites. ([[1996-matter|Matter, 1996, p. 46]])

- Used to identify the variety of carbonate components and characterize the reservoir units at outcrop scale. ([[2014-mohammed|Mohammed, 2014, p. 35]])

- Petrographic analysis confirmed that stromatoporoid packstones were more fractured than dolomitic mudstone lithofacies. ([[2015-abdlmutalib|Abdlmutalib, 2015, p. 25]])

- Identified distinct diagenetic textures (pseudomorphic replacement vs. drusy cement) in the Daddiyah member. ([[2016-radwan|Radwan, 2016, p. 74]])

- Identification of various carbonate and siliciclastic components in the Dam Formation. ([[2017-mohammed-2|Mohammed, 2017, p. 162]])

- Identified nine distinct lithofacies types (LFTs) based on petrographic analysis, with fissile shale being the most dominant at 36%. ([[2017-yousif|Yousif, 2017, p. 18]])

- Identified five distinct pore types: BC-nfp, BC-fp, WP, FR, and MP. ([[2022-stiawan|Stiawan, 2022, p. 53]])

- Petrographic analysis revealed specific lithofacies patterns in the Upper Khartam Member. ([[2022-babker|Babker, 2022]])

- Identified minerals as spinel lherzolite, spinel harzburgite, and wehrlites. ([[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023, p. 21]])

- Automated methods for labeling and annotating geoscientific images are still in their infancy, particularly in carbonate petrography and modal mineralogy analysis. ([[2025-ferreira|Ferreira, 2025, p. 10]])
