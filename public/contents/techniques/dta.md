# Differential Thermal Analysis

**Summary**: A technique measuring temperature differences between a sample and an inert substance during heating to identify chemical/physical changes.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[1999-iddris|Iddris, 1999]] used DTA to identify exothermic reactions like LTO and HTO in the oil samples.

- [[1985-ghaled|Ghaled, 1985]] applied DTA to determine the decomposition temperature of magnesite samples.

## Key Results and Findings

- DTA identified two distinct oxidation regimes: LTO (270 °C) and HTO (400 °C). ([[1999-iddris|Iddris, 1999, p. 64]])

- The decomposition temperature of Zarghat magnesite was found to be approximately 620°C. ([[1985-ghaled|Ghaled, 1985, p. 86]])
