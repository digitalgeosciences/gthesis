# Total Organic Carbon Analysis

**Summary**: A method to measure the total amount of carbon in a sample, representing organic matter.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2011-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2011]] used TOC analysis to characterize the organic content of source water samples.

- [[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012]] applied TOC analysis to determine the quantity of organic matter in the Hanadir formation.

- [[2015-akere|Akere, 2015]] performed TOC analysis to evaluate its influence on organotin concentration.
