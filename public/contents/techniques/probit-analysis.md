# Probit Analysis

**Summary**: A statistical method used to estimate the concentration of a substance that causes a specific level of response (e.g., mortality) in a population.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2018-amiruddin|Amiruddin, 2018]] used Probit analysis to calculate LC50 and EC50 values for both local fish and brine shrimp.
