## Key Results and Findings

- Identified five phosphoritic intervals in the Paleocene to Eocene succession. ([[1996-matter|Matter, 1996, p. 36]])

- The succession is here correlated with both the Dammam and the Asmari Formations. ([[1992-el-sahn|El-Sahn, 1992, p. 13]])

- The distribution of the Lower Cretaceous rock units in the southeastern Arabian Peninsula indicates their deposition in a broad basinal area. ([[1992-al-sulami|Al-Sulami, 1992, p. 113]])

- Identified a series of deepening pulses and shoaling-upward cycles within the formation. ([[2006-al-enezi|Al-Enezi, 2006, p. 96]])

- The high resolution stratigraphic study infers that Upper Khartam consists of three high frequency sequences (HFSs). ([[2014-sami|Sami, 2014, p. 18]])

- Identified several distinct members within the Saq Formation based on sedimentological character. ([[2014-mohammedfaiz|Mohammedfaiz, 2014, p. 70]])

- Identified 11 third order sequences in the third rifting cycle. ([[2016-awad|Awad, 2016, p. 155]])

- Stratigraphic markers were used to identify the transition from Lower to Upper Khartam. ([[2022-babker|Babker, 2022, p. 69]])

- Identified several major unconformity surfaces in the Late Cretaceous sequence. ([[1989-abdou|Abdou, 1989, p. 92]])
