# Gaussian Sequential Simulation

**Summary**: A stochastic simulation method that generates multiple realizations of a variable by sequentially adding random values from a Gaussian distribution.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[1996-al-salem|Al-Salem, 1996]] used GSS to generate ten realisations of the well-log porosity for the 3D grid.
