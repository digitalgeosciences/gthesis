# Sgr

**Summary**: A method to measure the natural radioactivity of minerals (K, U, Th) in rocks.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-abdalla|Abdalla, 2013]] used SGR logging to characterize lithofacies and determine cycle thicknesses from outcrop sections.

- [[2017-mohammed-2|Mohammed, 2017]] used SGR to evaluate the mineralogical response in mixed carbonate-siliciclastic sequences.
