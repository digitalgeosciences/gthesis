## Key Results and Findings

- The system was calibrated using standards of thiophene, BT, and DBT (1 to 75 ppm). ([[2015-danmaliki|Danmaliki, 2015, p. 86]])

- Used to select the appropriate emulsifier agent for the specific waste oil chemistry. ([[2016-sidaoui|Sidaoui, 2016, p. 111]])

- Gas chromatography confirmed the production of nitrogen gas as a result of the chemical reaction between ammonium chloride and sodium nitrite. ([[2020-mohammed|Mohammed, 2020, p. 119]])

- CPI values ranged from 0.9 to 1.4 across various lithofacies samples. ([[2020-usman|Usman, 2020, p. 58]])

- Differentiation of the FID pyrogram into parts allowed identification of volatile, heavy oil, and kerogen products. ([[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023, p. 104]])

- Analysis revealed the presence of light hydrocarbons, confirming the conversion of kerogen into hydrocarbons. ([[2024-moh-ali|Moh-Ali, 2024, p. 99]])
