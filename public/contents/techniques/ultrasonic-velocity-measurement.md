# Ultrasonic Velocity Measurement

**Summary**: Measurement of compressional and shear wave velocities to determine dynamic elastic moduli.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-mustafa|Mustafa, 2015]] used ultrasonic velocity measurement to determine dynamic Poisson's ratio and Young's modulus under varying confining pressures.

- [[2016-ejaz|Ejaz, 2016]] performed ultrasonic-velocity-measurement on 17 sandstone samples using the Autolab 500 system.

## Key Results and Findings

- P-wave and S-wave velocities showed a direct relationship with confining pressure, with P-waves ranging from 5558 to 5648 m/s in the Qusaiba Shale. ([[2015-mustafa|Mustafa, 2015, p. 84]])

- Ultrasonic measurements provided P and S wave velocities at varying confining pressures to simulate reservoir conditions. ([[2016-ejaz|Ejaz, 2016, p. 40]])
