# In Sar

**Summary**: A radar technique used to measure ground deformation by comparing the phase of two or more SAR acquisitions.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-prasetyo|Prasetyo, 2019]] applied InSAR processing to Sentinel-1A data to monitor local subsidence in specific municipalities and agricultural zones.

- [[2022-karami|Karami, 2022]] used InSAR to monitor ground surface displacement in the Wadi Al Sirhan Basin.
