# DSC

**Summary**: A thermo-analytical technique used to study transitions such as crystallization or gelation.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014]] applied DSC to determine the fractional gelation and kinetics of HPAM/PEI and A130/PEI systems.

- [[2022-radwan|Radwan, 2022]] used DSC/TGA to measure the specific heat capacity of sand samples from 25°C to 1000°C.

- [[2023-othman|Othman, 2023]] used DSC to determine gelation kinetics and suitable treatment times for fracturing fluids.
