# Magnetic Survey

**Summary**: Geophysical method using magnetic field variations to map subsurface geology and depth to basement.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-chavanidis|Chavanidis, 2024]] applied magnetic survey analysis to delineate regional structures and estimate Curie isotherm depth.

## Key Results and Findings

- The Werner method and Spectral analysis are used as reliable methods for determining basement depth in the absence of well data. ([[2020-abdallah-2|Abdallah, 2020, p. 18]])

- Analysis of airborne magnetic data allowed for the identification of mafic and felsic dikes as well as large-scale tectonic features. ([[2024-chavanidis|Chavanidis, 2024, p. 100]])
