# Pulse Decay

**Summary**: An unsteady-state technique where permeability is determined by monitoring the pressure decay over time after a pulse is introduced.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2000-hamid|Hamid, 2000]] used pulse-decay to determine permeability and Klinkenberg factors in whole core samples.
