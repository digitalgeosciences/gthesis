# Block Kriging

**Summary**: A geostatistical estimation method that assigns weights to nearby samples based on their spatial correlation.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[1996-jarad|Jarad, 1996]] applied block kriging to estimate P2O5 grades and tonnage in Horizon 1 and Horizon 3.
