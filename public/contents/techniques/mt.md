# Magnetotelluric Surveying (MT)

**Summary**: A passive electromagnetic method to measure subsurface electrical resistivity.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-ahmed|Ahmed, 2024]] applied magnetotelluric (MT) and audio-magnetotelluric (AMT) surveys to map subsurface resistivity and identify structures like salt domes.

- [[2024-chavanidis|Chavanidis, 2024]] used MT data to constrain the gravity model and identify fractured vs. fresh basement zones.

## Key Results and Findings

- Integrated MT data revealed lateral resistivity discontinuities and potential salt dome structures in the Al-Hassa area. ([[2024-ahmed|Ahmed, 2024, p. 20]])

- MT soundings showed a large resistivity contrast at depths consistent with the Curie isotherm. ([[2024-chavanidis|Chavanidis, 2024, p. 50]])
