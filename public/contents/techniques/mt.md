# Mt

**Summary**: A passive electromagnetic method used to measure the Earth's electrical resistivity at depth.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-ahmed|Ahmed, 2024]] used MT and AMT to map subsurface resistivity structures in the Al-Hassa area.

- [[2024-chavanidis|Chavanidis, 2024]] used mt to determine conductivity and verify Curie isotherm depth from aeromagnetic data.

## Key Results and Findings

- The 3D resistivity model was obtained using three-dimensional (3D) AMT/MT data inversion. ([[2024-ahmed|Ahmed, 2024, p. 58]])

- MT soundings showed a good conductor for long periods (greater than 1 s) and consistent behavior with slight curve splitting, indicating a non-layered subsurface model. ([[2024-chavanidis|Chavanidis, 2024, p. 84]])
