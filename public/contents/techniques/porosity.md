# Porosity

**Summary**: The measure of void spaces in a rock.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-alqubalee|Alqubalee, 2017]] measured porosity to evaluate reservoir quality.

- [[1999-iddris|Iddris, 1999]] evaluated the impact of porosity on oxidation rates.

- [[2002-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2002]] analyzed porosity distribution within the Arab-D reservoir.

- [[1990-al-jarri|Al-Jarri, 1990]] evaluated oil isothermal compressibility to improve well test analysis and reservoir performance calculations.

- [[1995-al-jaziri|Al-Jaziri, 1995]] Not used as a primary measurement in this thesis.

- [[1993-kandil|Kandil, 1993]] used porosity as a key parameter in flow equations.

- [[2001-al-khalifa|Al-Khalifa, 2001]] measured and modeled porosity as the primary reservoir property of interest.

- [[1995-al-khamis|Al-Khamis, 1995]] Not directly measured, but related to the context of gas behavior in reservoirs.

- [[1998-khan|Khan, 1998]] Not used directly as a measurement, but the Hele-Shaw model simulates a porous medium.

- [[2000-khan|Khan, 2000]] used porosity as a variable for correlating Klinkenberg and Forchheimer constants.

- [[1995-magaji|Magaji, 1995]] measured porosity using a helium porosimeter and estimated it from point-counting data.

- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] measured porosity as a primary reservoir property to be predicted from seismic attributes.

- [[1990-mimoune|Mimoune, 1990]] considered porosity as part of the reservoir properties.

- [[1993-moallim|Moallim, 1993]] analyzed aquifer storage based on hydrogeological parameters.

- [[2001-mohammed|Mohammed, 2001]] measured porosity and pore size distribution to determine the appropriate bridging particle sizes.
