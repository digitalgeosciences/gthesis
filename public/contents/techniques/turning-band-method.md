# Turning Band Method

**Summary**: A conditional simulation technique that reduces multi-dimensional simulations to a sum of one-dimensional ones.

---

- [[1996-jarad|Jarad, 1996]] used Turning Band method to produce several realizations for P2O5 content to assess uncertainty.
