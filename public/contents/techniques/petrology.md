# Petrology

**Summary**: The study of the origin, composition, and structure of rocks.

## Key Results and Findings

- Identified three distinct oxidation stages (Class 2, 3, and 4) based on mineralogical changes from titanium-rich magnetite to ilmenite. ([[1993-kaka|Kaka, 1993, p. 90]])

- Identified a high concentration of minerals like dolomite and calcite in the ore. ([[1996-matter|Matter, 1996, p. 17]])

- Identified A-type and I-type classifications based on mineralogy and chemistry. ([[2024-al-hakimi|Al-Hakimi, 2024, p. 43]])

- PetroGAN was used to generate 10,000 images of petrographical thin sections across four classes: Plutonic, Metamorphic, Volcanic, and Sedimentary rocks. ([[2025-ferreira|Ferreira, 2025, p. 42]])
