- Not stated in source ([[1995-al-jaziri|Al-Jaziri, 1995]])

- Identified three types of sedimentary phosphorites based on grain size: nodules, grains/pellets, and fine-grained. ([[1996-matter|Matter, 1996, p. 139]])

- Average framework grain composition is Q71F21L8. ([[2018-butt|Butt, 2018, p. 149]])

- QCI values confirmed that early Paleozoic sandstones are the main source for Arabian sands. ([[2022-radwan|Radwan, 2022, p. 50]])

- Olivine Fo# values ranged from 83.5 to 93.0, while clinopyroxene Mg# averaged 91.86. ([[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023, p. 75]])

- Not stated in source ([[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]])

- Al-Ghurayyah granites are classified as A-type (A2) based on mineralogy and chemistry. ([[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024, p. 75]])

- 40/70 ceramic was found to be 71.3% mullite. ([[2024-suleiman-5|Suleiman, 2024, p. 41]])

- Machine learning has led to a near-exponential growth in research for automated mineralogical analysis. ([[2025-ferreira|Ferreira, 2025, p. 17]])
