# Basin Modeling

**Summary**: Numerical simulation of the burial history and thermal evolution of a sedimentary basin.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012]] applied 1D basin modeling to understand the geological evolution of stratigraphic layers.

- [[2016-awad|Awad, 2016]] performed basin-analysis to understand the third rifting cycle.

- [[2017-mohammed|Mohammed, 2017]] performed basin modeling to investigate burial history and thermal maturity evolution.

- [[2017-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2017]] conducted basin-analysis to determine the history of the Sufyan Sub-basin.

## Key Results and Findings

- Used to calibrate predicted vitrinite reflectance and present-day temperatures against measured values. ([[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012, p. 12]])

- Identified three stages of basin evolution: rift initiation, rift climax, and late-rift. ([[2016-awad|Awad, 2016, p. 143]])

- Modeling results suggested that TQMN source rocks reached peak oil generation at approximately 70 Ma. ([[2017-mohammed|Mohammed, 2017, p. 25]])

- The Sufyan Sub-basin is identified as a dextral pull-apart basin. ([[2017-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2017, p. 90]])
