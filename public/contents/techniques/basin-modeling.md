# Basin Modeling

**Summary**: Numerical modeling of sedimentary basins to understand geological evolution.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012]] applied 1D basin models to simulate the thermal and burial history of three wells.

- [[2017-mohammed|Mohammed, 2017]] applied basin modeling to constrain the burial history and model the thermal maturation history of the Tuwaiq Mountain Formation.

## Key Results and Findings

- Modeling results showed TQMN source rocks started generating oil at ~100 Ma and reached peak oil generation at ~70 Ma. ([[2017-mohammed|Mohammed, 2017, p. 25]])
