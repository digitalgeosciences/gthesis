# Borehole Imaging

**Summary**: A borehole imaging tool that provides high-resolution images of near-borehole resistivity to identify fractures and features.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012]] used FMI logs from five wells to determine fracture orientation, intensity, and distribution.

- [[2016-saleh|Saleh, 2016]] used borehole imaging to identify stylolites and fracture types in Khuff A, B, and C.

## Key Results and Findings

- FMI data provided a high-resolution picture of near-borehole geometry allowing for the classification of fractures and intrusive contacts. ([[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012, p. 21]])

- No faults or major natural open fractures were identified on borehole resistivity images. ([[2016-saleh|Saleh, 2016, p. 123]])
