# Gravity_Survey

**Summary**: A geophysical method measuring variations in Earth's gravity to map subsurface density differences.

---

- [[2024-chavanidis|Chavanidis, 2024]] used gravity survey to model 3D subsurface structures and identify potential geothermal reservoirs.
