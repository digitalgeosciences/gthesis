# Viscometer

**Summary**: An instrument used to measure the resistance of a fluid to flow (viscosity).

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[1995-okasha|OKASHA, 1995]] used a Contraves Low Shear 30 viscometer to characterize the rheological properties of tar and its mixtures.
