# Two Phase Titration

**Summary**: A method to determine surfactant concentration in a mixture of oil and water using a titrant like HTAB.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[1987-ahmed|Ahmed, 1987]] used two-phase titration to measure residual surfactant concentrations in the presence and absence of crude oil.

- [[1987-fuseni|Fuseni, 1987]] used two-phase titration to determine the concentration of surfactants in the system.
