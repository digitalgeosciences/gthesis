# Atomic Absorption Spectrometry

**Summary**: A spectroscopic technique used to determine the concentration of specific metal ions in a solution.

---

- [[2012-jabbar|JABBAR, 2012]] used AAS to analyze the concentrations of Ca2+ and Mg2+ in various brine samples.

- [[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014]] used AAS to determine calcium and magnesium concentrations in samples for diffusion coefficient calculation.

## Key Results and Findings

- AAS (specifically an ICE-3000) was used to measure calcium and magnesium ion concentrations in brines. ([[2012-jabbar|JABBAR, 2012, p. 54]])

- The results from AAS were used to calculate a diffusion coefficient of 9.099 x 10^-07 cm^2/sec for the 10% spent acid case. ([[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014, p. 132]])
