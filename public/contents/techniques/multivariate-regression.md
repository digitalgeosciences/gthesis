# Multivariate Regression

**Summary**: A statistical method used to find the relationship between multiple independent variables (seismic attributes) and a dependent variable (reservoir property).

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] applied multivariate-regression to establish linear relationships between nine seismic attributes and three reservoir properties.

- [[2017-hasan|Hasan, 2017]] used multivariate-regression to develop a mathematical model for permeability prediction.
