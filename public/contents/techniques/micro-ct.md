# Micro Ct

**Summary**: A 3D imaging technique used to visualize internal pore structures and mineral differences.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2021-ahmed|Ahmed, 2021]] applied Micro-CT to visualize vuggy pore networks and microfractures in core plugs.

- [[2022-abdlmutalib|Abdlmutalib, 2022]] used micro-CT to provide 3D visualization of the pore system.

- [[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024]] used Micro-CT to analyze the extent of hematite invasion after filtration experiments.

- [[2025-refaat|Refaat, 2025]] used micro-ct to characterize the 3D pore system of burrow fillings in the Hanifa Formation.

## Key Results and Findings

- Vugs were found to be mostly isolated, which explains the low permeability observed in many samples. ([[2021-ahmed|Ahmed, 2021, p. 97]])

- Used to visualize pore systems associated with specific sedimentary structures in the Qusaiba Member. ([[2022-abdlmutalib|Abdlmutalib, 2022, p. 23]])

- Used for identifying different phases or materials within the sample and quantifying features like porosity and grain size. ([[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024, p. 43]])

- Micro-CT scans showed porosity values of 7.15%, 4%, and 7.75% for pipe-framework intervals 1, 4, and 5 respectively. ([[2025-refaat|Refaat, 2025, p. 33]])
