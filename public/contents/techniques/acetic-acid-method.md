# Acetic Acid Method

**Summary**: A chemical process used to dissolve carbonate matrix and release microfossils from lithified rock.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-septriandi|Septriandi, 2016]] used acetic acid method to extract foraminifera from the Dam Formation.

- [[2022-khamsin|Khamsin, 2022]] used acetic-acid-method to extract foraminifera from D1 and D2 units of the Dhruma Formation.

## Key Results and Findings

- Concentrations of 60% to 80% with a 10-hour processing time provided the best balance between fossil preservation and matrix removal. ([[2016-septriandi|Septriandi, 2016, p. 53]])

- The acetic acid method was successfully applied to the lower Dhruma limestones, yielding a high diversity of taxa. ([[2022-khamsin|Khamsin, 2022, p. 190]])
