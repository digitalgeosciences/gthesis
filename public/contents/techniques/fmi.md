# Fmi

**Summary**: A borehole imaging tool that provides high-resolution images of near-borehole resistivity to identify fractures and features.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012]] used FMI data from five wells to characterize fracture orientation, distribution, and geometry.

- [[2016-awad|Awad, 2016]] used FMI data to determine lithofacies associations in the Samma, Yabus, and Adar formations.
