# Seismic Refraction

**Summary**: A geophysical method used to determine the depth to bedrock or changes in rock properties using seismic waves.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2000-mohammad|Mohammad, 2000]] applied seismic-refraction to map the boundaries of the aquifer and study acoustic properties.

- [[2023-edigbue|Edigbue, 2023]] used SR to measure seismic wave velocities related to elastic properties and fluids.

- [[2023-alsahwan|AlSahwan, 2023]] used seismic-refraction to identify saturated zones in sabkhas and sand dunes.
