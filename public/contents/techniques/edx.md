# Energy Dispersive X-ray Spectroscopy

**Summary**: Energy-disperscribed X-ray spectroscopy used to determine elemental composition of a sample.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-jabbar|JABBAR, 2012]] applied EDX to quantify elements like Mg, S, and Ca on the treated surfaces.

- [[2013-khan|Khan, 2013]] used EDX to identify the presence of elements like Calcium, Carbon, and Oxygen in the limestone samples.

- [[2013-murtaza|Murtaza, 2013]] applied EDX to identify chemical elements within the cement samples.

- [[2014-adebayo|Adebayo, 2014]] used EDX to determine the elemental composition of the prepared adsorbent materials.

- [[2014-al-ameri|AL-AMERI, 2014]] used EDX to quantify chemical elements like Ca, O, and C in carbonate samples.

- [[2015-musa|Musa, 2015]] used EDX to confirm the presence and quantity of metal ions (Ni, Co, Cr) in the loaded resins.

- [[2015-khan-2|Khan, 2015]] used EDX to analyze the chemical composition of hydration products in cement.

- [[2016-salawu|Salawu, 2016]] used EDX mapping to confirm the chemical composition of the nanofibers and the presence of silver.

- [[2017-amao|Amao, 2017]] used EDX analysis to verify the mineralogy of the test wall in new species like Pseudonubeculina arabica.

- [[2019-salawudeen|Salawudeen, 2019]] used EDX to confirm the presence of nitrogen in GPDADMAC and oxygen/carbon in GPHM.

## Key Results and Findings

- Sulphur deposition was observed on both calcite and carbonate surfaces after treatment with all brines. ([[2012-jabbar|JABBAR, 2012, p. 106]])

- EDX analysis showed that while Calcium, Carbon, and Oxygen are from the rock itself, Chlorine levels varied based on acid interaction. ([[2013-khan|Khan, 2013, p. 105]])

- EDX spectra identified key minerals and elements like Calcium, Silicon, and Iron in the slurry systems. ([[2013-murtaza|Murtaza, 2013, p. 153]])

- EDX showed that raw fly ash is mainly composed of carbon and oxygen, with metal impurities removed after acid treatment. ([[2014-adebayo|Adebayo, 2014, p. 65]])

- EDX analysis for Sample 8 showed Ca at 59.5 wt% and O at 28.4 wt%. ([[2014-al-ameri|AL-AMERI, 2014, p. 98]])

- EDX confirmed Ni(II) at 5.4 and 0.57 keV in the Nickel-loaded resin. ([[2015-musa|Musa, 2015, p. 65]])

- Confirmed high CSH percentage in cement slurry with silica flour and CNT. ([[2015-khan-2|Khan, 2015, p. 124]])

- EDX mapping confirmed that the nanofiber was mostly composed of Carbon and Nitrogen, while also confirming the presence of metallic nanoparticles. ([[2016-salawu|Salawu, 2016, p. 133]])

- EDX confirmed a high-Mg calcite cement (calcium 20.54% and magnesium 3.15%) in the test walls. ([[2017-amao|Amao, 2017, p. 72]])

- EDX showed carbon (81.04%) and oxygen (18.96%) for GPHM, confirming successful functionalization. ([[2019-salawudeen|Salawudeen, 2019, p. 75]])
