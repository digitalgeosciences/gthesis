# Hcpc

**Summary**: A multivariate analysis technique used to group samples based on similar chemical compositions.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2022-septriandi|Septriandi, 2022]] used HCPC to cluster samples into six distinct groups (CG 1-6) based on their geochemistry.

- [[2022-stiawan|Stiawan, 2022]] used HCPC to separate the two main mineralogical clusters (fabric-preserving vs. non-fabric preserving).

- [[2022-argadestya|Argadestya, 2022]] used HCPC-PCA to confirm sediment provenance from Merapi source rocks.

## Key Results and Findings

- The result revealed six distinct clusters identifying associations and gradients of elemental concentrations. ([[2022-septriandi|Septriandi, 2022, p. 59]])

- HCPC confirmed the separation of Cluster 1 and Cluster 2 as distinct groups based on dolomite/calcite content. ([[2022-stiawan|Stiawan, 2022, p. 108]])

- The HCPC-PCA plot with a PC-1 value of 93.99% confirmed the sediment provenance comes from calc-alkaline, basalt-andesitic Merapi source rocks. ([[2022-argadestya|Argadestya, 2022, p. 100]])
