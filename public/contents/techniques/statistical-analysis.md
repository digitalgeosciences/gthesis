# Statistical Analysis

**Summary**: The application of statistical methods to analyze and interpret data related to waste management and environmental risk.

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-attyah|Attyah, 2012]] used statistical analysis to evaluate the risks and treatment options for drilling wastes.

- [[2016-armoh|Armoh, 2016]] used statistical-analysis to correlate evaporation rates with temperature, humidity, and wind speed.

- [[2019-alamran|AlAmran, 2019]] used ANOVA via Microsoft Excel 2019 to compare phytoremediation ability across plant parts and contamination levels.

- [[2023-nasir|Nasir, 2023]] applied multivariate statistical analysis using PAST software to determine community similarity and indicator species.

- [[2023-prayudi|Prayudi, 2023]] used statistical analysis to evaluate diversity indices and quantify morphological deformities in foraminiferal assemblages.

- [[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024]] applied statistical-analysis to determine mean, standard deviation, and coefficient of variation for HM concentrations.

- ANOVA was used to ascertain significant differences in phytoremediation among leaf, bark, and root tissues. ([[2019-alamran|AlAmran, 2019, p. 63]])
