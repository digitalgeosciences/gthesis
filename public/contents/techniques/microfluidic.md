# Microfluidic

**Summary**: A technology that handles small volumes of fluids in channels to perform chemical reactions.

## Key Results and Findings

- The flow reactor gave yields higher than 64% and maximum phenol removal of 86.6%. ([[2015-hassan|Hassan, 2015, p. 16]])

- Microfluidic results showed ultimate recovery of 56% for 4% GLDA with 1.25% VES, while pure VES at 1.25% gave 48%. ([[2023-ahmed|Ahmed, 2023, p. 24]])

- Microfluidic systems allowed for the observation of pore-filling behaviors and the quantification of hydrate growth rates. ([[2024-habiburrahman|HABIBURRAHMAN, 2024, p. 43]])
