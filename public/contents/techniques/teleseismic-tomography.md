# Teleseismic Tomography

**Summary**: A method to map the Earth's interior by analyzing travel-time residuals of distant earthquakes.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2025-kesumastuti|Kesumastuti, 2025]] used teleseismic tomography to construct a 3D velocity model of the lithosphere and upper mantle beneath Sulawesi.
