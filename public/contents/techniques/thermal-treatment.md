# Thermal Treatment

**Summary**: The application of heat to test the stability of a material.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-omer|Omer, 2012]] used thermal-treatment to evaluate the degradation of PAM and its derivatives at temperatures up to 70°C.

- [[1990-ali|Ali, 1990]] applied thermal treatment in a roller oven for up to 30 days to study the degradation and evolution of fluid properties.

## Key Results and Findings

- Pure PAM remained stable up to 70°C, but certain counterion concentrations (e.g., Mg2+ at 0.25%) caused a drop in stability to 40.6°C. ([[2012-omer|Omer, 2012, p. 83]])

- Aging effects on viscosity and yield point were found to be most prominent initially and diminished as aging continued. ([[1990-ali|Ali, 1990, p. 85]])
