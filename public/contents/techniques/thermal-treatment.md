# Thermal Treatment

**Summary**: A process using high temperatures to destroy hydrocarbons and reduce the volume of inorganic contaminants in waste.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-attyah|Attyah, 2012]] used thermal treatment to evaluate methods for destroying organics and reducing metal mobility in drilling wastes.
