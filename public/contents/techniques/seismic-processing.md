## Key Results and Findings

- Data at the CSA stage was selected to ensure consistency across different surveys. ([[2010-al-moqbel|Al-Moqbel, 2010, p. 44]])

- The hybrid approach successfully resolved cycle skips in the pre-stack shot records compared to conventional methods. ([[2011-toma|Toma, 2011, p. 60]])

- The proposed method significantly reduces sand dune amplification in a surface-consistent manner. ([[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017, p. 47]])

- Automated methods like MDPE and DBSCAN are being explored to replace time-consuming manual picking. ([[2021-almashhor|AlMashhor, 2021, p. 21]])

- The proposed workflow including deconvolution and cross-correlation significantly improved stack response in complex dune environments. ([[2022-aljishi|AlJishi, 2022, p. 83]])

- Automatic first break picking is critical for subsequent steps like static correction, velocity analysis, and deconvolution. ([[2022-awais|Awais, 2022, p. 20]])

- Standard processing (AGC, FK filter, deconvolution) was used to prepare the 2D land data from Texas. ([[2023-muhammad|Muhammad, 2023, p. 57]])
