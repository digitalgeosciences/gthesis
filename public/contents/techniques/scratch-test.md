# Scratch Test

**Summary**: A method to measure the strength and deformation of rock samples by scratching them with a diamond tip.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2021-albensaad|Albensaad, 2021]] applied scratch tests using an Epslog tool to determine Unconfined Compressive Strength (UCS).

- [[2022-samarkin|Samarkin, 2022]] applied scratch tests to evaluate the surface hardness and specific energy of samples.

## Key Results and Findings

- Scratch test results were used to derive UCS values for comparison with log-based calculations. ([[2021-albensaad|Albensaad, 2021, p. 43]])

- The scratch test showed an increase in average hardness by 15% for chalk after treatment. ([[2022-samarkin|Samarkin, 2022, p. 57]])
