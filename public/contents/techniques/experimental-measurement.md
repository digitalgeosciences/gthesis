## Key Results and Findings

- Sulfate ion desorption from limestone significantly decreased as temperature increased from 25°C to 90°C. ([[2012-kassim|KASSIM, 2012, p. 165]])

- The IFT700 enabled the determination of contact angles through computer processing of video frames using the Young-Laplace method. ([[2016-mahboob-2|Mahboob, 2016, p. 61]])

- The CO2-TEC blend at 85 mol% CO2 had a viscosity of the same order as reservoir fluids. ([[2019-al-azani|AL-AZANI, 2019, p. 99]])

- Experimental results confirmed that drag-reducing agents significantly reduced the friction factor in pipe flows. ([[2023-al-dogail|AL-DOGAIL, 2023]])

- Experimental results for lowpass gain showed a deviation of less than 1% from theory. ([[1997-al-gahtani-2|Al-Gahtani, 1997, p. 106]])
