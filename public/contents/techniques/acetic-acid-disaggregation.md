# Acetic Acid Disaggregation

**Summary**: A chemical process using acetic acid to dissolve carbonate matrix and release microfossils from lithified limestone.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-jalloh|Jalloh, 2024]] used acetic acid disaggregation to extract foraminiferal species from indurated limestone and marl samples of the Dhruma Formation.
