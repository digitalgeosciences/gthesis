# Ultrasonic Measurement

**Summary**: A non-destructive technique using ultrasonic wave propagation to detect changes in pore fluid and solid content.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2001-mohammed|Mohammed, 2001]] used ultrasonic measurement to determine the extent of formation damage by comparing velocity profiles of dry, saturated, and mud-invaded samples.
