- Identified main throat peaks and petrophysical parameters for various lithofacies. ([[2020-al-zoukani|Al-Zoukani, 2020, p. 102]])

- Core plug-based permeability in F2 ranged from 0.01 to 0.6 mD, while spot measurements reached up to 9 mmD. ([[2022-abdlmutalib|Abdlmutalib, 2022, p. 83]])
