# Multi Objective Optimization

**Summary**: The simultaneous optimization of two or more conflicting objectives.

## Key Results and Findings

- Pareto-based approaches provided multiple alternative solutions, ensuring a balance between profitability and environmental regulations. ([[2014-ahmed-2|Ahmed, 2014, p. 104]])

- Lexicographical approaches (Case 3a and 3b) yielded higher MT-NPV and LT-NPV values than single-objective cases. ([[2014-ali|Ali, 2014, p. 98]])

- A Pareto-optimal approach allows for a range of solutions where different constraints can be weighted. ([[2014-al-ismael|AL-ISMAEL, 2014, p. 19]])

- The NSGA II algorithm successfully identified Pareto optimal fronts for joint inversion. ([[2023-edigbue|Edigbue, 2023, p. 46]])
