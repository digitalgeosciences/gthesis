## Key Results and Findings

- Identified a partially restricted continental shelf as the depositional setting. ([[1996-matter|Matter, 1996, p. 26]])

- A total of five distinctive microfacies were identified in the succession. ([[1992-el-sahn|El-Sahn, 1992, p. 13]])

- Identified 12 lithofacies in the Wadi Waqb member, categorized into five distinct facies associations. ([[2015-koeshidayatullah|Koeshidayatullah, 2015, p. 85]])

- Detailed lithofacies study resulted in 21 different lithofacies, including two sandstone lithofacies. ([[2016-hassan-2|Hassan, 2016, p. 158]])

- Identified a restricted carbonate platform in a very shallow marine or hypersaline lagoonal environment. ([[2016-septriandi|Septriandi, 2016, p. 108]])

- Identified several distinct microfacies such as rudstone and wackestone. ([[2017-elyamani|ElYamani, 2017, p. 67]])

- Identified oolitic, peloidal, and bioclastic lithofacies within the Khartam member. ([[2017-adam|Adam, 2017, p. 64]])

- Identified 8 distinct microfacies including peloidal skeletal wackestone and burrowed wackestone. ([[2018-ismanto|Ismanto, 2018, p. 17]])
