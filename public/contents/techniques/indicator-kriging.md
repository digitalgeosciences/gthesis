# Indicator Kriging

**Summary**: A non-parametric kriging method used to estimate the probability of a variable exceeding a certain threshold.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[1997-al-qassab|Al-Qassab, 1997]] used indicator kriging as an alternative for modeling categorical lithofacies data.

- [[2013-atig|atig, 2013]] used indicator kriging to predict rock bodies and determine optimal semi-variogram parameters.

## Key Results and Findings

- Indicator kriging allows estimation without assuming a specific parametric distribution (e.g., Gaussian). ([[1997-al-qassab|Al-Qassab, 1997, p. 133]])

- Indicator Kriging was determined as the optimum way to predict rock bodies compared to SIS or Object-based models. ([[2013-atig|atig, 2013, p. 12]])
