# Microbiology

**Summary**: The study of microscopic organisms, especially their structure, function, and interaction with their environment.

---

## Key Results and Findings

- Two strains, Pseudoxanthomonas sp. and Bacillus sp., were identified as capable of degrading crude oil. ([[2011-salamah|Salamah, 2011, p. 44]])

- Culturing was attempted but failed to maintain viable cultures for genetic analysis. ([[2012-amao|Amao, 2012, p. 126]])

- Identified 15 bacterial strains belonging to the phylum Proteobacteria. ([[2015-oyehan|Oyehan, 2015, p. 17]])

- Identified four distinct bacteria colonies: Enterobacter spp., Staphylococcus spp., Pseudomonas spp., and Bacillus spp. ([[2015-bello|Bello, 2015, p. 109]])

- Eight types of pathogenic bacteria were identified including E. coli and Vibrio cholera. ([[2015-yusuf|Yusuf, 2015, p. 45]])

- B. fungorum and R. quinshengi showed high resilience in degrading pyrene across different pH levels. ([[2015-malik|Malik, 2015, p. 109]])

- Three strains were successfully isolated: Idiomarina sp. (10PY1A), Halomonas shengliensis (10PY2B), and Halomonas smyrnensis (20PY1A). ([[2017-budiyanto|Budiyanto, 2017, p. 53]])
