# Hydroponics

**Summary**: A method of growing plants in a mineral solution without soil, used here to isolate the effects of contaminants on plant growth.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-bello|Bello, 2015]] used hydroponics to study the uptake of heavy metals and organic pollutants by Bolboschoenus maritimus and Phragmites australis.

## Key Results and Findings
