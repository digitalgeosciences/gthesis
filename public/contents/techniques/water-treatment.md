## Key Results and Findings

- Referenced in this thesis. ([[1994-muhammad|Muhammad, 1994]])

- Gels were used as a substitute for squeeze cementing in near wellbore treatments to shut off water or gas. ([[1997-al-nashmi|Al-Nashmi, 1997, p. 71]])

- Ozone-based AOP is a promising technique for removing organic molecules like phenol. ([[2009-zubair|Zubair, 2009, p. 16]])

- MWCNTs showed high efficiency in removing both Hg(II) and As(III). ([[2009-al-khaldi|Al-Khaldi, 2009, p. 11]])

- Referenced in this thesis. ([[2011-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2011]])

- TiO2 was identified as the most effective photocatalyst among the tested materials. ([[2013-muhyedeen|Muhyedeen, 2013, p. 52]])

- Referenced in this thesis. ([[2012-omer|Omer, 2012]])

- Silver oxide impregnated-FA was identified as a promising candidate for treatment. ([[2014-adebayo|Adebayo, 2014, p. 12]])

- The study evaluates the effectiveness of different materials for capturing CO2. ([[2014-jamil|JAMIL, 2014, p. 84]])

- The proposed system was found to yield adequate gel strength at high temperature using field mixing water (1,122 ppm). ([[2014-mohamed|MOHAMED, 2014, p. 14]])

- Adsorption Evaluation of Cross-linked Polymers for Water Treatment ([[2015-musa|Musa, 2015, p. 13]])

- The combination of polyacrylamides (PAM) and electrolytes significantly improved turbidity reduction and volume of separated water. ([[2015-he|He, 2015, p. 17]])

- The amount of refractory sulfur compounds adsorbed followed the order DBT > BT > T. ([[2015-danmaliki|Danmaliki, 2015, p. 19]])

- Maximum phenol removal reached 86.6%. ([[2015-hassan|Hassan, 2015, p. 16]])

- Referenced in this thesis. ([[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014]])

- Several methods like adsorption, membrane filtration, and chemical precipitation were reviewed as viable options. ([[2016-salawu|Salawu, 2016, p. 36]])

- The UV-H2O2 process achieved 97% removal of Toluene in 15 minutes in deionized water. ([[2016-sameh|Sameh, 2016, p. 15]])

- A multi-component formulation (chelating agent, catalyst, enzyme) was developed to treat and remove filter cake. ([[2016-geri|GERI, 2016, p. 18]])

- Over 90% of the applied phosphate was removed by the three strains at both temperatures. ([[2016-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2016, p. 61]])

- Referenced in this thesis. ([[2016-sidaoui|Sidaoui, 2016]])

- Polyelectrolyte coating significantly improved the adsorption capacity and efficiency of fly ash. ([[2017-olabemiwo|Olabemiwo, 2017, p. 14]])

- SDAC was found to be a viable and cost-effective alternative to commercial activated carbon for water treatment. ([[2018-adeyemi|Adeyemi, 2018, p. 12]])

- GPDADMAC and GPHM showed good potential for phenol and cadmium removal, respectively. ([[2019-salawudeen|Salawudeen, 2019, p. 16]])

- High conductivity in saline water facilitates the formation of chlorine species which favor the oxidation of ammonia. ([[2020-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2020, p. 37]])

- Polyethylenimine modified graphene (PG) was identified as a highly efficient material for both batch and dynamic modes. ([[2021-alnasser|AlNasser, 2021, p. 19]])

- Type 1 demulsifiers (non-ionic) are more robust against varying brine conditions than Type 2. ([[2021-altowilib|Altowilib, 2021, p. 55]])

- Fe-modified biochar achieved 98% arsenic removal at pH 5. ([[2021-siddiq|Siddiq, 2021, p. 50]])

- The study successfully demonstrated that chelating agents can mitigate the impact of high TDS in produced water, allowing it to be used as a base fluid. ([[2021-alkhowaildi|Alkhowaildi, 2021, p. 12]])

- The use of 20 wt% EDTA was found to be highly effective at preventing ferric precipitation across multiple temperatures. ([[2021-gasimli|Gasimli, 2021, p. 115]])

- The study determined that a mixture of Latex and Amines provided superior results in both rheology and inhibition. ([[2022-alshalan|Alshalan, 2022, p. 93]])

- Ionic liquids were used to study the clay swelling inhibitor in drilling fluid application. ([[2021-khan|KHAN, 2021, p. 20]])

- A two-stage approach (WAO followed by electro-oxidation) was evaluated as a viable treatment path. ([[2023-al-aqeel|Al-Aqeel, 2023, p. 13]])

- Nano-silica gel showed superior durability compared to traditional polymers over a 6-month period. ([[2024-suleiman-4|Suleiman, 2024, p. 21]])

- CAC was found to be a feasible adsorbent for remediating groundwater from MTBE and BTEX. ([[2025-almousa|Almousa, 2025, p. 13]])
