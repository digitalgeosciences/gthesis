# Ft Icr Ms

**Summary**: A high-resolution mass spectrometry technique used to characterize complex organic molecules like asphaltenes.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2021-alqam|ALQAM, 2021]] employed FT-ICR MS to determine the molecular structure of asphaltene, sulfur, and nitrogen compounds in five crude oils.
