## Key Results and Findings

- Variograms were used to determine spatial continuity for model construction. ([[1997-al-qassab|Al-Qassab, 1997, p. 73]])

- Determined that the reservoir is not continuous based on comparison between observed and modeled data. ([[2001-al-subai|Al-Subai, 2001, p. 12]])

- A well-calibrated saturation height model populates the three-dimensional geological model with hydrocarbon saturations. ([[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010, p. 37]])

- Hybrid modeling combining IK and OM was found most effective for representing both continuous and discontinuous features. ([[2013-atig|atig, 2013, p. 83]])

- A 3D model with 6000 grid cells was constructed for simulation. ([[2015-adeniji|Adeniji, 2015, p. 70]])
