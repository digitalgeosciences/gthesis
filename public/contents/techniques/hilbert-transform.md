## Key Results and Findings

- The Hilbert transform allows for the calculation of the trace envelope and its derivatives to identify absorption effects. ([[2009-sitouah|Sitouah, 2009, p. 56]])

- The trace envelope is sensitive to acoustic impedance changes, porosity, thin-bed tuning, hydrocarbons, and lithology. ([[2021-almashhor|AlMashhor, 2021, p. 35]])

- The Hilbert transform allows for the calculation of instantaneous attributes like amplitude and frequency. ([[2023-bargees|Bargees, 2023, p. 21]])
