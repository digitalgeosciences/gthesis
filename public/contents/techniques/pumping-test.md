# Pumping Test

**Summary**: A method to determine the hydraulic properties of an aquifer, such as transmissivity and storage coefficient.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[1993-moallim|Moallim, 1993]] used pumping tests to determine hydraulic properties of the Baidoa, Jesomma, Aurado, Taleh, and Merca aquifers.

- [[2020-mansouri|Mansouri, 2020]] applied pumping-test results from multiple wells to evaluate aquifer performance.

## Key Results and Findings

- Pumping test data were used to calculate transmissivity and storage coefficients for various aquifers. ([[1993-moallim|Moallim, 1993, p. 65]])

- Identified distinct differences in hydraulic conductivity between the eastern and western parts of the aquifer. ([[2020-mansouri|Mansouri, 2020, p. 83]])
