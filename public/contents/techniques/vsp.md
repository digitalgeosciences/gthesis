# VSP

**Summary**: A seismic survey where a source is at the surface and receivers are in a borehole to provide high-resolution data.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-kola|Kola, 2014]] used VSP data to evaluate the accuracy of new transmission amplitude approximations.

- [[2023-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2023]] analyzed VSP data specifically the zero-offset corridor stack for velocity prediction.

## Key Results and Findings

- VSP provides a better understanding of the propagating wavefield due to being recorded in situ, close to the formation. ([[2014-kola|Kola, 2014, p. 27]])

- ZVSP provides an accurate time-depth relationship and a corridor stack with minimal multiples. ([[2023-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2023, p. 23]])
