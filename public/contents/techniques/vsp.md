# VSP

**Summary**: A seismic survey method where a source and receiver are moved vertically in a borehole to provide high-resolution data.

---

## Key Results and Findings

- Analysis based on receiver gather is only applicable to VSP data as presented here, due to limitations with lateral inhomogeneity. ([[2014-kola|Kola, 2014, p. 77]])

- Zero-offset VSP (ZVSP) provides a clean corridor stack that is useful for determining time-depth relationships and estimating attenuation. ([[2023-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2023, p. 23]])
