# Point Counting

**Summary**: A method used to quantify the proportions of different components in a thin section.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[1995-magaji|Magaji, 1995]] applied point counting to determine grain size and pore sizes.

- [[2014-mohammedfaiz|Mohammedfaiz, 2014]] applied point counting to quantify mineral proportions in the Siq, Quweira, and Saq Sandstones.
