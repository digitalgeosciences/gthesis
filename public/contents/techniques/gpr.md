# Gpr

**Summary**: A geophysical method to image the subsurface structure using electromagnetic pulses.

## Key Results and Findings

- GPR data provided information on burrow attributes (morphology, size, and percentage) that cannot be obtained from CT scans or outcrop photographs. ([[2024-saraih|Saraih, 2024, p. 20]])
