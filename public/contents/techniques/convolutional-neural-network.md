## Key Results and Findings

- The custom CNN achieved 65.3% accuracy on the lithofacies dataset, significantly lower than the DINOv2 + SVM approach. ([[2025-ferreira|Ferreira, 2025, p. 48]])

- The U-net model achieved an IoU of 97% and showed the best performance in capturing fine details of each rock type. ([[2024-jibrin|Jibrin, 2024, p. 72]])
