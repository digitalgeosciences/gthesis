# UV-Vis Spectroscopy

**Summary**: A technique used to measure the absorbance of light by a sample to determine concentration.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2009-zubair|Zubair, 2009]] used UV/VIS spectrophotometry for the determination of ozone and other chemical parameters in the water samples.

- [[2009-al-khaldi|Al-Khaldi, 2009]] used UV-Vis spectroscopy for monitoring metal ion concentrations.

- [[2014-adebayo|Adebayo, 2014]] used uv-vis-spectroscopy to monitor MTBE concentrations during treatment.

- [[2015-hassan|Hassan, 2015]] used uv-vis-spectroscopy to characterize the absorption properties of various organic dyes like Rose Bengal and Fluorescein.

- [[2016-salawu|Salawu, 2016]] used UV-Vis to monitor the synthesis process and develop a colorimetric detection method for Hg(II).

- [[2016-sameh|Sameh, 2016]] used uv-vis-spectroscopy to monitor toluene and by-products during treatment.
