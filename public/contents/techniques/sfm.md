# Sfm

**Summary**: A process-based modeling approach used to predict sedimentary architecture and geometry by simulating the interplay of allogenic and autogenic factors.

- [[2024-fahmi|Fahmi, 2024]] used SFM to unravel the interplay between different allogenic and autogenic factors contributing in the formation of different sequence architectures in the Marrat Formation.
