# Rock-Eval Pyrolysis

**Summary**: A method used to evaluate the geochemical potential of organic matter in sedimentary rocks.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012]] used Rock-Eval pyrolysis to determine TOC, HI, and Tmax for Hanadir samples from three wells.

- [[2017-mohammed|Mohammed, 2017]] applied Rock-Eval pyrolysis to determine TOC, S1, S2, and Tmax for source rock characterization.
