# Sag Test

**Summary**: A method to measure the settling of weighting materials in drilling fluid over time at specific temperatures.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2018-basfar|BASFAR, 2018]] used sag tests to evaluate barite settling in vertical and deviated wells.

- [[2023-basfar|Basfar, 2023]] used sag tests to evaluate the impact of ilmenite and Micromax on solids sag tendency.

## Key Results and Findings

- The copolymer reduced the sag factor from 0.55/0.60 to approximately 0.50-0.51 across all tested temperatures. ([[2018-basfar|BASFAR, 2018, p. 50]])

- 40 wt.% ilmenite was sufficient to eliminate barite sag in both static and dynamic conditions, achieving a sag factor of ~0.51 and a VSST of 0.5. ([[2023-basfar|Basfar, 2023, p. 125]])
