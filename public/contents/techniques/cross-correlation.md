# Cross Correlation

**Summary**: A mathematical operation that compares the similarity of two signals to identify common features and remove incoherent noise.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-edigbue|Edigbue, 2016]] used cross-correlation to generate virtual traces from raw seismic data to isolate head waves.

## Key Results and Findings
