# Geostatistical Modeling

**Summary**: A suite of statistical methods used to interpolate and model spatial uncertainty in geological data.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-abdalla|Abdalla, 2013]] used geostatistical modeling to create high-resolution 3-D models of lithofacies stacking patterns.

- [[2015-elzain|Elzain, 2015]] applied geostatistical modeling (SIS and SGS) to simulate porosity, permeability, and facies distribution.

- [[2016-hassan-2|Hassan, 2016]] applied geostatistical modeling to create realizations for lithofacies, porosity, and permeability.

- [[2016-makkawi|Makkawi, 2016]] used geostatistical modeling to characterize the 3D distribution of facies and petrophysical properties.

- [[2017-mahgoub|Mahgoub, 2017]] applied 3D geostatistical modeling using SIS and truncated Gaussian simulation to map lithofacies and porosity.

- [[2017-yousif|Yousif, 2017]] applied geostatistical modeling to represent the spatial distribution of lithofacies across defined zones.

## Key Results and Findings

- The 3-D geocellular model captures small-scale reservoir variability, which is reflected in the petrophysical data distribution. ([[2013-abdalla|Abdalla, 2013, p. 18]])

- Used SIS for facies modeling and SGS for property modeling with 20 realizations. ([[2015-elzain|Elzain, 2015, p. 58]])

- Models showed small scale heterogeneity in porosity and permeability even within the same lithofacies. ([[2016-hassan-2|Hassan, 2016, p. 14]])

- Lithofacies were modeled deterministically using Indicator Kriging and stochastically using Truncated Gaussian Simulation and Sequential Indicator Simulation. ([[2016-makkawi|Makkawi, 2016, p. 123]])

- Generated a 3D model showing the decrease of depositional cycles thickness away from the deeper part of the basin. ([[2017-mahgoub|Mahgoub, 2017, p. 84]])

- Used variogram models to characterize the spatial continuity of lithofacies in the D5 and D6 members. ([[2017-yousif|Yousif, 2017, p. 139]])
