# Spectroscopic Holographic Imaging and Reconstruction Method (SHIRM)

**Summary**: A technique using a microprobe to perform in-situ U-Pb dating on small crystals like zircon.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-al-hakimi|Al-Hakimi, 2024]] applied SHRIMP analysis to zircons for precise geochronology.

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] used U-Pb SHRIMP dating on zircons to constrain the ages of the Al-Ghurayyah pluton.

## Key Results and Findings

- Zircon U–Pb SHRIMP dating reveals that granite has Ediacaran- Cryogenian ages (~597- 645 Ma). ([[2024-al-hakimi|Al-Hakimi, 2024, p. 102]])

- U-Pb SHRIMP dating of zircons provides an age of 647 ± 9.3 Ma to 635 Ma for the main pluton. ([[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024, p. 15]])
