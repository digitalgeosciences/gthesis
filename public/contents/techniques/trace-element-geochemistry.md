## Key Results and Findings

- High values of uranium and vanadium can justify the economic exploitation of the ore. ([[1996-matter|Matter, 1996, p. 26]])

- Negative correlation between Fe/Mn and δ18O suggests influence of meteoric fluids. ([[2015-koeshidayatullah|Koeshidayatullah, 2015, p. 119]])

- A-type granites were identified as having high 10,000Ga/Al ratios (>2.5) compared to I-type (<2.5). ([[2024-al-hakimi|Al-Hakimi, 2024, p. 63]])

- All units show a pronounced tetrad effect (TE1,3 > 1.1), indicating high differentiation. ([[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024, p. 72]])
