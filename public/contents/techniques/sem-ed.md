# SEM-ED

**Summary**: Scanning electron microscopy combined with energy-dispersive X-ray spectroscopy to identify mineral phases and elemental composition.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-alqubalee|Alqubalee, 2017]] applied SEM and EDX to determine pore-filling minerals and grain coatings in the Sarah Formation.

- [[1996-matter|Matter, 1996]] used SEM-EDS to study microstructures of the phosphorite ore and perform microchemical analysis on 10 samples.

- [[2013-khan|Khan, 2013]] used sem-ed to characterize the mineralogy and grain texture of the rock samples post-acidizing.

- [[2013-murtaza|Murtaza, 2013]] applied SEM-EDS to observe the microstructure of cement hydration products.

- [[2015-thalab|Thalab, 2015]] used SEM-EDS to confirm the presence of silica and calcium in the cement products.

- [[2015-ali|Ali, 2015]] applied SEM-EDS to characterize grain surfaces and identify elements like Ca, Mg, and Si.

- [[2016-salih|Salih, 2016]] used SEM-EDS to identify evaporite minerals like halite in fractures and characterize dolomite textures.

- [[2016-geri|GERI, 2016]] used SEM-EDS to evaluate the purity of barite before and after dissolution.

- [[2016-elnaeem|Elnaeem, 2016]] used SEM-EDS to characterize the rock sample surfaces.

- [[2017-mohamed|MOHAMED, 2017]] used SEM to investigate the effect of ball milling on the morphological properties of barite.

- [[2017-sadeed|SADEED, 2017]] used SEM-EDS to validate the adsorption of stearic acid on rock surfaces.

- [[2021-alqarni|Alqarni, 2021]] applied SEM-ED to characterize clay minerals, cementation types, and pore connectivity in the samples.

- [[2022-samarkin|Samarkin, 2022]] used SEM-ED to evaluate the grain morphology and chemical composition of carbonate rocks.

- [[2021-serag|SERAG, 2021]] applied SEM-EDS to analyze the internal surface topography of sandstone samples after mud exposure.

- [[2024-muhammed|Muhammed, 2024]] applied SEM-EDX to observe dolomite dissolution and pore blockage by clay minerals.

- [[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024]] used SEM-EDX to determine the morphology and particle shapes of hematite and perlite additives.

- [[2024-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2024]] used SEM to analyze the morphological features of Claytone-IMG 400 and MC-TONE.

- [[2026-fatima|Fatima, 2026]] used SEM-ED to characterize the morphology of grains and identify clogging materials.

## Key Results and Findings

- SEM/EDX identified siderite cement in Well-D and anhydrite in Well-C. ([[2017-alqubalee|Alqubalee, 2017, p. 102]])

- Identified trace elements including V, Cr, Fe, Ni, Cu, Zn, Hg, As, U, Sr, and Y in mineral grains. ([[1996-matter|Matter, 1996, p. 90]])

- SEM was used on 3-4 mm thick slices to provide qualitative description of mineralogy and pore structure. ([[2013-khan|Khan, 2013, p. 48]])

- Confirmed high percentages of calcium and silica in the cement mix, indicating CSH formation. ([[2013-murtaza|Murtaza, 2013, p. 132]])

- Confirmed higher weight percentages of silica and calcium in samples containing 1% and 2% SB. ([[2015-thalab|Thalab, 2015, p. 116]])

- EDS analysis of foraminifera showed an abundance of Ca and Mg, indicating the presence of dolomite. ([[2015-ali|Ali, 2015, p. 70]])

- SEM-EDS identified halite filling fractures in the wackestone facies (f 13). ([[2016-salih|Salih, 2016, p. 105]])

- Used to assess changes in chemical composition during solubility tests. ([[2016-geri|GERI, 2016, p. 57]])

- SEM images showed Pink Desert limestone is a fine-grained, moderately sorted, sub-rounded carbonate with micritized peloids. ([[2016-elnaeem|Elnaeem, 2016, p. 44]])

- Milling produced a very fine powder where small particles clustered due to increased surface energy. ([[2017-mohamed|MOHAMED, 2017, p. 61]])

- SEM-EDS showed that modified calcite had lower calcium concentration (31.9%) compared to unmodified calcite (50.0%). ([[2017-sadeed|SADEED, 2017, p. 112]])

- Illite was identified as the most common clay mineral, appearing as grain coating and detrital grain replacement. ([[2021-alqarni|Alqarni, 2021, p. 97]])

- SEM was utilized to observe changes in rock texture and mineralogy after treatment. ([[2022-samarkin|Samarkin, 2022, p. 12]])

- SEM images showed that microcline (clay) appeared to swell, increasing with longer exposure times. ([[2021-serag|SERAG, 2021, p. 61]])

- Identified dolomite dissolution at specific sites after 50 days of treatment. ([[2024-muhammed|Muhammed, 2024, p. 197]])

- Used to characterize the physical properties of the additives (hemat1e and perlite) to understand their role in drilling fluid behavior. ([[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024, p. 42]])

- SEM images showed that Claytone-IMG 400 had a more uniform distribution and smaller size compared to MC-TONE, which had higher irregularity. ([[2024-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2024, p. 69]])

- SEM images revealed that clumping particles (clastic material) were predominantly composed of calcium, indicating calcite presence. ([[2026-fatima|Fatima, 2026, p. 89]])
