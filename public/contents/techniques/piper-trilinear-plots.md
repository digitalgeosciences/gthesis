**Summary**: A graphical method used to classify groundwater based on its chemical composition.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[1992-hassan|Hassan, 1992]] applied Piper trilinear plots to identify three distinct hydrogeochemical facies in the Alat aquifer and four in the Khobar aquifer.
