# Ultrasonic Cement Analyzer

**Summary**: A method to measure the compressive strength of cement slurry by measuring the transit time of ultrasonic waves.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-amer|Amer, 2012]] used ultrasonic-cement-analyzer (UCA) to evaluate the development of compressive strength over 24 hours at high temperature and pressure.

- [[2013-murtaza|Murtaza, 2013]] used ultrasonic cement analyzer (UCA) to evaluate compressive strength, transit time, and acoustic impedance under HPHT conditions.

- [[2015-thalab|Thalab, 2015]] used ultrasonic-cement-analyzer to monitor real-time strength development under high pressure and temperature.

## Key Results and Findings

- The 2.0% Nano silica system showed a lower compressive strength trend in UCA compared to crushing tests due to multi-sized particles affecting sonic wave paths. ([[2012-amer|Amer, 2012, p. 115]])

- Determined that Class G cement experiences significant degradation in strength at temperatures exceeding 230°F. ([[2013-murtaza|Murtaza, 2013, p. 106]])

- The 1% SB mixture showed the shortest transition time (31 minutes) to reach 500 psi compared to other mixtures. ([[2015-thalab|Thalab, 2015, p. 107]])
