# Gamma Ray

**Summary**: A well logging technique that measures the natural radioactivity of rocks to identify lithology and clay content.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2010-al-mojel|Al-Mojel, 2010]] used gamma-ray logs to differentiate between carbonate facies and overlying/underlying calcareous shales.

- [[2011-al-dhufairi|AL-DHUFAIRI, 2011]] used gamma ray as a predictor variable to identify lithology.

## Key Results and Findings

- Gamma-ray readings were lower in the Lower Fadhili reservoir compared to adjacent shale facies, allowing for clear definition of the reservoir's top and base. ([[2010-al-mojel|Al-Mojel, 2010, p. 67]])

- Gamma Ray (GR) was used to provide geological context in the prediction models. ([[2011-al-dhufairi|AL-DHUFAIRI, 2011, p. 43]])
