# Filtration Test

**Summary**: A procedure to measure the volume and thickness of the filter cake formed by drilling fluids.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-jafarov|Jafarov, 2017]] conducted static filtration tests using a Modified Fann HPHT Filter Press to evaluate fluid loss and cake formation.

- [[2018-basfar|BASFAR, 2018]] conducted filtration tests to evaluate the effect of copolymer on fluid loss.

- [[2022-aljaberi|AlJaberi, 2022]] conducted filtration tests to evaluate the effectiveness of perlite in reducing fluid loss and improving filter cake quality.

## Key Results and Findings

- The test allowed for the determination of optimal sodium silicate concentration to minimize filter cake thickness. ([[2017-jafarov|Jafarov, 2017, p. 57]])

- The copolymer reduced the filtration volume by 20% (from 2.6 to 2.1 cm3/30min). ([[2018-basfar|BASFAR, 2018, p. 64]])

- The addition of 1 lb/bbl of perlite reduced the filtration volume by 32% from 18 cm3 to 12.2 cm3 for ilmenite-based mud. ([[2022-aljaberi|AlJaberi, 2022, p. 118]])
