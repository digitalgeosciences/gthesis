# Nonlinear Regression

**Summary**: A mathematical method for finding the best fit for a set of data points by minimizing the sum of squared differences.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2009-bajaalah|BAJAALAH, 2009]] applied nonlinear regression to analyze pressure pulse decay data and estimate permeability parameters.

- [[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017]] applied nonlinear regression to develop ROP correlation equations.

- [[2018-homoud|Homoud, 2018]] used nonlinear regression to determine the influence of parameters on pressure build-up behavior.

- [[1994-al-fattah|Al-Fattah, 1994]] applied nonlinear regression to develop empirical models for water and oil relative permeability.

## Key Results and Findings

- The non-linear regression program showed close agreement with the Jones method results. ([[2009-bajaalah|BAJAALAH, 2009, p. 115]])

- The results of the ROP correlation developed shows good agreement between the actual and the calculated ROP, with absolute error less than 12%. ([[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017, p. 147]])

- Used to develop an empirical correlation for early-time wellbore pressure build-up. ([[2018-homoud|Homoud, 2018, p. 78]])

- The new equations were both evaluated using the data utilized in development and validated against published data. ([[1994-al-fattah|Al-Fattah, 1994, p. 16]])
