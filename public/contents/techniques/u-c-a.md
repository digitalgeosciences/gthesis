# U C A

**Summary**: A method using ultrasonic waves to measure the development of compressive strength in cement.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-amer|Amer, 2012]] used UCA to evaluate the rate of compressive strength development via sonic wave transit times.

- [[2015-khan-2|Khan, 2015]] used UCA to evaluate compressive strength, transit time, and acoustic impedance under HPHT conditions.

## Key Results and Findings

- The 'sonic waves' test revealed that 1.0% Nano silica allowed for faster development of compressive strength. ([[2012-amer|Amer, 2012, p. 126]])

- The 0.5% CNT system showed a significant increase in compressive strength compared to standard Class G cement at high temperatures. ([[2015-khan-2|Khan, 2015, p. 100]])
