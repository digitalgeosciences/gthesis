# Rce

**Summary**: An electrochemical technique used to study the effect of hydrodynamics on corrosion.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[1994-shokoya|Shokoya, 1994]] applied RCE to determine the impact of agitation and flow rate on corrosion rates in drilling mud.
