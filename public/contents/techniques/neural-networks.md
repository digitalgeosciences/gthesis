# Neural Networks

**Summary**: A machine learning approach used here to automate the classification of lithofacies from seismic attributes.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2002-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2002]] applied neural-networks to determine spatial distribution of lithofacies and calibrate petrophysical models.

- [[2002-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2002]] applied neural-networks to lithofacies classification and porosity prediction from wireline logs.

- [[2009-sitouah|Sitouah, 2009]] used neural networks to predict reservoir properties like porosity, permeability, and lithofacies from well log data and seismic attributes.

- [[2013-el-kheir|El-Kheir, 2013]] used neural-networks for facies classification based on GR, DT, NPHI, RHOZ, and RLA5 logs.

- [[2013-atig|atig, 2013]] used neural networks to predict depositional facies in 188 non-cored wells based on RHOB, NPHI, VSH, and PHIE logs.

- [[2015-fuad|Fuad, 2015]] used neural-networks (ANN) to correlate multi-attribute transforms with well logs for porosity and lithology prediction.

## Key Results and Findings

- Neural Network technology was used for multi-attribute regression to generate petrophysical models with higher confidence. ([[2002-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2002, p. 32]])

- Genetically derived porosity showed better agreement with neutron porosity than non-genetic methods. ([[2002-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2002, p. 23]])

- Neural Networks are identified as powerful tools for modeling non-linear, complex systems compared to traditional regression methods. ([[2009-sitouah|Sitouah, 2009, p. 20]])

- Used to classify rock types into distinct lithofacies categories. ([[2013-el-kheir|El-Kheir, 2013, p. 24]])

- Grouping similar facies into a 'fluvial' category significantly improved the correlation coefficients for prediction. ([[2013-atig|atig, 2013, p. 43]])

- ANNs were utilized to overcome the limitations of standard statistical tools in predicting reservoir properties from non-linear seismic data. ([[2015-fuad|Fuad, 2015, p. 30]])
