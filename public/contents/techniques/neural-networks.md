# Neural Networks

**Summary**: A machine learning approach used here to automate the classification of lithofacies from seismic attributes.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2002-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2002]] applied neural-networks to determine spatial distribution of lithofacies and calibrate petrophysical models.

- [[2002-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2002]] applied neural-networks to lithofacies classification and porosity prediction from wireline logs.

- [[2009-sitouah|Sitouah, 2009]] used neural networks to predict reservoir properties like porosity, permeability, and lithofacies from well log data and seismic attributes.

- [[2013-el-kheir|El-Kheir, 2013]] used neural-networks for facies classification based on GR, DT, NPHI, RHOZ, and RLA5 logs.

- [[2013-atig|atig, 2013]] used neural networks to predict depositional facies in 188 non-cored wells based on RHOB, NPHI, VSH, and PHIE logs.

- [[2015-fuad|Fuad, 2015]] used neural-networks (ANN) to correlate multi-attribute transforms with well logs for porosity and lithology prediction.

- [[2019-al-dawood|Al-Dawood, 2019]] used neural-networks to predict porosity and gas saturation from inversion results.

- [[2020-abdallah|Abdallah, 2020]] used neural-networks to model heterogeneous channel reservoirs and predict permeability.

- [[2021-shaheen|Shaheen, 2021]] used artificial neural networks (ANN) as the foundational framework for developing a deep learning approach to detect seismic events.

- [[2021-al-malki|Al-Malki, 2021]] applied neural networks to predict the rheological properties of drilling fluids.

- [[2023-kusuma|Kusuma, 2023]] used ANN to predict missing log porosity with an R2 of 0.95.

- [[2023-alzayer|Alzayer, 2023]] utilized neural-networks (specifically 'White Box') to model sonic times and other well log parameters.

- [[2023-gad|Gad, 2023]] used ANN to model the relationship between well logs and water saturation.

- [[2023-suleymanli|Suleymanli, 2023]] implemented a feed-forward artificial neural network to interpolate missing receiver values.

- [[2023-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2023]] used neural networks to automate the extraction of velocity information from VSP corridor stacks.

- [[2024-hassan|Hassan, 2024]] used neural-networks for regional correlation and prediction of rock properties.

- [[2025-ferreira|Ferreira, 2025]] applied neural networks, specifically CNNs and ViTs, for image classification and segmentation.

## Key Results and Findings

- Neural Network technology was used for multi-attribute regression to generate petrophysical models with higher confidence. ([[2002-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2002, p. 32]])

- Genetically derived porosity showed better agreement with neutron porosity than non-genetic methods. ([[2002-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2002, p. 23]])

- Neural Networks are identified as powerful tools for modeling non-linear, complex systems compared to traditional regression methods. ([[2009-sitouah|Sitouah, 2009, p. 20]])

- Used to classify rock types into distinct lithofacies categories. ([[2013-el-kheir|El-Kheir, 2013, p. 24]])

- Grouping similar facies into a 'fluvial' category significantly improved the correlation coefficients for prediction. ([[2013-atig|atig, 2013, p. 43]])

- ANNs were utilized to overcome the limitations of standard statistical tools in predicting reservoir properties from non-linear seismic data. ([[2015-fuad|Fuad, 2015, p. 30]])

- Porosity derived from neural networks showed a matching value of approximately 78% compared to log porosity. ([[2019-al-dawood|Al-Dawood, 2019, p. 99]])

- The ANN-SAE system was tested using 40 reservoir models, showing successful performance in predicting properties. ([[2020-abdallah|Abdallah, 2020, p. 31]])

- The CNN, based on ANN principles, successfully identified moveout patterns in borehole data to distinguish signal from noise. ([[2021-shaheen|Shaheen, 2021, p. 29]])

- Artificial neural networks were utilized for real-time prediction of mud rheology and evaluating cuttings transport efficiency. ([[2021-al-malki|Al-Malki, 2021, p. 101]])

- ANN reached 0.95 R2, outperforming other algorithms for 1D porosity prediction. ([[2023-kusuma|Kusuma, 2023, p. 17]])

- Artificial Neural Networks are utilized for predicting lithology, identifying drilling hazards, and estimating rock properties. ([[2023-alzayer|Alzayer, 2023, p. 34]])

- ANN can solve mapping problems by finding a close approximation of the correlation between input and output data. ([[2023-gad|Gad, 2023, p. 20]])

- The four-layer feed-forward network with 60 nodes per layer successfully restored input shapes for the FNO model when data was missing. ([[2023-suleymanli|Suleymanli, 2023, p. 31]])

- The consecutive neural network (split-training) approach outperformed standalone models by providing faster results and higher accuracy. ([[2023-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2023, p. 16]])

- Used as a tool for predicting reservoir properties in the context of large-scale variability. ([[2024-hassan|Hassan, 2024, p. 22]])

- Deep learning models like CNNs are standard but require large labeled datasets which are often unavailable in geosciences. ([[2025-ferreira|Ferreira, 2025, p. 16]])
