# Zeta Potential Analysis

**Summary**: A measurement of the surface charge of particles in a suspension to determine stability.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-elhag|ELHAG, 2014]] used zeta potential analysis to evaluate the interaction forces and separation mechanism of bentonite particles.

- [[2015-musa|Musa, 2015]] used Zeta potential analysis to evaluate electrostatic surface interactions during adsorption.

## Key Results and Findings

- The addition of soda ash and heating/stirring treatment improved the dispersion characteristics by moving ZP values toward more negative numbers. ([[2014-elhag|ELHAG, 2014, p. 101]])

- Zeta potentials are often used as an important parameter in analyzing the electrostatic surface interaction in adsorption. ([[2015-musa|Musa, 2015, p. 45]])

- Higher charge density PAM led to higher zeta potential, increasing electrostatic repulsion and making droplet coalescence more difficult. ([[2015-he|He, 2015, p. 59]])
