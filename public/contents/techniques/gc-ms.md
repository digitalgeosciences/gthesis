# Gas Chromatography–Mass Spectrometry

**Summary**: A technique used to separate and identify compounds in a mixture for quantification.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2011-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2011]] used GC-MS with a Purge and Trap technique to quantify TTHMs.

- [[2011-alsadeq|Alsadeq, 2011]] Referenced in this thesis.

- [[2013-muhyedeen|Muhyedeen, 2013]] applied gc-ms to determine the concentration of MTBE, acetone, TBA, and TBF.

- [[2013-mohammed|Mohammed, 2013]] used GC-MS to quantify OCPs and OPPs in sea water, sediment, and biota samples.

- [[2014-adebayo|Adebayo, 2014]] used GC-MS to analyze the concentration of MTBE in the samples collected during batch experiments.

- [[2015-oyehan|Oyehan, 2015]] used GC-MS to quantify the degradation of phenanthrene, pyrene, and diesel components.

- [[2015-bello|Bello, 2015]] used GC/MS to analyze naphthalene concentrations in the water and plant tissues.

- [[2015-akere|Akere, 2015]] used GC-MS to quantify six organotin species in sediment and biota samples.

- [[2015-malik|Malik, 2015]] applied GC/MS to detect and analyze residual PAHs after SPME extraction.

- [[2016-sameh|Sameh, 2016]] used GC-MS to quantify toluene and its by-products during the treatment process.

- [[2017-budiyanto|Budiyanto, 2017]] used GC-MS to quantify pyrene concentration during the biodegradation process.

- [[2018-amiruddin|Amiruddin, 2018]] used GC-MS to determine the concentration of PAHs in Arabian Light crude oil, WSF, and SPP samples.

- [[2018-adeyemi|Adeyemi, 2018]] used GC-MS to quantify MTBE concentrations in the aqueous samples during batch experiments.

- [[2019-alamran|AlAmran, 2019]] used GC-MS for analyzing aromatic hydrocarbons (PAHs) in sediment and plant tissues.

- [[2019-alhaiqi|Alhaiqi, 2019]] used GC/MS to analyze the concentration of Phenanthrene, Fluoranthene, and Pyrene in soil and plant tissues.

- [[2020-usman|Usman, 2020]] applied GC-MS for biomarker analysis of steranes and hopanes.

- [[2024-almutawa|AlMutawa, 2024]] used GC-FID to measure soil TPHs and analyze the degradation of hydrocarbon components.

- [[2025-almousa|Almousa, 2025]] used GC/MS to determine the MTBE and BTEX concentrations before and after treatment.

## Key Results and Findings

- Bromoform was identified as the dominant component (over 80%) of THMs formed during chlorination. ([[2011-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2011, p. 52]])

- Referenced in this thesis. ([[2011-alsadeq|Alsadeq, 2011]])

- TBF concentrations were significantly reduced to below detectable levels (<5 ppb) under optimal conditions. ([[2013-muhyedeen|Muhyedeen, 2013, p. 73]])

- Successfully identified and quantified 11 different pesticides using GC-MS with specific oven programs and calibration curves. ([[2013-mohammed|Mohammed, 2013, p. 70]])

- The GC-MS was calibrated to cover a range of 0.5 to 5,000 μg/L for MTBE analysis. ([[2014-adebayo|Adebayo, 2014, p. 49]])

- Used to assess the reduction of 100 ppm of phenanthrene by approximately 95% in specific isolates. ([[2015-oyehan|Oyehan, 2015, p. 18]])

- The system was used to confirm removal percentages of 96% and 91% for naphthalene by the two species. ([[2015-bello|Bello, 2015, p. 53]])

- The system was run by a Chemstation D.01.02.16, fitted with NIST 98.L library, using an Agilent 19091Z-213 column. ([[2015-akere|Akere, 2015, p. 29]])

- The GC system used an Agilent Technologies (series 6890N) with an inert XL EI/CI MSD (series 5975B). ([[2015-malik|Malik, 2015, p. 43]])

- GC-MS was used to confirm that H2O2 alone did not significantly reduce toluene levels, while UV-H2O2 successfully removed it. ([[2016-sameh|Sameh, 2016, p. 57]])

- Quantified residual pyrene concentrations in samples after liquid-liquid extraction. ([[2017-budiyanto|Budiyanto, 2017, p. 49]])

- The analysis identified specific PAH concentrations in various oil fractions. ([[2018-amiruddin|Amiruddin, 2018, p. 48]])

- The system was calibrated for an MTBE range of 0.5 to 5,000 μg/L. ([[2018-adeyemi|Adeyemi, 2018, p. 59]])

- Detected mean PAH concentrations ranging from 44.26 to 511.7 ng/g across different tissue types. ([[2019-alamran|AlAmran, 2019, p. 15]])

- The system was equipped with an ISQ single quadrupole mass spectrometer and a Triplus Autosampler for analysis. ([[2019-alhaiqi|Alhaiqi, 2019, p. 48]])

- Analysis revealed a mixture of Type II/III and Type III kerogen initially, but later confirmed as mature Type II. ([[2020-usman|Usman, 2020, p. 85]])

- The method was used to determine that crude oil contains various hydrocarbons including aromatic and aliphatic compounds. ([[2024-almutawa|AlMutawa, 2024, p. 27]])

- Used to quantify concentration of MTBE, Benzene, Toluene, Ethylbenzene, and Xylene. ([[2025-almousa|Almousa, 2025, p. 30]])
