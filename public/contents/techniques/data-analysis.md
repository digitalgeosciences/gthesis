## Key Results and Findings

- Data were smoothed for $T_r = 1.05$ and $1.10$ to resolve inconsistencies before correlation evaluation. ([[1995-al-khamis|Al-Khamis, 1995, p. 48]])

- A lower value of AAPE indicates a better correlation. ([[1989-al-muraikhi|Al-Muraikhi, 1989, p. 83]])

- Correlation analysis showed that GPM had a very strong linear relationship with ROP (R=92%). ([[2017-al-abduljabbar|Al-AbdulJabbar, 2017, p. 63]])

- Correlation between ECD-PWD and GPM was found to be 0.951726. ([[2023-murif|Murif, 2023, p. 140]])

- Comparison between original and modified coefficients showed significant adjustments for specific parameters like a1 in the Glaso GOR model (from 2.8869 to 2.876). ([[2005-ademola|ADEMOLA, 2005, p. 159]])
