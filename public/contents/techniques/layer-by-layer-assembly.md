# Layer-by-Layer Assembly

**Summary**: A method to build multi-layered structures by alternating the deposition of oppositely charged polyelectrolytes.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-olabemiwo|Olabemiwo, 2017]] applied layer-by-layer assembly to coat fly ash with PDADMAC and PSS to enhance its adsorption capacity.
