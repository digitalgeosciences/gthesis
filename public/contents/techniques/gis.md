# GIS

**Summary**: A system for capturing, managing, and displaying geographic information.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-al-rowaished|Al-Rowaished, 2012]] applied gis to calculate the area of polygons and quantify reclaimed land.

- [[2014-waseem|Waseem, 2014]] used GIS to link petrophysical and sedimentological data to specific outcrop locations in the Al-Ilb paleochannel.

- [[2014-adedapo|Adedapo, 2014]] used GIS (ArcMAP, Surfer) to produce geochemical maps and identify hot spots.

- [[2015-jawad|Jawad, 2015]] used GIS to map trace metals distribution and water level fluctuations in the Sabkha Al-Fasl.

- [[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016]] used GIS to integrate shoreline types, biological resources, and socio-economic features into a unified sensitivity map.

- [[2016-yasin|Yasin, 2016]] used GIS to map larval breeding sites and analyze the spatial distribution of mosquitoes in the Eastern Province.

- [[2022-mustapha|Mustapha, 2022]] used GIS to georeference terrain data and define receptor locations in the Dammam area.

- [[2025-al-afif|Al-Afif, 2025]] used QGIS to generate AQI distribution maps based on CALPUFF results.

## Key Results and Findings

- GIS was used to draw the boundary of the Eastern Province coast line and quantify the reclaimed land for different periods. ([[2012-al-rowaished|Al-Rowaished, 2012, p. 37]])

- GIS was used to create 'smart maps' where attributes like SEM images and XRD results were linked to georeferenced coordinates. ([[2014-waseem|Waseem, 2014, p. 172]])

- Identified specific areas of high metal concentration in the Dammam area. ([[2014-adedapo|Adedapo, 2014, p. 55]])

- ArcGIS 10.1 was used to develop spatial variation maps of trace metal concentrations and seasonal water levels. ([[2015-jawad|Jawad, 2015, p. 14]])

- GIS allowed the integration of different spatial data with standard tools to identify and describe spatial associations in the Gulf coast. ([[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016, p. 19]])

- GIS was used to create thematic maps and determine buffer zones to identify high-risk areas for mosquito control. ([[2016-yasin|Yasin, 2016, p. 51]])

- Not stated in source ([[2022-mustapha|Mustapha, 2022]])

- AQI values were converted to maps using QGIS to visualize concentration levels across the study domain. ([[2025-al-afif|Al-Afif, 2025, p. 36]])
