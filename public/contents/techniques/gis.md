# GIS

**Summary**: Geographic Information Systems are used for spatial analysis and mapping of environmental data.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2011-alsadeq|Alsadeq, 2011]] used ArcGIS to develop a spatial analysis of all measured parameters of groundwater analyzed samples.

- [[2012-al-rowaished|Al-Rowaished, 2012]] used ArcView GIS to create coastal line mosaic images and quantify land reclamation area.

- [[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012]] used GIS to delineate gravity lineaments and process satellite imagery for surface feature mapping.

- [[2014-waseem|Waseem, 2014]] used GIS to construct a smart map of the Sarah Formation's distribution and features.

- [[2014-adedapo|Adedapo, 2014]] used GIS to perform spatial interpolation and identify high-risk 'hot spots' of metal concentration.

- [[2015-jawad|Jawad, 2015]] utilized ArcGIS 10.1 to develop maps showing trace metal distribution and water level fluctuations.

- [[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016]] used GIS to integrate shoreline types, biological resources, and socio-economic features into a unified mapping system.

- [[2016-yasin|Yasin, 2016]] used GIS to map the location of larvae breeding sites in the study area.

- [[2017-mahgoub|Mahgoub, 2017]] used GIS to analyze satellite imagery and map lineaments.

- [[2017-benaafi|Benaafi, 2017]] used GIS to delineate lineaments and perform statistical analysis on orientation and length.

## Key Results and Findings

- Spatial maps were created by using ArcGIS to explore the relationship between element distribution and contamination sources. ([[2011-alsadeq|Alsadeq, 2011, p. 50]])

- Used to integrate multi-temporal satellite imagery into a unified coordinate system for comparison. ([[2012-al-rowaished|Al-Rowaished, 2012, p. 23]])

- GIS was utilized to delineate the gravity lineaments features from both 2km and 5km resolution datasets. ([[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012, p. 64]])

- Created a 'Sarah Formation Smart Map' to visualize geological data spatially. ([[2014-waseem|Waseem, 2014, p. 148]])

- Enabled the production of geochemical maps showing the distribution of trace metals across different land uses. ([[2014-adedapo|Adedapo, 2014, p. 17]])

- Spatial variation maps were developed in GIS to show the distribution of metals across the study area. ([[2015-jawad|Jawad, 2015, p. 14]])

- The GIS method successfully integrated different spatial data with standard tools to identify and describe spatial associations in the Gulf coast. ([[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016, p. 19]])

- GIS combined with GPS facilitated the identification and mapping of larval breeding sites across eight locations in the Eastern Province. ([[2016-yasin|Yasin, 2016, p. 16]])

- Over 120 regional-scale lineaments were identified using ArcMap. ([[2017-mahgoub|Mahgoub, 2017, p. 41]])

- Used ArcGIS 10.2 to process satellite images and DEMs for mapping lineament traces across multiple scales. ([[2017-benaafi|Benaafi, 2017, p. 54]])
