# Phytoremediation

**Summary**: The use of plants and associated microorganisms to remove, contain, or degrade environmental contaminants.

## Key Results and Findings

- Both B. maritimus and P. australis showed high removal efficiencies (83-99%) for heavy metals. ([[2015-bello|Bello, 2015, p. 14]])

- Avicennia marina is considered an efficient species for sequestration of petroleum hydrocarbons. ([[2019-alamran|AlAmran, 2019, p. 16]])

- Phytoremediation is both economic and environmentally friendly because it saves money and minimizes soil erosion. ([[2019-alhaiqi|Alhaiqi, 2019, p. 14]])
