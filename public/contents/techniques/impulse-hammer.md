# Impulse Hammer

**Summary**: A non-destructive method to measure rock elastic properties like reduced Young's modulus.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2020-gbiyanju|Gbiyanju, 2020]] applied impulse hammer measurements to obtain a continuous geomechanical profile of the reservoir rocks.

- [[2022-samarkin|Samarkin, 2022]] used impulse hammering via the AutoscanTM device to determine the hardness and Young's modulus of carbonate rocks.

- [[2023-alabdrabalnabi|ALABDRABALNABI, 2023]] used an impulse hammer to evaluate the changes in rock hardness after acid treatment.
