# Nodal Analysis

**Summary**: A method used to model the performance of wellbore and reservoir systems by analyzing pressure drops across various components.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2011-almubarak|ALMUBARAK, 2011]] used nodal analysis to model reservoir inflow and well outflow as functions of flow rate for three multilateral configurations.

- [[2024-moh-ali|Moh-Ali, 2024]] used nodal-analysis in PIPESIM to evaluate the impact of TCF on well deliverability.

## Key Results and Findings

- The methodology successfully modeled both inflow and outflow independently, allowing for accurate prediction of total rates based on valve settings. ([[2011-almubarak|ALMUBARAK, 2011, p. 50]])

- Nodal analysis showed that TCF injection shifted the VLP curve, enabling an operating point for gas flow. ([[2024-moh-ali|Moh-Ali, 2024, p. 78]])
