# Petrel

**Summary**: A 3D modeling software used for reservoir engineering and geological modeling.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012]] used Petrel to build the 3D grid and model fracture attributes.

- [[2013-atig|atig, 2013]] used Petrel to integrate well logs, perform neural network analysis, and build the 3D geocellular model.

- [[2013-abdalla|Abdalla, 2013]] used Petrel to construct a 3-D geostatistical model of the outcrop analog.

- [[2015-jabir|Jabir, 2015]] used Petrel for the construction of the 3D geocellular model.

- [[2015-elzain|Elzain, 2015]] used Petrel to construct the 3D grid and perform geostatistical simulations of porosity and permeability.

- [[2016-hassan-2|Hassan, 2016]] used Petrel to perform 3D modeling of lithofacies, porosity, and permeability.

- [[2016-makkawi|Makkawi, 2016]] used Petrel to map the distribution of sample points and visualize outcrop sections as pseudo-wells.

- [[2017-mahgoub|Mahgoub, 2017]] used Petrel to create a 3D geostatistical model of the lithofacies distribution.

- [[2017-yousif|Yousif, 2017]] used Petrel to simulate facies and petrophysical properties (porosity and permeability).

- [[2017-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2017]] used Petrel to construct 3D geostatistical models of lithofacies and petrophysical properties.

## Key Results and Findings

- The model was built on a 124 sq km area with a cell size of 50x50m across 25 zones. ([[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012, p. 112]])

- Not stated in source ([[2013-atig|atig, 2013]])

- The 3D model was constructed using 14 stratigraphic sections to represent lithofacies boundaries and petrophysical data. ([[2013-abdalla|Abdalla, 2013, p. 60]])

- Not stated in source ([[2015-jabir|Jabir, 2015]])

- The software allowed for the integration of high-resolution outcrop data into a 3D model with an average vertical resolution of 1m. ([[2015-elzain|Elzain, 2015, p. 132]])

- The software allowed for the generation of 40 realizations of porosity and permeability models. ([[2016-hassan-2|Hassan, 2016, p. 125]])

- The software was used to generate a map showing the distribution of samples across the study area. ([[2016-makkawi|Makkawi, 2016, p. 71]])

- The 3D model successfully mapped the distribution of porosity and lithofacies across three depositional zones. ([[2017-mahgoub|Mahgoub, 2017, p. 65]])

- Petrel was used to perform kriging and simulation to develop 3D models of the Dhruma Formation. ([[2017-yousif|Yousif, 2017, p. 81]])

- Used to model the distribution of facies and properties in a 3D grid. ([[2017-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2017, p. 197]])
