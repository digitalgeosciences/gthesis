# Fuzzy Logic

**Summary**: A form of computing based on fuzzy set theory to handle uncertainty and ambiguity in data.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2011-al-shammari|Al-Shammari, 2011]] used Fuzzy Logic to model the prediction of bottom-hole pressure by handling non-crisp inputs like flow conditions.

- [[2014-al-harbi|Al-Harbi, 2014]] used fuzzy-logic (specifically Type-2) to manage the uncertainties inherent in carbonate reservoir petrophysics.

- [[2018-al-mashhad|Al-Mashhad, 2018]] applied Fuzzy Logic using the Cluster Radius method to evaluate well performance.

- [[2019-ahmed|Ahmed, 2019]] applied fuzzy logic (FL) to predict lost circulation zones.

- [[2020-abdallah|Abdallah, 2020]] evaluated fuzzy logic as a method for handling uncertainty in reservoir property estimation and lithology classification.

- [[2022-alsulaiman|AlSulaiman, 2022]] applied fuzzy-logic to predict productivity folds and compare it against SVM and ANN models.

- [[2022-alkathim|Alkathim, 2022]] utilized fuzzy logic as one of the three AI techniques for predicting acidizing efficiency.

- [[2024-almomen|Almomen, 2024]] applied fuzzy logic to evaluate ROP prediction in cases with uncertain data inputs.

## Key Results and Findings

- The ANFIS model, utilizing a Sugeno-type FIS, was found to be more compact and efficient for representing rules compared to Mamdani types. ([[2011-al-shammari|Al-Shammari, 2011, p. 33]])

- T2FLS was successfully used to predict permeability and porosity in both gas and oil reservoirs. ([[2014-al-harbi|Al-Harbi, 2014, p. 34]])

- Not stated in source (compared only generally against other models). ([[2018-al-mashhad|Al-Mashhad, 2018]])

- Fuzzy logic was one of the five AI techniques tested with high accuracy (R > 0.98). ([[2019-ahmed|Ahmed, 2019, p. 20]])

- Fuzzy logic offered the best estimation of permeability compared to multilinear regression or direct correlation. ([[2020-abdallah|Abdallah, 2020, p. 32]])

- The fuzzy logic model performed better than the SVM model with a CC of 0.969 and AAPE of 6.06%. ([[2022-alsulaiman|AlSulaiman, 2022, p. 68]])

- Not stated in source (compared against ANN and SVM). ([[2022-alkathim|Alkathim, 2022, p. 85]])

- The fuzzy logic model was used to provide the best decision based on available information regarding drilling parameters. ([[2024-almomen|Almomen, 2024, p. 38]])
