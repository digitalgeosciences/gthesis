# Morphometric Measurement

**Summary**: The quantitative analysis of the size and shape of biological structures to identify species.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-amao|Amao, 2012]] used morphometric measurements of apical, vegetative, and reproductive cells to differentiate species of endolithic characteristics.
