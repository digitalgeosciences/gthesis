## Key Results and Findings

- The inclusion of nano-silica resulted in an increase in compressive strength for the cement samples. ([[2012-amer|Amer, 2012, p. 45]])

- The addition of 0.05 wt% carbon nanotubes resulted in up to 70% increase in compressive strength. ([[2015-khan-2|Khan, 2015, p. 55]])

- Determined that fiber addition improves mechanical properties including compressive strength. ([[2017-ahmed|Ahmed, 2017, p. 17]])

- Compressive strength at 180°C with 3% ferric nitrate reached over 1700 psi in just 4 hours. ([[2020-hiba|HIBA, 2020, p. 58]])
