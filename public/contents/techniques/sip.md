# SIP

**Summary**: A geophysical technique that measures complex impedance to provide information on pore size and fluid properties.

---

## How It Is Used in the Thesis Collection

- [[2021-siddiq|Siddiq, 2021]] applied SIP to monitor the breakthrough and exhaustion of arsenic in a column system.

- [[2023-badhafere|Badhafere, 2023]] used SIP to quantify pyrite concentration in limestone and monitor drilling fluid components.

## Key Results and Findings

- SIP monitoring showed changes in recorded signals consistent with the removal of arsenic as confirmed by chemical analysis. ([[2021-siddiq|Siddiq, 2021, p. 12]])

- Successfully detected pyrite as low as 0.25% by volume and monitored barite/calcite sag within 3 hours. ([[2023-badhafere|Badhafere, 2023, p. 16]])
