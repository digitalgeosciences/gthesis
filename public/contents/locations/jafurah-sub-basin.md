## Key Findings From This Location

- The basin is considered a new frontier unconventional play equivalent to the Eagle Ford in Texas. ([[2017-mohammed|Mohammed, 2017, p. 16]])

- The Tuwaiq Mountain formation within this basin is a key target for gas production. ([[2018-aleid|Aleid, 2018, p. 23]])

- The Jafurah Sand Sea covers approximately 40,000 km2 and is characterized by Barchanoid dunes and a high humidity belt. ([[2022-al-alawi|Al-Alawi, 2022, p. 37]])

- The basin contains multiple organic-rich source-rock formations, including those of Kimmeridgian age. ([[2020-usman|Usman, 2020, p. 21]])

- The basin contains three organic-rich source rocks: Tuwaiq Mountain, Hanifa, and Jubaila Formations. ([[2021-lawal|LAWAL, 2021, p. 19]])
