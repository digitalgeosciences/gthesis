## Key Findings From This Location

- Three outcrops (Northwestern, Central, Southeastern) were identified and sampled. ([[2016-abbas|Abbas, 2016, p. 12]])

- The outcrop provided a clear view of multi-scale fractures and distinct lithofacies. ([[2017-mahgoub|Mahgoub, 2017, p. 71]])

- Two sections (Section 1 and Section 2) were identified near the site. ([[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018, p. 21]])
