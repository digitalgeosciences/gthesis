## Key Findings From This Location

- The area provides a unique opportunity for studying carbonate platforms in active rifting. ([[2015-koeshidayatullah|Koeshidayatullah, 2015, p. 32]])

- The subsoil conditions along the Red Sea coast are complex due to existence of coralline limestone beds. ([[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017, p. 15]])

- The Wajh Basin is one of the most prominent sedimentary basins of the Red Sea, with 130 km in length and 50 km in width. ([[2022-al-sadah|Al-Sadah, 2022, p. 22]])

- The Al Wajh Formation provides a unique opportunity to study early rift alluvial-to-fluvial systems. ([[2023-butt|Butt, 2023, p. 30]])
