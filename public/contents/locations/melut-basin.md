# Melut Basin

**Summary**: A sedimentary basin in Sudan characterized by a complex history of rifting and faulting.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012]] focused on the Melut basin to study fractured basement reservoirs.

## Geological and Structural Setting

- [[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012]] Central African Shear Zone (CASZ) influence.

## Formations Present

- [[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012]] Al-Gayger, Al-Renk, Galhak, Melut, Samma, Yabus, Adar, Daga

## Key Findings From This Location

- The basin is an intra-continental Cretaceous-Tertiary rift basin formed along the Central African Shear Zone (CASZ). ([[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012, p. 43]])
