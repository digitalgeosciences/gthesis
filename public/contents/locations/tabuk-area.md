# Tabuk Area

**Summary**: A study area in northwest Saudi Arabia.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-mohammedfaiz|Mohammedfaiz, 2014]] conducted studies in the Tabuk area to compare facies and mineralogy with the Al Ula area.

## Geological and Structural Setting

- [[2014-mohammedfaiz|Mohammedfaiz, 2014]] Cambro-Ordovician clastic succession

## Formations Present

- [[2014-mohammedfaiz|Mohammedfaiz, 2014]] Siq Sandstone, Quweira Sandstone, Saq Sandstone

## Key Findings From This Location

- The Quweira Sandstone in the Tabuk area shows more mature sandstones (more quartz-rich) than those in the Al Ula area. ([[2014-mohammedfaiz|Mohammedfaiz, 2014, p. 219]])
