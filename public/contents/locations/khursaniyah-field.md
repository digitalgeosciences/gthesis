# Khursaniyah Field

**Summary**: A carbonate reservoir field in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia.

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

## Key Findings From This Location

- The structure is a NE-SW, doubly-plunging near domal anticline with maximum dips of 9 to 10 degrees. ([[2011-busubait|Busubait, 2011, p. 31]])
