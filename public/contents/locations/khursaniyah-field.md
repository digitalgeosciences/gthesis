# Khursaniyah Field

**Summary**: An onshore field in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2011-busubait|Busubait, 2011]] studied the khursaniyah-field to develop a predictive conceptual model of reservoir development.

## Geological and Structural Setting

- [[2011-busubait|Busubait, 2011]] NE-SW, doubly-plunging near domal anticline

## Formations Present

- [[2011-busubait|Busubait, 2011]] Arab-A, Arab-B, Arab-C, Arab-D, Hanifa

## Key Findings From This Location

- The field was discovered by the Khursaniyah-01 well in 1956 and contains major reservoirs in Arab-A, B, C, and D members. ([[2011-busubait|Busubait, 2011, p. 16]])
