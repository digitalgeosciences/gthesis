## Key Findings From This Location

- Located approximately 70 km from Dhahran city. ([[2012-elhaj|Elhaj, 2012, p. 26]])

- The Dam Formation in Al-Lidam Area is characterized by a mixture of carbonate and siliciclastic sediments. ([[2015-ali|Ali, 2015, p. 122]])

- The study area includes 6 outcrops with a variety of sedimentary features. ([[2016-bashri|Bashri, 2016, p. 41]])

- Over 40 outcrops of the Dam Formation are well exposed in this area. ([[2016-salih|Salih, 2016, p. 23]])

- Located between 26°15’30” N and 49°31’30” E, it features well-exposed mixed carbonate and siliciclastic facies. ([[2016-septriandi|Septriandi, 2016, p. 22]])

- Samples were collected between 26°15’30” N to 49°28’30” E. ([[2022-kalam|Kalam, 2022, p. 120]])

- The Lidam area rocks are stratigraphically equivalent to the Miocene Dam Formation. ([[2023-dafaalla|Dafaalla, 2023, p. 63]])
