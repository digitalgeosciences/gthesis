## Key Findings From This Location

- The study area includes several key aquifers and is located along the Arabian Gulf coast. ([[2000-mohammad|Mohammad, 2000, p. 18]])

- The average concentration of particulate matter in Dammam was 126.69 µg/m3. ([[2016-lawal-2|Lawal, 2016, p. 52]])

- Average annual temperature rose by 1°C and wind speed dropped by 0.6 m/s over a decade. ([[2022-mustapha|Mustapha, 2022, p. 43]])

- The results showed that the approach was able to delineate the major geological structures of the area, including faults, fractures, and lithological boundaries. ([[2023-edigbue|Edigbue, 2023, p. 17]])

- The study area was located 30 km east of Dhahran city. ([[2023-alsahwan|AlSahwan, 2023, p. 37]])
