## Key Findings From This Location

- Several major phosphorite deposits were identified in this region since 1965. ([[1996-matter|Matter, 1996, p. 27]])

- Well locations are at the NE flank of the NW-SE oriented Al Jauf graben. ([[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012, p. 20]])

- Not stated in source ([[2013-mark|Mark, 2013]])

- Identified distinct sedimentary characteristics between Al Ula and Tabuk areas, including different facies associations. ([[2014-mohammedfaiz|Mohammedfaiz, 2014, p. 232]])

- The Midyan Peninsula area shows complex syn-rift sequences including siliciclastics, carbonates, and evaporites. ([[2015-koeshidayatullah|Koeshidayatullah, 2015, p. 40]])

- The study area includes several well-exposed outcrops of the Nutaysh member. ([[2018-butt|Butt, 2018, p. 6]])
