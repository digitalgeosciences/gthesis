## Key Findings From This Location

- The dome is characterized by fractured carbonates, joints, and a history of dissolution. ([[2018-hanif|Hanif, 2018, p. 17]])

- The case study in the Dammam Dome demonstrated the effectiveness of the combined optimization approach in accurately characterizing the subsurface geology. ([[2023-edigbue|Edigbue, 2023, p. 17]])

- The dome covers an area of about 500 km2. ([[2023-alshaibani|AlShaibani, 2023, p. 16]])

- The Khobar aquifer is absent at the top of the Dammam Dome where the member is eroded. ([[1992-hassan|Hassan, 1992, p. 43]])
