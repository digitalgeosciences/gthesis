## Key Findings From This Location

- The dome is characterized by fractured carbonates with joints, karst features, and small-scale reverse faults. ([[2018-hanif|Hanif, 2018, p. 17]])

- The inversion results provided valuable information regarding the location, depth, and extent of subsurface structures within the dome. ([[2023-edigbue|Edigbue, 2023, p. 117]])

- The dome covers an area of about 500 km2 and has an oval-shaped structure. ([[2023-alshaibani|AlShaibani, 2023, p. 16]])
