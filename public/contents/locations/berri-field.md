**Summary**: A mature oil field in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia.

## Geological and Structural Setting

- [[2001-al-khalifa|Al-Khalifa, 2001]] Located on a ramp margin between the Rimthan platform and Arabian Basin.

## Formations Present

- [[2001-al-khalifa|Al-Khalifa, 2001]] Hanifa, Arab A, Arab B, Arab C, Arab D, Fadhli, Hadriya

## Key Findings From This Location

- The Berri field is one of the largest fields in the world and contains several major reservoirs including Arab, Hanifa, and Fadhli. ([[2001-al-khalifa|Al-Khalifa, 2001, p. 39]])
