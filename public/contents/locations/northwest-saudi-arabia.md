## Key Findings From This Location

- conducted research at Northwest Saudi Arabia ([[1996-jarad|Jarad, 1996]])

- The region contains several major phosphorite areas including Thaniyat Turayf and Al Jalamid. ([[1996-matter|Matter, 1996, p. 29]])

- Located at the NE flank of the NW-SE oriented Al Juf Graben. ([[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012, p. 20]])

- The Qalibah formation is a significant source and reservoir rock in this region. ([[2013-mark|Mark, 2013, p. 44]])

- The Siq Sandstone is found to unconformably overlie Precambrian rocks. ([[2014-mohammedfaiz|Mohammedfaiz, 2014, p. 38]])

- The area contains three distinct types of carbonate platforms influenced by active rifting. ([[2015-koeshidayatullah|Koeshidayatullah, 2015, p. 19]])

- The area contains several sedimentary units from Cretaceous to Pleistocene. ([[2018-butt|Butt, 2018, p. 40]])

- The area contains three paleovalleys: Misha, Rahal Dhab, and Ghunaym. ([[2020-islam|Islam, 2020, p. 24]])

- The Midyan Basin contains several rift sub-basins (Sukk, Ifal, Maqna-1, and Maqna-2). ([[2023-butt|Butt, 2023, p. 69]])

- The Dhaiqa Basin located near the historic city of Al-Ula contains some of the best exposures of Ediacaran Mattar and Dhaiqa Formations. ([[2024-malik|Malik, 2024, p. 70]])

- Al-Ghurayyah is a large peralkaline deposit enriched in REE and HFSE. ([[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024, p. 26]])
