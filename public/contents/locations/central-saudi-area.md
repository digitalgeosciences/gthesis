## Key Findings From This Location

- The study area is located in a narrow belt between the Biyadh low relief area and the Aruma carbonate beds. ([[1995-magaji|Magaji, 1995, p. 43]])

- The total tonnage in the Central Hill was found to be about 3.2 million tonnes. ([[1991-makkawi|Makkawi, 1991, p. 106]])

- Data were collected from four fields spanning the area from central to eastern Saudi Arabia. ([[2011-al-masrahy|AL-MASRAHY, 2011, p. 10]])

- The outcrop area is approximately 90 km long and 2 to 3.7 km wide. ([[2013-abdalla|Abdalla, 2013, p. 27]])

- The study area includes several distinct zones: proximal, medial, and distal parts of the paleochannel. ([[2014-waseem|Waseem, 2014, p. 53]])

- The Khartam outcrop is well exposed with 1.5 km width and 25 km length. ([[2014-mohammed|Mohammed, 2014, p. 23]])

- The study area is located in Al-Tarafiyah town in Buraydah quadrangle, Qasim area, central Saudi Arabia. ([[2014-sami|Sami, 2014, p. 27]])

- Not stated in source ([[2015-own|Own, 2015]])

- The study area is located in Wadi Nisah graben nearly 90 kilometers south of Riyadh. ([[2015-abdlmutalib|Abdlmutalib, 2015, p. 18]])

- The outcrop provides a very good cross section of the Upper Ulayyah member. ([[2015-elzain|Elzain, 2015, p. 20]])

- The study area is a small field at the SE corner of the giant Ghawar field. ([[2016-hassan|Hassan, 2016, p. 34]])

- The outcrop at Rawd Al-Jawa paleovalley provides a representative sample of the lithofacies and geomechanical properties of the Sarah Formation. ([[2016-babker|Babker, 2016, p. 124]])

- The area contains a significant portion of the Tuwaiq Mountain Formation with distinct sedimentary cycles. ([[2017-elyamani|ElYamani, 2017, p. 23]])

- Local samples were confirmed to be 98% calcite with low porosity. ([[2017-ahmed-2|AHMED, 2017, p. 40]])

- The Upper Khartam Member in central Saudi Arabia possibly represents an excellent analog for the Khuff reservoirs in Eastern Arabia. ([[2017-adam-2|Adam, 2017, p. 32]])

- Outcrops are located southwest of Riyadh city near Khashm Mi’dad area. ([[2017-yousif|Yousif, 2017, p. 28]])

- Outcrops are well-exposed about 60 kilometers west of Riyadh. ([[2018-ismanto|Ismanto, 2018, p. 16]])

- The study area includes a 3D seismic volume covering approximately 3400 km2. ([[2019-al-dawood|Al-Dawood, 2019, p. 40]])

- Groundwater in this area is characterized by high TDS and hardness, with 100% of samples being unsuitable for drinking due to these factors. ([[2019-ahmed-2|Ahmed, 2019, p. 41]])

- Outcrops of the D5-D6 members were investigated in the Darma Quadrangle, approximately 50 km west of Riyadh City. ([[2020-al-zoukani|Al-Zoukani, 2020, p. 28]])

- The area contains several proven petroleum systems, including a Paleozoic system. ([[2020-almumtin|Almumtin, 2020, p. 23]])

- The study area is known to have various marine macro and micro endemic species, including foraminifera. ([[2022-khamsin|Khamsin, 2022, p. 188]])

- Outcrops occur along a North-South trending belt about 50 km wide and extending over 1200 km. ([[2022-salih|Salih, 2022, p. 46]])

- The study area covers approximately 350,000 km^2 across three provinces (Riyadh, Qasim, and Eastern Province). ([[2022-pambudi|Pambudi, 2022, p. 19]])

- Outcrops are distributed across six quadrangles along a N-S trending belt. ([[2022-babker|Babker, 2022, p. 61]])

- The carbonate rocks were deposited in shallow marine carbonate ramps. ([[2024-allam|Allam, 2024, p. 72]])

- The Qalibah Formation is extensively exposed in this region, providing an opportunity to test 3D modeling. ([[2024-abdrabo|Abdrabo, 2024, p. 19]])

- The study area covers an extensive range from non-marine clastic to deep marine carbonate environments. ([[2024-fahmi|Fahmi, 2024, p. 25]])

- The study was conducted at an outcrop northwest of Riyadh city. ([[2024-allehyani|allehyani, 2024, p. 34]])

- The study area is located mainly along Highway 5395 near Haniya Village (24°10.779'N, 46°26.853'E). ([[2024-jalloh|Jalloh, 2024, p. 30]])

- The type section of the Hanifa Formation is defined at Jabal Al-Abakkayn, near Riyadh City. ([[2025-refaat|Refaat, 2025, p. 32]])
