## Key Findings From This Location

- The field produces 52 degree API crude oil. ([[1999-iddris|Iddris, 1999, p. 19]])

- The outcrop provides an excellent analog for the upper parts of the Khuff-A reservoirs. ([[2014-sami|Sami, 2014, p. 75]])

- Not stated in source ([[2015-abdlmutalib|Abdlmutalib, 2015]])

- The Sarah Formation is well exposed in the Al-Qaseem area, providing a suitable outcrop analogue for subsurface evaluation. ([[2016-babker|Babker, 2016, p. 18]])

- The outcrop is situated approximately 35km from the main Riyadh-Makkah Highway. ([[2016-malik|Malik, 2016, p. 24]])

- The study area includes a 1200km long belt of outcrops extending N-S. ([[2016-makkawi|Makkawi, 2016, p. 22]])

- Outcrops provide a continuous stratigraphic context for the Khuff formation. ([[2017-adam-2|Adam, 2017, p. 77]])

- Outcrop sections were located near Khashm Mi'dad area, southwest of Riyadh city. ([[2017-yousif|Yousif, 2017, p. 158]])

- Outcrops are well-exposed about 60 kilometers west of Riyadh. ([[2018-ismanto|Ismanto, 2018, p. 57]])

- Outcrops of the Dhruma Formation are exposed approximately 50 km west of Riyadh City. ([[2020-al-zoukani|Al-Zoukani, 2020, p. 1]])

- Not stated in source ([[2021-al-nuwais|Al-Nuwais, 2021]])

- Samples were collected from three outcrops near the reference section at Khashm ad Dhibi. ([[2022-khamsin|Khamsin, 2022, p. 23]])

- The Hanifa Formation is exposed along a 700 km belt in Central Saudi Arabia. ([[2022-syahputra|Syahputra, 2022, p. 30]])

- Study area includes a 535 km-long N-S transect of the Hanifa Formation. ([[2022-bashri|Bashri, 2022, p. 19]])

- The study area covers a 1000 km belt of the Khuff Formation. ([[2022-babker|Babker, 2022, p. 14]])

- The study area provides a well-exposed outcrop for analyzing burrow and fracture characteristics. ([[2024-saraih|Saraih, 2024, p. 78]])

- The Dhruma Formation provides essential data for regional biostratigraphic correlation and hydrocarbon exploration. ([[2024-jalloh|Jalloh, 2024, p. 112]])
