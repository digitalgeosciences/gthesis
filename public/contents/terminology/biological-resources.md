# Biological Resources

**Summary**: Natural organisms like mangroves, seagrasses, and corals that are sensitive to oil.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016]] mapped biological resources to identify habitats requiring high protection during spills.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016]] Determines the ecological impact of oil spills on local biodiversity.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016]] Mapped as polygons and categorized by sensitivity.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Mangroves and salt-marshes were identified as the most impacted habitats if oil reaches the shoreline. ([[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016, p. 79]])
