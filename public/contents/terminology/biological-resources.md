# Biological Resources

**Summary**: Living organisms such as seagrasses, corals, and mangroves that are impacted by oil.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016]] mapped biological resources to identify sensitive marine habitats.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016]] Identifies areas where biological diversity is at risk from pollution.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016]] Mapped based on presence of seagrass, coral communities, and mangrove forests.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Mangrove is the most impacted habitat if the oil spill reaches the shoreline along with salt-marshes. ([[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016, p. 79]])
