# S2

**Summary**: A measure of the volume of hydrocarbons that formed during thermal pyrolysis.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]] measured S2 to assess potential hydrocarbon content.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]] S2 represents the hydrocarbons generated during the thermal cracking of kerogen.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]] measured via pyrolysis between 300 and 600°C

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Un-preserved samples showed significant drops in S2 compared to preserved samples from the same interval. ([[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023, p. 17]])
- [[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]] Significant drops observed in un-prepared samples.
