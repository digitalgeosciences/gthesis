# Invasion Depth

**Summary**: The distance into the formation that drilling fluids and solids penetrate.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2001-mohammed|Mohammed, 2001]] measured invasion depth to determine how effectively bridging additives blocked the entry of larger particles.

## Scientific Context

- [[2001-mohammed|Mohammed, 2001]] Used to evaluate the effectiveness of bridging agents in preventing deep invasion.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2001-mohammed|Mohammed, 2001]] determined from ultrasonic velocity profiles.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Invasion depth decreased by 1.35 cm as median particle size increased from 8 to 41 μm. ([[2001-mohammed|Mohammed, 2001, p. 86]])
