# Condensate Blockage

**Summary**: The reduction in gas permeability near the wellbore due to the formation of a liquid condensate phase.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-baarimah|BAARIMAH, 2012]] analyzed condensate blockage as a primary factor reducing productivity in retrograde systems.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Horizontal wells reduce the effects of condensate banking by providing more reservoir contact and lower drawdown. ([[2012-baarimah|BAARIMAH, 2012, p. 24]])


- Condensate blockage can reduce effective gas permeability by up to 80% in carbonate reservoirs. ([[2020-mohammed|Mohammed, 2020, p. 27]])

- Well productivity is reduced as the critical condensate saturation increases. ([[1999-fawumi|Fawumi, 1999, p. 18]])

- Hydraulic fracturing was shown to be effective in reducing the effect of condensate blockage on the productivity index (PI). ([[2000-hashmi|Hashmi, 2000, p. 22]])
