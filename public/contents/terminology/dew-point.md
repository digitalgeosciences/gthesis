# Dew Point

**Summary**: The pressure at which a gas mixture containing heavy hydrocarbons begins to form liquid.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2010-al-dhamen|Al-Dhamen, 2010]] investigated the prediction of dew-point pressure for gas condensate reservoirs.

## Scientific Context

- [[2010-al-dhamen|Al-Dhamen, 2010]] Critical for fluid characterization and determining if condensate banking is causing production decline.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2010-al-dhamen|Al-Dhamen, 2010]] Experimental determination via Constant Mass Expansion (CME) or Constant Volume Depletion (CVD).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Dew-point occurs when a gas mixture is depressurized until liquid is formed, resulting in significant production decline due to condensate banking. ([[2010-al-dhamen|Al-Dhamen, 2010, p. 18]])
