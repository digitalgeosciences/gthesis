# Interporosity Flow Coefficient

**Summary**: A parameter describing the ability of fluid to flow between the matrix and the fractures.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-al-abdulmohsin|Al-Abdulmohsin, 2012]] used interporosity flow coefficient (ג) to model the transition period in dual-porosity systems.

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-al-abdulmohsin|Al-Abdulmohsin, 2012]] Determines the time it takes for the matrix to start contributing to production.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2012-al-abdulmohsin|Al-Abdulmohsin, 2012]] Calculated based on matrix/fracture permeability and wellbore radius.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The value of γ determines the duration and shape of the transition period during well testing. ([[2012-al-abdulmohsin|Al-Abdulmohsin, 2012, p. 31]])
