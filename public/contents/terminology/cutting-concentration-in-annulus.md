# Cutting Concentration In Annulus

**Summary**: The percentage of cuttings volume in the annulus during drilling.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017]] monitored cutting concentration in annulus (CCA) to determine the maximum allowable ROP.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017]] Used to determine the maximum drilling rate that can be sustained without accumulation.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017]] Calculated using Newitt's or API methods.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- A CCA limit of 0.05 is established; exceeding this leads to potential hole problems like stuck pipe. ([[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017, p. 124]])
