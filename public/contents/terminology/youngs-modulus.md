# Youngs Modulus

**Summary**: A measure of the stiffness of a material.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2021-albensaad|Albensaad, 2021]] measured Young's modulus via impulse hammer and calculated from log data.

- [[2023-al-garni|Al-Garni, 2023]] measured Young's modulus to determine the stability and closure of wormhole channels.

- [[2023-aldhafeeri|Aldhafeeri, 2023]] measured Young's modulus to assess rock degradation.

- [[2002-elhassan|ElHassan, 2002]] correlated quartz percentage and grain contact with Young's modulus.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Static Young’s modulus resolved smaller variations within the mudrock layers compared to dynamic values. ([[2021-albensaad|Albensaad, 2021, p. 76]])

- Acidized samples with wormholes showed a higher rate of change in YM compared to intact samples. ([[2023-al-garni|Al-Garni, 2023, p. 60]])

- Young's modulus was reduced by nearly half in tight limestone after acid flood. ([[2023-aldhafeeri|Aldhafeeri, 2023, p. 39]])

- Correlation between quartz % and Young's modulus was 0.24 (1000 psi) and 0.42 (2900 psi). ([[2002-elhassan|ElHassan, 2002, p. 76]])
