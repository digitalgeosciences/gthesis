# Wellbore Hydraulics

**Summary**: The study of fluid flow and pressure distribution within the wellbore.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 2014]] included the impact of wellbore hydraulics in the solution to account for frictional losses.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 2014]] Essential for long horizontal wells where friction can significantly impact productivity.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 2014]] Modeled using finite conductivity equations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Wellbore hydraulics change the flux distribution (inflow profile) along the wellbore, resulting in additional pressure drop in the reservoir. ([[2014-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 2014, p. 35]])
