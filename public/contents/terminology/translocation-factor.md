# Translocation Factor

**Summary**: The ratio of the concentration of a substance in the plant's aerial parts to its concentration in the roots.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-alamran|AlAmran, 2019]] calculated TF to determine the movement of contaminants from roots to leaves/bark.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-alamran|AlAmran, 2019]] Used to understand the internal movement of contaminants within the plant.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-alamran|AlAmran, 2019]] Ratio calculation between leaf/bark and root concentrations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The maximum translocation factor (TF) was 7.15 for leaf tissue. ([[2019-alamran|AlAmran, 2019, p. 16]])
