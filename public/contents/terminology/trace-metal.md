# Trace Metal

**Summary**: Elements occurring in small amounts in soil and rock, often with significant biological impact.

---

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Cd, Cr, Cu, and Zn exceeded allowable limits in industrial/residential areas. ([[2014-adedapo|Adedapo, 2014, p. 12]])


- Average concentrations were V(1.46ppm), Cr (1.59ppm), Co(0.37ppm), Ni(2.48ppm), Cu(0.8lppm), Zn(2.56ppm), Mo(0.28ppm), Ba(2.67ppm) and Pb(0.21ppm). ([[2015-jawad|Jawad, 2015, p. 14]])

- Trace metals like lead and cadmium were found within the regulatory limits of 0.5 and 0.0025 µg/m3 respectively. ([[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019, p. 168]])
