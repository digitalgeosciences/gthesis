# Mmp

**Summary**: The lowest pressure at which a gas and oil become miscible in a reservoir.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-al-hakami|Al-Hakami, 2023]] focused on reducing the MMP to improve CO2 flooding for heavy crude oils.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-al-hakami|Al-Hakami, 2023]] Lowering MMP allows for higher recovery without fracturing the formation.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-al-hakami|Al-Hakami, 2023]] Determined via STE, RBA, and VIT techniques.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Ionic liquids were found to be the optimum chemicals to reduce both MMP and FCMP values. ([[2023-al-hakami|Al-Hakami, 2023, p. 20]])
