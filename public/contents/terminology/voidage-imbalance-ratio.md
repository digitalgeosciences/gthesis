# Voidage Imbalance Ratio

**Summary**: A metric used to quantify the imbalance in fluid withdrawal and injection, often used as a proxy for environmental or reservoir integrity risks.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-ahmed-2|Ahmed, 2014]] used VIR as an environmental objective to be minimized during optimization.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-ahmed-2|Ahmed, 2014]] Used to ensure environmental compliance and reservoir stability.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-ahmed-2|Ahmed, 2014]] Calculated based on production and injection rates.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The Pareto-based approach successfully reduced the VIR compared to the weighted sum technique in all scenarios. ([[2014-ahmed-2|Ahmed, 2014, p. 86]])
- [[2014-ahmed-2|Ahmed, 2014]] Values ranged from 0.3379 (Base Case) down to 0.0157 in some scenarios.
