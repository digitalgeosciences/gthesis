# Viscosity Ratio

**Summary**: The ratio of the viscosity of the displacing fluid to the viscosity of the displaced fluid.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1990-al-mumen|Al-Mumen, 1990]] evaluated the impact of the viscosity ratio on oil mobility.

## Scientific Context

- [[1990-al-mumen|Al-Mumen, 1990]] Viscosity ratio determines how easily a displacement fluid can move oil out of pores.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1990-al-mumen|Al-Mumen, 1990]] Calculated from measured viscosities of oil and water.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Decreasing viscosity ratios with higher salinity improved the relative mobility of the oil phase. ([[1990-al-mumen|Al-Mumen, 1990, p. 75]])
