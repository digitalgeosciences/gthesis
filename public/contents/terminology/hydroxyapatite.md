# Hydroxyapatite

**Summary**: A mineral form of calcium phosphate that is significantly stiffer than calcite.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-desouky|Desouky, 2023]] identified hydroxyapatite (HAP) as the primary product of the DAP-calcite reaction.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-desouky|Desouky, 2023]] HAP is used as a reinforcing phase to improve the mechanical properties of carbonate rocks.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-desouky|Desouky, 2023]] Identified via XRD and SEM.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The formation of HAP resulted in a significant increase in the stiffness of the rock. ([[2023-desouky|Desouky, 2023, p. 23]])
