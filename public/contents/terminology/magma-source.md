# Magma Source

**Summary**: The determination of the origin of a magma.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] investigated the magma source to distinguish between crustal melting and mantle contribution.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] Determines if the granite is a product of primary mantle melting or secondary crustal melting.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] Analyzed via Nd isotopes and trace element ratios (Y/Nb, Nb/Ta).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Evidence suggests melts were derived from a more reduced mantle environment with little crustal contamination. ([[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024, p. 77]])
