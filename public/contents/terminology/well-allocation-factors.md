# Well Allocation Factors

**Summary**: A measure of the amount of injected fluid that reaches a specific producer.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2010-mohammed|Mohammed, 2010]] used well allocation factors to quantify the distribution of fluids between injector and producer pairs.

## Scientific Context

- [[2010-mohammed|Mohammed, 2010]] Used in the Thiele and Batycky method for optimization.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2010-mohammed|Mohammed, 2010]] Calculated using streamline simulation data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- WAFs allow for calculating the efficiency of injection wells as the ratio of injected water to oil produced at offset wells. ([[2010-mohammed|Mohammed, 2010, p. 17]])
