# Real Conductivity

**Summary**: The real part of complex conductivity, representing ohmic losses in a material.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-badhafere|Badhafere, 2023]] used real conductivity to detect small concentrations of calcite (0.1 wt.%) and track the settlement of barite/calcite.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-badhafere|Badhafere, 2023]] Used to differentiate between mineral types and concentrations in drilling fluids.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-badhafere|Badhafere, 2023]] Measured via SIP at various frequencies.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Real conductivity showed a minimum difference of 24.6% between control and 20% concentration samples, providing high sensitivity. ([[2023-badhafere|Badhafere, 2023, p. 80]])
