# Heterogeneity

**Summary**: The variation in physical properties or characteristics of a reservoir.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The Sarah Formation is considered to represent heterogeneous to very heterogeneous reservoir. ([[2014-el-deek|El-Deek, 2014, p. 16]])

- Heterogeneity was quantified using a specific key based on measurements from three distinct reservoir units (A, B, and C). ([[2014-sami|Sami, 2014, p. 138]])

- Porosity and permeability patterns are highly variable, indicating both depositional and diagenetic controls. ([[2015-ali|Ali, 2015, p. 25]])

- Heterogeneity is both depositional and diagenetic, occurring at scales from cm to km. ([[2017-mohammed-2|Mohammed, 2017, p. 20]])

- Heterogeneity is controlled by depositional and post-depositional processes. ([[2017-adam|Adam, 2017, p. 78]])
