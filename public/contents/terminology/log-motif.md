# Log Motif

**Summary**: The shape of a well log curve (e.g., bell, boxcar) reflecting the sediment's grain size.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-awad|Awad, 2016]] used log-motif analysis to identify sedimentary environments.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-awad|Awad, 2016]] Log motifs are used to identify sedimentary environments in the absence of core data.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-awad|Awad, 2016]] Analysis of Gamma Ray (GR) curves.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Four common log motifs: funnel-shaped, bell-shaped, boxcar-shaped, and bow-shaped. ([[2016-awad|Awad, 2016, p. 59]])
