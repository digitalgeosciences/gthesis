# Chlorophyll A

**Summary**: A pigment used by algae and other photosynthetic organisms for photosynthesis.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-yusuf|Yusuf, 2015]] measured chlorophyll-a to assess eutrophication levels.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-yusuf|Yusuf, 2015]] High levels indicate poor water quality and potential harmful algal blooms.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-yusuf|Yusuf, 2015]] Measured via fluorescence detection on a Turner Designs Trilogy fluorometer.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Chlorophyll a concentrations were significantly high only in Dammam at levels indicative of poor water quality. ([[2015-yusuf|Yusuf, 2015, p. 14]])
