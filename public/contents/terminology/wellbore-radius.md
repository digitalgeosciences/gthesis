# Wellbore Radius

**Summary**: The internal radius of the wellbore, which affects flow capacity.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] calculated changes in wellbore radius due to erosion.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] A larger radius reduces pressure drop and increases flow capacity.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] Calculated by subtracting eroded volume from initial volume.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Wellbore radius increases significantly near the heel where shear stress is highest. ([[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019, p. 64]])
- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] Initial: 7 inches; change is significant at the heel.
