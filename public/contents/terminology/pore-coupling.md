**Summary**: The effect where small and large pores are connected, complicating the interpretation of petrophysical properties.

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-al-garadi|AL-GARADI, 2019]] Pore coupling in carbonates can mask the distinction between different pore sizes or fluid types.
