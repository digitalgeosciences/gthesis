# Tuning

**Summary**: The interference of seismic wavelets due to thin bed geometry.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] analyzed tuning effects on amplitude and thickness interpretation.

## Scientific Context

- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] Critical for distinguishing lithology-driven changes from geometry-induced artifacts.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] evaluated using a wedge model and synthetic trace analysis.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Tuning was identified as a primary cause for amplitude anomalies in beds with thicknesses between 18ms and 33ms. ([[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997, p. 86]])
