# Tuning

**Summary**: The interference of seismic waves due to thin bed geometry.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] analyzed tuning effects on amplitude and thickness.

## Scientific Context

- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] Critical for distinguishing lithology changes from geometric effects.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] Evaluated using wedge models and isochron vs. amplitude crossplots.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- High amplitudes at certain thicknesses (e.g., 23 msec) were identified as being caused by tuning rather than lithology. ([[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997, p. 82]])
