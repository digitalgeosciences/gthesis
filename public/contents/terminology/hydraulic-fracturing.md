# Hydraulic Fracturing

**Summary**: The process of injecting high-pressure fluids to create fractures in rock.

---

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Hydraulic fracturing is a key technology for developing unconventional reservoirs like the Qusaiba Member. ([[2015-own|Own, 2015, p. 7]])

- Accurate estimation of these properties prevents 'hit or miss' fractures, making fracking more cost-effective. ([[2016-lawal|Lawal, 2016, p. 19]])

- Microseismic data was used to estimate fracture geometry and impact on drainage. ([[2017-mohammed|Mohammed, 2017, p. 210]])
