# Reservoir Quality Index

**Summary**: A petrophysical index used to characterize the quality of a reservoir.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-sami|Sami, 2014]] calculated RQI to evaluate the heterogeneity and flow units of the reservoir.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-sami|Sami, 2014]] Used to identify and categorize flow units within a reservoir.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-sami|Sami, 2014]] Calculated from core or log data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The correlation between RQI and porosity (Phi-z) was low for all units, suggesting no distinct flow units could be defined easily. ([[2014-sami|Sami, 2014, p. 159]])

## Contrasting Values and Interpretations

- [[2014-sami|Sami, 2014]] R2 values for RQI vs Phi-z were 0.177, 0.162, and 0.004 for units A, B, and C respectively.
