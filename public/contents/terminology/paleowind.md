# Paleowind

**Summary**: The historical direction of prevailing winds in a geological setting.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2011-al-masrahy|AL-MASRAHY, 2011]] investigated paleowind directions to understand dune distribution and shape.

## Scientific Context

- [[2011-al-masrahy|AL-MASRAHY, 2011]] Determining paleowind helps in understanding reservoir heterogeneity and dune morphology.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

## Key Findings Across Sources
