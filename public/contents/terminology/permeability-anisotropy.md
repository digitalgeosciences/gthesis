# Permeability Anisotropy

**Summary**: The variation of permeability in different directions within a rock.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Optimum permeability is expected to be perpendicular to the paleowind direction (east-northeast). ([[2011-al-masrahy|AL-MASRAHY, 2011, p. 125]])

- The hydraulic conductivity on wells (RAH-09 and RAH-22) is low, while the hydraulic conductivity values on wells (366 and 316) are high. ([[2020-mansouri|Mansouri, 2020, p. 95]])

- The variance map for F7 showed the most anisotropic fabric due to vertical sand-filled burrows. ([[2022-abdlmutalib|Abdlmutalib, 2022, p. 84]])
