# Pore_Size_Distribution

**Summary**: The distribution of pore sizes within a rock, often categorized by T2 relaxation times in NMR.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2020-islam|Islam, 2020]] analyzed the pore size distribution to identify macropores, mesopores, and micropores.

## Scientific Context

- [[2020-islam|Islam, 2020]] Determines the connectivity and mobility of fluids in the reservoir.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2020-islam|Islam, 2020]] Derived from NMR T2 relaxation time spectra.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Macropores (D1) were identified as having T2 peak values between 112.20 and 223.87 ms. ([[2020-islam|Islam, 2020, p. 133]])
