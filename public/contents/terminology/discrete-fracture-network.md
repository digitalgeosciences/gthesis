# Discrete Fracture Network

**Summary**: A 3D model representing the geometry and distribution of fractures in a rock mass.

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012]] Used to model permeability and connectivity in fractured media.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2012-abdelgader|Abdelgader, 2012]] Stochastic modeling based on orientation, length, and intensity.

## Key Findings Across Sources
