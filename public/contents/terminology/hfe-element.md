# Hfe Element

**Summary**: High Field Strength Elements (e.g., Zr, Hf, Nb, Ta).

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] analyzed HFSE elements to characterize the mineralization of the Al-Ghurayyah pluton.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] HFSE enrichment is characteristic of A-type granites.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] Quantified via ICP-MS and XRF.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- All lithological units are high in HFSE elements, with arfvedsonite bearing granites having higher Sn + Hf and medium grained granites showing higher Hf. ([[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024, p. 66]])
