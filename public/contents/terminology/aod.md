# AOD

**Summary**: A measure of how much light is blocked by aerosol particles in the atmosphere.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019]] analyzed AOD values to track particulate matter trends over time and space.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019]] Used to quantify the concentration of airborne particles from satellite imagery.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019]] Derived from MODIS sensor data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- AOD 500 showed an increasing trend at $\tau_{550}$ in both northern and southern regions of the Arabian Peninsula. ([[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019, p. 147]])
