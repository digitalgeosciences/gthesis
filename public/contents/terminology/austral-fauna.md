# Austral Fauna

**Summary**: A specific faunal assemblage characteristic of the Southern Hemisphere during the Cretaceous.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-alhilali|Alhilali, 2024]] identified an Austral faunal province based on the presence of specific taxa like Bulbobaculites and Textulariopsis.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-alhilali|Alhilali, 2024]] Used to differentiate between regional faunal provinces (e.g., Tethyan vs. Austral).

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-alhilali|Alhilali, 2024]] Comparison of local assemblages with known global and regional standards.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Evidence for the existence of the 'Austral' faunal province was provided by several new species and the absence of Tethyan fauna. ([[2024-alhilali|Alhilali, 2024, p. 155]])
