# S1

**Summary**: A measure of free hydrocarbons in a rock sample from Rock-Eval pyrolysis.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]] measured S1 to assess the impact of preservation on available hydrocarbon data.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]] S1 is a primary indicator for determining oil-in-place and hydrocarbon mobility.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]] Rock-Eval pyrolysis analysis.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Unpreserved samples showed S1 drops of up to 87% in high maturity cores. ([[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023, p. 91]])

## Contrasting Values and Interpretations

- [[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]] Ranges from 2.3 to 4.3 mgHC/g in corrected samples.
