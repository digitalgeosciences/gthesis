# S1

**Summary**: A measure of free hydrocarbons present in a sample.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]] measured S1 to evaluate the impact of preservation on available hydrocarbons.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]] S1 represents the free hydrocarbons that have already been generated in situ.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]] measured via pyrolysis at 300°C

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Un-preserved samples showed significant drops in S1 compared to preserved samples from the same interval. ([[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023, p. 17]])
- [[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]] Significant drops observed in un-preserved samples.
