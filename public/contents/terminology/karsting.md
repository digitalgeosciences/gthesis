# Karsting

**Summary**: The chemical weathering of carbonate rock, often resulting in caves and sinkholes.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2010-al-moqbel|Al-Moqbel, 2010]] analyzed the impact of karsting on seismic continuity and velocity picking.

## Scientific Context

- [[2010-al-moqbel|Al-Moqbel, 2010]] Creates significant geological challenges for seismic imaging due to lack of continuity.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

## Key Findings Across Sources
