# Flow Reactor

**Summary**: A system where reactants are pumped through a tube to facilitate continuous chemical reactions.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-hassan|Hassan, 2015]] evaluated the impact of a flow reactor on the efficiency of both synthesis and degradation processes.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-hassan|Hassan, 2015]] used to improve reaction kinetics and scalability in chemical synthesis.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

## Key Findings Across Sources
