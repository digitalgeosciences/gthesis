# Monooxygenases

**Summary**: Enzymes that incorporate one oxygen atom from O2 into a substrate while reducing the other to water.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-almutawa|AlMutawa, 2024]] screened for monooxygenase enzymes to understand their role in the metabolic processes of hydrocarbon-degrading bacteria.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-almutawa|AlMutawa, 2024]] These enzymes are pivotal in bacterial metabolism for the detoxification of xenobiotic compounds like hydrocarbons.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-almutawa|AlMutawa, 2024]] Quantified using fluorescence assays with substrates such as benzyl phenyl sulfide.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Screening methods like fluorescence assays were used to identify and characterize these enzymes' specificities. ([[2024-almutawa|AlMutawa, 2024, p. 24]])
