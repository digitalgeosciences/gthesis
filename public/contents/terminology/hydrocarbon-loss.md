# Hydrocarbon Loss

**Summary**: The reduction of hydrocarbon content in a rock sample due to evaporation or other processes.

---

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Lighter hydrocarbons (C3-C7) are more susceptible to loss during handling and storage. ([[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023, p. 104]])
- [[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]] Up to 87% loss in S1 for unpreserved samples.
