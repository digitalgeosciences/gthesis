# Well Location Optimization

**Summary**: The process of determining the optimal spatial coordinates for production and injection wells.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-sibaweihi|Sibaweihi, 2013]] developed a well location optimization strategy that incorporated minimum inter-well distance constraints.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-sibaweihi|Sibaweihi, 2013]] Critical to avoid well interference and ensure even drainage of the reservoir.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2013-sibaweihi|Sibaweihi, 2013]] Optimized using CMA-ES and DE algorithms.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Incorporating a 1000 ft minimum distance resulted in NPVs comparable to unconstrained cases while ensuring feasible well spacing. ([[2013-sibaweihi|Sibaweihi, 2013, p. 91]])
