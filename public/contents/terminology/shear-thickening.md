# Shear Thickening

**Summary**: An increase in the viscosity of a fluid as the shear rate increases.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-dafaalla|Dafaalla, 2023]] Key mechanism for improving sweep efficiency in high-flow conditions.

- [[2023-dafaalla|Dafaalla, 2023]] Observed via rheological testing and core flooding.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Shear-thickening is prominent in extensional or elongational flow regimes in porous media. ([[2023-dafaalla|Dafaalla, 2023, p. 34]])
- [[2023-dafaalla|Dafaalla, 2023]] Occurs at higher flow rates.
