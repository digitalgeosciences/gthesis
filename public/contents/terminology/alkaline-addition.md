# Alkaline Addition

**Summary**: The addition of alkaline agents like sodium carbonate to the surfactant slug.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-geri|Geri, 2013]] evaluated the effect of alkali on reducing surfactant adsorption and improving mobility.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-geri|Geri, 2013]] Alkali helps in creating soap and reducing surfactant loss to rock surfaces.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2013-geri|Geri, 2013]] compared results with and without alkali addition.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The addition of alkali assists in achieving low IFT conditions without adding extra surfactant and reduces surfactant adsorption. ([[2013-geri|Geri, 2013, p. 28]])
