# Critical Rate

**Summary**: The maximum production rate at which a stable water cone is maintained; exceeding this leads to immediate water breakthrough.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1994-hakim|Hakim, 1994]] evaluated the critical rate for horizontal wells under various conditions.

## Scientific Context

- [[1994-hakim|Hakim, 1994]] Crucial for determining the economic life and management of wells in aquifers.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1994-hakim|Hakim, 1994]] Determined via simulation by identifying the point where the saturation function exceeds a threshold.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Critical rate is directly proportional to k_x and inversely proportional to sqrt(k_y). ([[1994-hakim|Hakim, 1994, p. 60]])
