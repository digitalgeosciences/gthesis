# Drilling Fluids

**Summary**: Suspensions of solids and dissolved materials in a water, oil, or synthetic base used in drilling operations.

---

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Drilling fluids are formulated for each well to meet specific physical and chemical requirements. The primary functions include transporting cuttings, controlling pressure, and lubricating the drill string. ([[2012-attyah|Attyah, 2012, p. 15]])


- Formulations were tested for rheology and HP/HT stability. ([[2014-elhag|ELHAG, 2014]])

- The use of date seeds, grass, and grass ash is suitable for the formulation of a low-cost, environment-friendly and sustainable drilling fluid system. ([[2014-wajheeuddin|Wajheeuddin, 2014, p. 18]])
