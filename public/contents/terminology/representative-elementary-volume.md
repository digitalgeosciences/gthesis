# Representative Elementary Volume

**Summary**: The smallest volume of a material that is large enough to contain a representative sample of its properties.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-allehyani|allehyani, 2024]] determined the REV for different bioturbation styles to ensure accurate petrophysical measurements.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-allehyani|allehyani, 2024]] Crucial for determining if laboratory samples are representative of the reservoir.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-allehyani|allehyani, 2024]] Determined by analyzing Vp variance across different core diameters.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The REV for BN samples was found to be larger than standard 1.5-inch cores, while BR samples had smaller REVs. ([[2024-allehyani|allehyani, 2024, p. 64]])
