# Hydrophile Lipophile Balance

**Summary**: A measure of the balance between hydrophilic and lipophilic parts of a surfactant molecule.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-alghamdi|AlGhamdi, 2019]] used the HLB concept to classify surfactants and predict their behavior in oil-water systems.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-alghamdi|AlGhamdi, 2019]] HLB is used to predict whether a surfactant will form an oil-in-water or water-in-oil emulsion.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-alghamdi|AlGhamdi, 2019]] Determined by experimental data or calculated via chemical formulas.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The HLB number could not predict how efficiently the surfactant would perform the expected action. ([[2019-alghamdi|AlGhamdi, 2019, p. 27]])
