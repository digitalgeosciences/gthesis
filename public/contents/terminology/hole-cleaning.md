# Hole Cleaning

**Summary**: The process of removing rock fragments (cuttings) from the wellbore during drilling.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2021-al-malki|Al-Malki, 2021]] evaluated hole cleaning efficiency in deviated and extended reach wells

## Scientific Context

- [[2021-al-malki|Al-Malki, 2021]] Critical for maintaining wellbore integrity and ensuring safe, efficient drilling in complex geometries.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2021-al-malki|Al-Malki, 2021]] Calculated via HCF (Hole Cleaning Factor) and CCI (Cuttings Carrying Index).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Inefficient hole cleaning may lead to high nonproductive time (NPT) due to lost circulation, excessive overpull while tripping, slow rate of penetration (ROP), excessive bit wear and stuck pipe. ([[2021-al-malki|Al-Malki, 2021, p. 13]])
