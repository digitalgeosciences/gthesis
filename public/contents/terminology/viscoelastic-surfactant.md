# Viscoelastic Surfactant

**Summary**: A surfactant that forms wormlike micelles to provide high viscosity in fluids.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-alao|Alao, 2014]] studied the properties of viscoelastic surfactants (VES) for use as acid diversion agents.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-alao|Alao, 2014]] VES is used as a diversion agent because it can be tailored for specific temperature and salinity conditions.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-alao|Alao, 2014]] Characterized via rheometry, TGA, and NMR.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Modified VES formulations with nanoparticles showed improved thermal stability and rheological performance. ([[2014-alao|Alao, 2014, p. 18]])
