# Chemisorption

**Summary**: Adsorption where gas molecules are chemically bonded to a surface.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-elnaeem|Elnaeem, 2016]] identified chemisorption on SH2 shale based on endothermic enthalpy values.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-elnaeem|Elnaeem, 2016]] Used to describe the adsorption behavior on SH2 shale where enthalpy is positive.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-elnaeem|Elnaeem, 2016]] Analysis of ΔHads values from isotherm fitting.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- High positive ΔHads values (e.g., 147.931 kJ/mol) for SH2 indicated the endothermic nature of chemisorption. ([[2016-elnaeem|Elnaeem, 2016, p. 96]])
