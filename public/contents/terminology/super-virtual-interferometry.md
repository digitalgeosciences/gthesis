# Super Virtual Interferometry

**Summary**: A technique to enhance the signal-to-noise ratio of seismic data.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-alshaibani|AlShaibani, 2023]] applied SVI to improve the quality of first arrival picks in the seismic survey.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-alshaibani|AlShaibani, 2023]] Used to overcome low SNR in seismic data for better traveltime tomography results.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-alshaibani|AlShaibani, 2023]] Convolved recorded data with virtual data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- SVI enhances SNR by a factor of sqrt(N). ([[2023-alshaibani|AlShaibani, 2023, p. 30]])
