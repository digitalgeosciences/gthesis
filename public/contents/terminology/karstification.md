# Karstification

**Summary**: The dissolution of carbonate rocks by water, creating secondary porosity.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-iwalewa|Iwalewa, 2012]] characterized the UER aquifer as a karstified fractured bedrock aquifer.

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-iwalewa|Iwalewa, 2012]] Karstification is the primary mechanism for secondary porosity in the UER aquifer.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2012-iwalewa|Iwalewa, 2012]] identified through geological mapping and well logs.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Karstification in vertical and horizontal directions is unevenly distributed, leading to large heterogeneities in permeability and storativity. ([[2012-iwalewa|Iwalewa, 2012, p. 46]])
