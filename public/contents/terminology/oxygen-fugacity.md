# Oxygen Fugacity

**Summary**: A measure of the chemical potential of oxygen in a system.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023]] calculated oxygen fugacity to determine the oxidation state of the mantle.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023]] Used to determine if the mantle was oxidized or reduced.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023]] Calculated using the oxygen barometer of Ballhaus et al. (1990).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Oxygen fugacity values ranged from -1.89 to -0.22, indicating a reduced environment. ([[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023, p. 90]])
