# Oxygen Fugacity

**Summary**: A measure of the chemical potential of oxygen in a system.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023]] calculated oxygen fugacity to determine the oxidation state of the mantle source.

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] determined oxygen fugacity to assess the oxidation state of the magmatic systems.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023]] Indicates the redox state of the mantle source during melting.

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] determined oxygen fugacity to assess the oxidation state of the magmatic systems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023]] Calculated based on olivine-orthopyroxene-spinel compositions.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Oxygen fugacity values are -1.89 to -0.22 (relative to FMQ), suggesting reduced conditions. ([[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023, p. 22]])

- Average log fO2 values ranged from -11.4 to -13.2, with arfvedsonite bearing granites being more oxidized. ([[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024, p. 74]])
