# Formation Damage

**Summary**: The reduction in permeability or injectivity of a formation.

---

## Key Findings Across Sources

- AlF3 precipitation is a major cause of damage in acid treatments. ([[2012-al-harbi|Al-Harbi, 2012, p. 45]])


- Damage was identified as being caused by fines migration and precipitation of reaction products in sandstone, and VES adsorption on carbonate. ([[2016-jad|Jad, 2016, p. 15]])

- Solids from conventional drill-in fluids were identified as a primary cause of permeability reduction. ([[2017-mohamed|MOHAMED, 2017, p. 19]])

- Iron sulfide minerals with sizes up to 296 μm can block pore throats as small as 19 μm. ([[2021-gasimli|Gasimli, 2021, p. 54]])
