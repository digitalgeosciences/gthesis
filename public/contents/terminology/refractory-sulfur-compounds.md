# Refractory Sulfur Compounds

**Summary**: Sulfur compounds that are difficult to remove by standard hydrodesulfurization processes.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-danmaliki|Danmaliki, 2015]] evaluated the removal of refractory sulfur compounds (T, BT, DBT) from model fuel samples.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-danmaliki|Danmaliki, 2015]] These are the primary targets in desulfurization studies due to their persistence in fuel.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-danmaliki|Danmaliki, 2015]] Quantification of concentration before and after adsorption.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The adsorbent performed extremely well in removing the larger sulfur molecule (DBT). ([[2015-danmaliki|Danmaliki, 2015, p. 193]])
