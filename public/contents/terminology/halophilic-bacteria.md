# Halophilic Bacteria

**Summary**: Microorganisms that are capable of surviving and growing in environments with high salt concentrations.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-budiyanto|Budiyanto, 2017]] studied halophilic bacteria for their ability to degrade pyrene in hypersaline conditions.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-budiyanto|Budiyanto, 2017]] Halophiles are essential for bioremediation in hypersaline environments like produced water from oil fields.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-budiyanto|Budiyanto, 2017]] Isolation from contaminated soil followed by growth testing in varying salinity.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Three strains were isolated: Idiomarina sp. 10PY1A, Halomonas shengliensis 10PY2B, and Halomonas smyrnensis 20PY1A. ([[2017-budiyanto|Budiyanto, 2017, p. 18]])
