# Anoxic Conditions

**Summary**: A state where oxygen levels are depleted, often leading to the preservation of organic matter.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2022-septriandi|Septriandi, 2022]] evaluated anoxic conditions using redox-sensitive trace elements and TOC-S-Fe plots.

## Scientific Context

- [[2022-septriandi|Septriandi, 2022]] Anoxia is a key driver for organic matter preservation in unconventional mudstones.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2022-septriandi|Septriandi, 2022]] Quantified via Mo, Cu, Ni concentrations and TOC content.
