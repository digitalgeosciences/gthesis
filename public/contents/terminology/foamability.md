# Foamability

**Summary**: The ability of a surfactant solution to generate a stable and uniform foam.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-al-drwish|AL-DRWISH, 2024]] evaluated foamability to compare the efficiency of different surfactants in creating bubbles.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-al-drwish|AL-DRWISH, 2024]] Foamability is linked to the ability of surfactant molecules to move quickly to the gas/liquid interface.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-al-drwish|AL-DRWISH, 2024]] Measured by observing bubble count and volume required for maximum height.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Duomeen TTM showed the highest foamability at HPHT due to its methyl group which reduces hydration. ([[2024-al-drwish|AL-DRWISH, 2024, p. 101]])
