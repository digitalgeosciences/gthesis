## Key Findings Across Sources

- Five facies were identified: skeletal coated-grain grainstone, peloid coated-grain grainstone/packstone, Thaumatoporella wackestone, stromatoporoid wackestone/packstone, and Pfenderina trochoidea wackestone/mudstone. ([[2010-al-mojel|Al-Mojel, 2010, p. 93]])

- Four distinctive aeolian depositional facies were identified: dunes, interdunes, sand sheet, and playa lakes. ([[2011-al-masrahy|AL-MASRAHY, 2011, p. 10]])

- Identified lithofacies such as St, Sh, and Sp based on Miall's (1985) classification. ([[2014-waseem|Waseem, 2014, p. 77]])

- Identified three main types of facies associations: fluvial-dominated I, fluvial-dominated II, and fluvial-tidal mixed. ([[2014-mohammedfaiz|Mohammedfaiz, 2014, p. 180]])

- Identified various lithofacies including coral algal reef, mollusc-rich facies, and oolitic grainstones. ([[2015-ali|Ali, 2015, p. 21]])

- Fifteen lithofacies were identified and categorized into subtidal, intertidal, foreshore, supratidal, and estuarine environments. ([[2016-bashri|Bashri, 2016, p. 14]])

- Eleven lithofacies types were distinguished and listed into four groups. ([[2016-makkawi|Makkawi, 2016, p. 122]])

- Four Pickering facies classes (A, B, C, and E) were identified based on grain size and sedimentary structures. ([[2018-butt|Butt, 2018, p. 198]])
