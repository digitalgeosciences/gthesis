# Impedance Contrast

**Summary**: The ratio between the wave velocity and density of different geologic materials.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017]] analyzed the impact of impedance contrast on seismic wave amplification at the sand dune-Sabkha interface.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017]] High impedance contrast between sand and Sabkha leads to significant amplification in the low-frequency band.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017]] calculated as a ratio of velocity and density

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The fundamental mechanism for amplification in sand dunes is the trapping of seismic waves due to high impedance contrast between sediments and the underlying formation. ([[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017, p. 27]])

## Contrasting Values and Interpretations

- [[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017]] Transmission coefficients for typical values range from 1.5 to 1.8.
