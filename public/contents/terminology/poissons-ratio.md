# Poissons Ratio

**Summary**: The ratio of transverse strain to axial strain.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2021-albensaad|Albensaad, 2021]] calculated Poisson's ratio from ultrasonic wave velocities and log data.

- [[2023-aldhafeeri|Aldhafeeri, 2023]] measured Poisson's ratio to assess rock degradation.

- [[2002-elhassan|ElHassan, 2002]] correlated grain contact and quartz percentage with Poisson's ratio.

## Scientific Context

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2021-albensaad|Albensaad, 2021]] Calculated from P and S wave velocities.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Poisson’s ratio had the largest range in Well 2, while it had the least across the other wells. ([[2021-albensaad|Albensaad, 2021, p. 62]])

- Poisson's ratio was reduced after acid treatment in limestone. ([[2023-aldhafeeri|Aldhafeeri, 2023, p. 40]])

- Correlation between grain contact % and Poisson's ratio was 0.77 (1000 psi) and 0.73 (2900 psi). ([[2002-elhassan|ElHassan, 2002, p. 79]])
