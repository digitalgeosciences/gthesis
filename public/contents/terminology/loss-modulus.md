# Loss Modulus

**Summary**: A measure of the energy dissipated as heat in a material.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-omer|Omer, 2012]] measured loss modulus (G“) to evaluate the viscous component of the polymer solution.

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-omer|Omer, 2012]] Helps distinguish between elastic and viscous behavior in the polymer network.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2012-omer|Omer, 2012]] Measured during frequency sweep tests.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- At low angular velocities, G“ values were 0.4, 0.65, 3.19, and 8.35 Pa for concentrations of 0.05, 0.1, 0.5, and 1 wt% respectively. ([[2012-omer|Omer, 2012, p. 56]])
