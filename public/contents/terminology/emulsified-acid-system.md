# Emulsified Acid System

**Summary**: A water-in-oil emulsion containing acid used to provide retarded reaction rates in carbonate reservoirs.

---

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-al-dogail|AL-DOGAIL, 2023]] Used to overcome the limitations of conventional acid systems in carbonate reservoirs.
