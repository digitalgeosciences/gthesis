# Wall Shear Stress

**Summary**: The force per unit area exerted by a fluid as it flows over a surface.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] calculated wall shear stress to determine the erosion rate of wellbore walls.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] It is the primary driver for mechanical erosion of the wellbore wall.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] Calculated using flow velocity and friction factors.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Wall shear stress increases from toe to heel due to increased gas volume and flow velocity. ([[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019, p. 64]])
- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] Varies from 0.744 to 2.904 N/m2 across different grid blocks.
