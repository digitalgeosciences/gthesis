# Weight On Bit

**Summary**: The amount of weight applied to the drill bit during drilling operations.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-khamis|Khamis, 2013]] optimized weight-on-bit (WOB) to balance ROP and bit life.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-khamis|Khamis, 2013]] A primary controllable parameter in drilling optimization.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2013-khamis|Khamis, 2013]] Measured via surface and downhole sensors.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Increasing WOB can increase ROP but may lead to premature bit failure or shorter bit life. ([[2013-khamis|Khamis, 2013, p. 18]])
