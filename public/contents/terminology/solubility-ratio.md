# Solubility Ratio

**Summary**: The ratio of the volume of one phase to the other in a microemulsion.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-hajiyev|Hajiyev, 2023]] analyzed solubilization_ratio to determine the balance between oil and water in the surfactant system.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-hajiyev|Hajiyev, 2023]] Crucial for determining the efficiency of surfactant and co-surfactant combinations.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-hajiyev|Hajiyev, 2023]] Measured via visual observation in sapphire cells.

## Key Findings Across Sources
