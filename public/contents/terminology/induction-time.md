# Induction Time

**Summary**: The time required for the first appearance of a hydrate crystal.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-habiburrahman|HABIBURRAHMAN, 2024]] measured induction time as a function of flow rate and chemical promoters.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-habiburrahman|HABIBURRAHMAN, 2024]] Critical for determining the efficiency of chemical promoters.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-habiburrahman|HABIBURRAHMAN, 2024]] Measured via high-pressure stirred systems.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Higher flow rates were found to reduce the induction time of CO2 hydrates. ([[2024-habiburrahman|HABIBURRAHMAN, 2024, p. 114]])
- [[2024-habiburrahman|HABIBURRAHMAN, 2024]] Induction times ranged from 42 to 8640 seconds depending on conditions.
