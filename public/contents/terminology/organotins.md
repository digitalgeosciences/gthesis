# Organotins

**Summary**: Organometallic compounds containing tin, often used as antifouling agents.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-akere|Akere, 2015]] measured organotin concentrations in sediment and biota.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-akere|Akere, 2015]] Used as indicators of marine pollution from antifouling paints.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-akere|Akere, 2015]] Quantified via GC-MS and HPLC analysis.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Concentrations of six species (MBT, MPhT, DBT, DPhT, TBT, TPhT) were found to be high near the shoreline. ([[2015-akere|Akere, 2015, p. 58]])
