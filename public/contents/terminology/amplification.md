# Amplification

**Summary**: The increase in the amplitude of seismic waves due to high impedance contrasts or resonance.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017]] investigated sand dune amplification caused by high impedance contrast between dunes and underlying formations.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017]] Amplification masks true reservoir signatures, making AVO analysis difficult.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017]] Analyzed via average amplitude spectra and comparison between dune/Sabkha locations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- High contrast in acoustic impedance results in an amplification of seismic waves recorded at stations on the surface. ([[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017, p. 14]])
