# Amplification

**Summary**: The increase in seismic wave amplitude caused by changes in the properties of overlying layers.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017]] studied the amplification effects caused by sand dunes on seismic data.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017]] Amplification complicates the interpretation of true reservoir signals by creating false amplitude variations.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017]] Compared average amplitude spectra of dune and Sabkha traces.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Lower frequencies exhibited much stronger amplification than higher frequencies in the dune sections. ([[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017, p. 56]])
