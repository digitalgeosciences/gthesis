# Cycle Ratio

**Summary**: The ratio of the duration of water injection to gas injection.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-sibaweihi|Sibaweihi, 2013]] optimized the cycle ratio to determine the most effective timing for alternating fluids.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-sibaweihi|Sibaweihi, 2013]] Cycle ratio is a critical design parameter in WAG and FAWAG processes to control mobility.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2013-sibaweihi|Sibaweihi, 2013]] Optimized via stochastic algorithms.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- A cycle ratio between 0.5 and 1 was found to be optimal for maximizing recovery at a constant injection rate. ([[2013-sibaweihi|Sibaweihi, 2013, p. 15]])
