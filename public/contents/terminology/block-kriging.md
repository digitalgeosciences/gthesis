# Block Kriging

**Summary**: A geostatistical method used to estimate the average grade of a block of material.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1987-abdul-latif|Abdul-Latif, 1987]] utilized block kriging to determine the distribution of phosphate grades.

## Scientific Context

- [[1987-abdul-latif|Abdul-Latif, 1987]] Essential for determining the economic viability of mineral deposits.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1987-abdul-latif|Abdul-Latif, 1987]] Applied to drilling data from the Egyptian Geological Survey.

## Key Findings Across Sources
