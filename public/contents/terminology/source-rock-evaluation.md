# Source Rock Evaluation

**Summary**: The assessment of the ability of a rock to generate hydrocarbons.

---

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Hanadir is identified as a candidate for being a main source of gas in the region. ([[2012-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2012, p. 15]])


- Both Tuwaiq Mountain and Jubaila formations possess excellent hydrocarbon attributes (high TOC, sufficient maturity). ([[2017-mohammed|Mohammed, 2017, p. 65]])

- Identified several lithofacies with high organic richness and significant generation potential. ([[2020-usman|Usman, 2020, p. 77]])

- The study identified a high natural gas generative potential due to methane production increasing with temperature. ([[2021-lawal|LAWAL, 2021, p. 51]])

- Determined that the J1 unit is in the gas condensate window based on Tmax and Ro data. ([[2022-septriandi|Septriandi, 2022, p. 51]])

- Un-preserved samples showed significant drops in TOC compared to preserved samples from the same interval. ([[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023, p. 17]])
