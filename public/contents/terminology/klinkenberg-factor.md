# Klinkenberg Factor

**Summary**: A correction factor used to account for gas slippage at the pore walls in low-permeability rocks.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2000-hamid|Hamid, 2000]] calculated Klinkenberg factors using non-linear regression from pulse-decay data.

## Scientific Context

- [[2000-hamid|Hamid, 2000]] Essential for correcting gas permeability measurements in tight formations.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2000-hamid|Hamid, 2000]] Determined via non-linear regression of pulse-decay data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The correlation b = 6.9 was used for permeability values between 0.01 md and 1000 md. ([[2000-hamid|Hamid, 2000, p. 55]])
