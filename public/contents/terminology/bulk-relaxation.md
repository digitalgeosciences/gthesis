# Bulk Relaxation

**Summary**: Relaxation of the fluid molecules in the bulk of the pore space.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024]] analyzed how bulk relaxation (T2B) of drilling fluids affects T2 distribution interpretation.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024]] A critical factor in NMR interpretation when drilling fluids have high polymer or solid content.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024]] Determined from the T2 distribution of the fluid itself.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- In permeable rocks, when T2B is comparable to T2S, it leads to a shift in the T2 distribution and an apparent increase in the microporosity peak. ([[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024, p. 47]])
