# Bulk Relaxation

**Summary**: The relaxation of the entire fluid volume, independent of surface interactions.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024]] investigated how bulk relaxation of drilling fluids impacts NMR T2 values.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024]] Crucial for distinguishing between different fluids and identifying macropores.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024]] Measured via NMR T2 relaxation curves.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Additives like XC polymer and hematite decrease the T2 bulk values of the drilling fluid, impacting macropore interpretation. ([[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024, p. 12]])

## Contrasting Values and Interpretations

- [[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024]] T2B for 5g XC polymer was ~1000 ms; T2B for hematite-containing fluid was < 80 ms.
