# Stromatolites

**Summary**: Laminated structures formed by the binding and trapping of sediment by microbial mats.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-bashri|Bashri, 2016]] identified several types of stromatolite lithofacies in the Dam Formation.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-bashri|Bashri, 2016]] Used to identify depositional environments ranging from intertidal to supratidal.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-bashri|Bashri, 2016]] Morphological classification based on the Logan et al. (1964) scheme.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Identified four morphologically distinct stromatolite types: LLH, SS, SH, and combination forms. ([[2016-bashri|Bashri, 2016, p. 75]])
