# Resonance Effects

**Summary**: The amplification of seismic waves within soft sedimentary cover at the soil's natural frequency.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017]] investigated resonance effects as a major contributor to low-frequency amplification in sediment basins.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-almarzooq|Almarzooq, 2017]] Resonance is a primary driver of signal amplification in sedimentary basins.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

## Key Findings Across Sources
