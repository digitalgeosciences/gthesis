# Tritium

**Summary**: A radioactive isotope of hydrogen used to date water and determine recharge history.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-ahmed-2|Ahmed, 2019]] measured tritium concentrations in the Wasia aquifer.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-ahmed-2|Ahmed, 2019]] Used to distinguish between modern and fossil groundwater.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-ahmed-2|Ahmed, 2019]] Liquid Scintillation Counting.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Samples with tritium below 0.8 TU are likely fossil water from the Late Pleistocene. ([[2019-ahmed-2|Ahmed, 2019, p. 59]])
