# Organotin

**Summary**: Chemical compounds containing a bond between carbon and tin, often used as biocides or catalysts.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-akere|Akere, 2015]] investigated six species of organotins (MBT, MPhT, DBT, DPhT, TBT, TPhT).

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-akere|Akere, 2015]] These are primary pollutants of concern in marine environments due to their use in antifouling paints.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-akere|Akere, 2015]] Quantified via GC-MS analysis.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Concentrations varied significantly based on location and distance from the shoreline. ([[2015-akere|Akere, 2015, p. 11]])
