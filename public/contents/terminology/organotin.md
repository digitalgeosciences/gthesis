# Organotin

**Summary**: Chemical compounds containing a bond between carbon and tin.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-akere|Akere, 2015]] investigated the concentration of organotins in marine environments.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-akere|Akere, 2015]] Used in industrial applications like antifouling paints, they are toxic to marine life and humans.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-akere|Akere, 2015]] Quantified via GC-MS after extraction from sediment and biota.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Six species were investigated: MBT, MPhT, DBT, DPhT, TBT, and TPhT. ([[2015-akere|Akere, 2015, p. 13]])
