# Aqueous Phase Trapping

**Summary**: The trapping of water in the pore spaces of a reservoir, which reduces the relative permeability of hydrocarbons.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-mohammed-3|MOHAMMED, 2017]] addressed the problem of aqueous phase trapping (APT) caused by standard fracturing fluids in tight formations.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-mohammed-3|MOHAMMED, 2017]] A major challenge in tight gas production where it leads to significant reduction in gas flow.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-mohammed-3|MOHAMMED, 2017]] Evaluated via coreflooding experiments.

## Key Findings Across Sources
