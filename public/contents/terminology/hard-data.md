# Hard Data

**Summary**: Data sampled at specific locations, typically from wells.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2001-al-khalifa|Al-Khalifa, 2001]] used hard-data (well logs) as the primary constraint for geostatistical models.

## Scientific Context

- [[2001-al-khalifa|Al-Khalifa, 2001]] Hard data provides the primary constraint for geostatistical estimation.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2001-al-khalifa|Al-Khalifa, 2001]] measured directly from well logs.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Well data are considered 'hard' because they provide direct measurements of reservoir properties at well locations. ([[2001-al-khalifa|Al-Khalifa, 2001, p. 51]])
