# Sagging

**Summary**: The settling of weighting agents out of the suspension in a drilling fluid.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2022-aljaberi|AlJaberi, 2022]] evaluated perlite to mitigate sagging issues.

- [[2024-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2024]] evaluated the ability of OCs to mitigate sagging issues in OBDFs.

## Scientific Context

- [[2022-aljaberi|AlJaberi, 2022]] A critical issue in high-density muds that can lead to well control problems and pipe sticking.

- [[2024-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2024]] evaluated the ability of OCs to mitigate sagging issues in OBDFs.

## Key Findings Across Sources
