# Flow Zone Indicator

**Summary**: A parameter used to identify hydraulic units by combining porosity and permeability.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-adam-2|Adam, 2017]] calculated FZI to define distinct hydraulic units.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-adam-2|Adam, 2017]] FZI helps in identifying zones with similar flow characteristics.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-adam-2|Adam, 2017]] calculated from RQI and normalized porosity.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- 50% of the identified hydraulic units had mid-center FZI values between 0.75 and 1.5. ([[2017-adam-2|Adam, 2017, p. 196]])
