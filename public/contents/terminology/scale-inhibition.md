# Scale Inhibition

**Summary**: The process of using chemicals to prevent or delay the formation of mineral scales in production and injection wells.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2021-gasimli|Gasimli, 2021]] studied scale-inhibition using aminocarboxylic acids as chelating agents.

## Scientific Context

- [[2021-gasimli|Gasimli, 2021]] Critical for maintaining well productivity and preventing equipment failure in sour fields.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2021-gasimli|Gasimli, 2021]] Dynamic adsorption experiments and coreflooding.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Chelating agents like EDTA and DTPA were found effective for inhibition across specific pH ranges. ([[2021-gasimli|Gasimli, 2021, p. 16]])
