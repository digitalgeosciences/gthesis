# Cia

**Summary**: Chemical Index of Alteration, used to assess the degree of chemical weathering.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2022-argadestya|Argadestya, 2022]] used CIA values to determine that Merapi volcaniclastics have low chemical alteration.

## Scientific Context

- [[2022-argadestya|Argadestya, 2022]] Used to compare terrestrial volcaniclastics with Martian minerals.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2022-argadestya|Argadestya, 2022]] Calculated from major elements (Al, CaO, Na2O, K2O) via XRF data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Merapi samples had an average CIA of 45, similar to some Martian sites like Gale Crater (50-56). ([[2022-argadestya|Argadestya, 2022, p. 87]])
