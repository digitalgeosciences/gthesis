# Thermal Maturity

**Summary**: The degree to which organic matter has been altered by heat and pressure.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Samples with TOC > 2% were mostly in the normal oil to condensate-wet gas window (VRo 0.9-1.2%). ([[2020-usman|Usman, 2020, p. 54]])

- TMAX values were also affected by preservation due to changes in S1 and S2 values. ([[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023, p. 13]])
