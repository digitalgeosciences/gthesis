# Wettability

**Summary**: The preference of a solid surface to be in contact with one fluid over another.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-ahmed|Ahmed, 2023]] studied wettability alteration as a critical factor for enhancing oil recovery.

- [[2024-mohamed|Mohamed, 2024]] measured wettability via contact angle to assess the impact of flowback additives on rock surfaces.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-ahmed|Ahmed, 2023]] Wettability determines the mobility and distribution of fluids in the reservoir.

- [[2024-mohamed|Mohamed, 2024]] measured wettability via contact angle to assess the impact of flowback additives on rock surfaces.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-ahmed|Ahmed, 2023]] measured via contact angle and micro-scale flow observations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- A higher degree of oil-wetness was found to provide greater additional recovery from gas injection due to the connectivity of the oil phase. ([[2023-ahmed|Ahmed, 2023, p. 46]])

- Contact angle measurements showed that treatment with flowback additives changed the rock surface to be water-wet. ([[2024-mohamed|Mohamed, 2024, p. 14]])
