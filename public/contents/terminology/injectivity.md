# Injectivity

**Summary**: The ease with which a fluid can be injected into a formation.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-alameer|Alameer, 2023]] evaluated injectivity to determine the optimal horizontal well length for different polymer types.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-alameer|Alameer, 2023]] Critical for determining the feasibility of polymer flooding in high-viscosity reservoirs.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-alameer|Alameer, 2023]] Calculated from simulation data comparing injection rates and pressure drops.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Optimized horizontal wells provided an injectivity index slightly higher than the thinning base case (N1). ([[2023-alameer|Alameer, 2023, p. 67]])
- [[2023-alameer|Alameer, 2023]] Not specifically quantified as a range, but used to compare vertical vs. horizontal cases.
