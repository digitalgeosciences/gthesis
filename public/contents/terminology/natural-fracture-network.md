# Natural Fracture Network

**Summary**: A network of interconnected fractures in a rock mass.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-mahgoub|Mahgoub, 2017]] analyzed the fracture-network to determine its relationship with lithology and geomechanics.

- [[2017-benaafi|Benaafi, 2017]] analyzed the fracture-network within the Wajid Group to assess its impact on fluid flow.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-mahgoub|Mahgoub, 2017]] Fractures are critical for permeability and geomechanical stability in tight shale reservoirs.

- [[2017-benaafi|Benaafi, 2017]] analyzed the fracture-network within the Wajid Group to assess its impact on fluid flow.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-mahgoub|Mahgoub, 2017]] Measured via field counts, spacing analysis, and satellite lineament interpretation.

## Key Findings Across Sources
