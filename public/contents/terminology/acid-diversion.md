# Acid Diversion

**Summary**: The process of ensuring that acid treatment fluids are distributed evenly across all layers in a heterogeneous reservoir.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-alawi|ALAWI, 2019]] evaluated the efficiency of acid diversion using thermochemical reaction products.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-alawi|ALAWI, 2019]] Essential for maximizing the effectiveness of matrix acidizing in heterogeneous carbonate and sandstone formations.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-alawi|ALAWI, 2019]] Measured via injection rate ratios and skin factor reduction.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Diversion is critical to ensure even distribution in zones with high permeability contrast. ([[2019-alawi|ALAWI, 2019, p. 14]])
