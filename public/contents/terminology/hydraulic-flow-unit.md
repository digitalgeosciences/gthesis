# Hydraulic Flow Unit

**Summary**: A rock unit characterized by a unique ratio of permeability to porosity.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010]] divided the reservoir into hydraulic-flow-units based on core data and log responses.

## Scientific Context

- [[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010]] Used to group rocks with similar flow characteristics for saturation modeling.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010]] Calculated from core data and validated using log responses.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Five distinct hydraulic flow units (HU1-HU5) were identified, with HU1 representing the highest quality rock. ([[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010, p. 159]])
