# Ultimate Recovery

**Summary**: The total amount of oil that can be recovered from a reservoir.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-ahmed|AHMED, 2013]] used ultimate recovery (UR) as the primary technical objective function for optimization.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-ahmed|AHMED, 2013]] Used to evaluate the technical efficiency of the flooding process.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2013-ahmed|AHMED, 2013]] Calculated as the total amount of oil produced over the reservoir's life.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Well placement optimization increased UR by 4.37% to 8.94% in Reservoir Model-1 compared to non-optimized well placement. ([[2013-ahmed|AHMED, 2013, p. 163]])
- [[2013-ahmed|AHMED, 2013]] Case-4a showed an increase in UR of 7.49% to 17.50% over waterflooding.
