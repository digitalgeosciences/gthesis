# Nmo Velocity

**Summary**: The velocity used to correct for the moveout of reflections caused by the offset between source and receiver.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-muhammad|Muhammad, 2023]] estimated NMO velocity from surface-consistent statics.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-muhammad|Muhammad, 2023]] Essential for accurate migration and imaging of subsurface structures.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-muhammad|Muhammad, 2023]] Determined via semblance analysis or the proposed parabolic fit to residual statics.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The method achieved a good velocity estimate in the synthetic dataset, with an error of 2.5% relative to the correct velocity. ([[2023-muhammad|Muhammad, 2023, p. 93]])
