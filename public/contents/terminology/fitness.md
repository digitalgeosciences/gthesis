# Fitness

**Summary**: A metric used in optimization algorithms to evaluate the quality of a solution.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-al-nemer|Al-Nemer, 2013]] measured best fitness across multiple model runs for different algorithm configurations.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-al-nemer|Al-Nemer, 2013]] Used to evaluate the convergence of optimization algorithms like LUS and MOL.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2013-al-nemer|Al-Nemer, 2013]] Calculated as the best result found during a set number of model runs.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The LUS algorithm showed significant improvement initially, followed by a plateau in fitness values. ([[2013-al-nemer|Al-Nemer, 2013, p. 81]])
- [[2013-al-nemer|Al-Nemer, 2013]] Values ranged from 0.11 to over 420 depending on the specific algorithm and scale.
