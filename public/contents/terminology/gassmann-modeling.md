# Gassmann Modeling

**Summary**: A theory for the change in elastic properties of a rock or sediment when its pore fluid is changed.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-tarchi|Tarchi, 2024]] used the Gassmann-Hertz-Mindlin model to calculate P-wave velocity.

- [[2025-oyekan|Oyekan, 2025]] applied Gassmann-modeling to calculate the effect of water on the rock's bulk modulus.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The model was used to account for the effects of pressure and mineralogy on seismic velocity. ([[2024-tarchi|Tarchi, 2024, p. 26]])

- The Gassmann equation is valid only at low frequencies where induced pore pressures equilibrate. ([[2025-oyekan|Oyekan, 2025, p. 38]])
