# Gassmann Modeling

**Summary**: A model used to predict the change in P-wave and S-wave velocities when a rock is saturated with different fluids.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-elhaj|Elhaj, 2012]] tested the Gassmann theory for carbonate rocks in the Dam formation.

- [[2024-alkhunaizi|Alkhunaizi, 2024]] used Gassmann-modeling to relate P-wave velocity (VP) to mineralogy and fluid properties.

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-elhaj|Elhaj, 2012]] Used to predict the impact of gas or water saturation on seismic data.

- [[2024-alkhunaizi|Alkhunaizi, 2024]] used Gassmann-modeling to relate P-wave velocity (VP) to mineralogy and fluid properties.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2012-elhaj|Elhaj, 2012]] Applied by comparing dry and saturated velocity measurements.

## Key Findings Across Sources
