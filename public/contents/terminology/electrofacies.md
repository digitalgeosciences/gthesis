# Electrofacies

**Summary**: Facies identified based on unique responses in well log measurements.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-ejaz|Ejaz, 2016]] developed electrofacies for the Sarah Formation using unsupervised clustering.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-ejaz|Ejaz, 2016]] Used to overcome the lack of core data by using continuous well log records.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-ejaz|Ejaz, 2016]] K-means clustering and PCA on a suite of logs including DTCO, DTSM, GR, LLD, PEFL, TNPH, HFK, and WQFM.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The electrofacies model was highly effective for identifying Sarah Formation boundaries and distinguishing between sandstone, shale, and mudstone. ([[2016-ejaz|Ejaz, 2016, p. 98]])
