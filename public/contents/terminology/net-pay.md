# Net Pay

**Summary**: The portion of the reservoir rock that meets specific cutoffs for porosity, permeability, and water saturation.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2003-al-shridi|Al-Shridi, 2003]] determined net pay by applying petrophysical cutoffs to well log data.

## Scientific Context

- [[2003-al-shridi|Al-Shridi, 2003]] Determines the thickness of the wellbore exposed to hydrocarbons.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2003-al-shridi|Al-Shridi, 2003]] determined by applying cutoffs to core and log data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Net pay is essential in determining the total amount of hydrocarbon in a reservoir. ([[2003-al-shridi|Al-Shridi, 2003, p. 28]])
