# Net Pay

**Summary**: The portion of the reservoir rock that meets specific cutoffs for porosity and permeability to be economically productive.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2003-al-shridi|Al-Shridi, 2003]] determined net-pay as a critical variable in the volumetric calculation of hydrocarbon resources.

## Scientific Context

- [[2003-al-shridi|Al-Shridi, 2003]] Determines the vertical extent of the pay zone for volume calculations.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2003-al-shridi|Al-Shridi, 2003]] determined by applying cutoffs to porosity and permeability data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Net pay is determined by the application of cutoffs, which refer to specified lower limits of core or log data. ([[2003-al-shridi|Al-Shridi, 2003, p. 28]])
- [[2003-al-shridi|Al-Shridi, 2003]] Varies between 30 feet and 120 feet.
