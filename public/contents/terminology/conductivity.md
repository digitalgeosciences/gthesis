**Summary**: The ability of a material to conduct electricity.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Adding DTPA-K5 to any brine increased the conductivity, with the most significant differences observed in DI water compared to low salinity water. ([[2015-alarifi|Alarifi, 2015, p. 85]])


- A bell-shaped conductivity profile was observed for dolomite due to its high sensitivity to temperature during reaction. ([[2022-alsulaiman|AlSulaiman, 2022, p. 40]])

- The DC conductivity of water-wet samples was at least ten times the value of oil-wet matches. ([[2023-abdulwahab|Abdulwahab, 2023, p. 45]])

- Coating 40/70 sand with phenolic resin resulted in a five-fold increase in conductivity at 6,000 psi. ([[2024-suleiman-5|Suleiman, 2024, p. 44]])
