# Environmental Sensitivity Index

**Summary**: A numerical value used to rank the vulnerability of an area to environmental damage.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016]] developed an Environmental Sensitivity Index (ESI) for oil spill response.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016]] Used to prioritize cleanup and protection efforts during oil spills.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016]] Categorical scoring based on habitat type.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Assigned scores ranging from 1 to 5, with higher values indicating greater sensitivity. ([[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016, p. 123]])
