# Environmental Sensitivity Index

**Summary**: A ranking system used to determine the sensitivity of coastal areas to oil spills based on physical, biological, and socio-economic factors.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016]] applied an Environmental Sensitivity Index (ESI) to rank shoreline segments for protection priority.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016]] Used to prioritize response efforts during oil spill events.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016]] Multi-criteria analysis of three primary parameters.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The ESI map identified Safaniyah, Khursaniyah-Jubail, Tarut Bay, and several offshore islands as high-priority areas. ([[2016-sudibyo|Sudibyo, 2016, p. 14]])
