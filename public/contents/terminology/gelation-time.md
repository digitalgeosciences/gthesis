# Gelation Time

**Summary**: The time required for a solution to reach a specified high viscosity.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Gelation time was found to be highly sensitive to polymer type, concentration, and temperature. ([[1994-muhammad|Muhammad, 1994, p. 59]])


- Sodium lactate was effective in prolonging the gelation time from hours to days. ([[1997-al-nashmi|Al-Nashmi, 1997, p. 16]])

- Gelation time is exponentially related to temperature and activator concentration. ([[2024-alabdrabalnabi|Alabdrabalnabi, 2024, p. 71]])
