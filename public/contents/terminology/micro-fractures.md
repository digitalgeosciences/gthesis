# Micro Fractures

**Summary**: Small cracks in the rock matrix created by pressure pulses or chemical reactions.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2020-mohammed|Mohammed, 2020]] identified micro_fractures as a mechanism for reducing capillary forces and improving connectivity.

## Scientific Context

- [[2020-mohammed|Mohammed, 2020]] Critical for enhancing flow in tight reservoirs.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2020-mohammed|Mohammed, 2020]] Identified via NMR T2 distribution analysis.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Micro-fractures were confirmed via NMR, showing an increase in cumulative porosity from 17.6% to 18.4% in the Scioto sample. ([[2020-mohammed|Mohammed, 2020, p. 128]])
