# Hysteresis

**Summary**: The difference in capillary pressure or saturation values depending on whether the rock is undergoing drainage or imbibition.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010]] analyzed hysteresis to distinguish between primary and secondary drainage cycles.

## Scientific Context

- [[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010]] Important for correctly modeling reservoir behavior during production and injection.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010]] Comparing drainage and imbibition cycles in laboratory tests.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Trap hysteresis is the difference between first and second drainage, while drag hysteresis is the difference between first imbibition and second drainage. ([[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010, p. 66]])
