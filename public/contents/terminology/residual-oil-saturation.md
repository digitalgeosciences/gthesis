# Residual Oil Saturation

**Summary**: The amount of oil that remains in the reservoir after a water flood.

## Scientific Context

- [[1990-al-mumen|Al-Mumen, 1990]] Sor is a key indicator of the efficiency of the displacement process.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1990-al-mumen|Al-Mumen, 1990]] Determined from core flooding and capillary pressure analysis.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Sor decreased from 43.4% to 15.7% as IFT was reduced by higher salt concentrations. ([[1990-al-mumen|Al-Mumen, 1990, p. 88]])


- Viscoelastic polymers can reduce SOR by up to 24% compared to standard waterfloods in some cases. ([[2023-dafaalla|Dafaalla, 2023, p. 43]])

- Viscoelastic polymer flooding resulted in a significant reduction in Sor compared to standard methods. ([[2024-jameel|Jameel, 2024, p. 46]])
