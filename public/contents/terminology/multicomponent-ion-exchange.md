# Multicomponent Ion Exchange

**Summary**: The exchange of multiple types of ions between the rock surface and the brine.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-suleiman-3|Suleiman, 2024]] analyzed multicomponent ion exchange as a mechanism for wettibility alteration.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-suleiman-3|Suleiman, 2024]] A key mechanism for the alteration of rock surface chemistry in saline environments.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-suleiman-3|Suleiman, 2024]] Inferred from XRF and ICP analysis of effluent.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Na+ replaced Ca2+ and Al3+ on the rock surface, expanding the electrical double layer to promote hydrophilicity. ([[2024-suleiman-3|Suleiman, 2024, p. 67]])
