# Pyrene

**Summary**: A tetracyclic aromatic hydrocarbon, often used as a model for high molecular weight PAHs.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Pyrene was more resistant to biodegradation than naphthalene or phenanthrene. ([[2015-malik|Malik, 2015, p. 95]])


- The fastest degradation rate was observed in Halomonas smyrnensis 20PY1A at 37 °C and pH 7. ([[2017-budiyanto|Budiyanto, 2017, p. 20]])
