# Pore System

**Summary**: The interconnected network of pores within a rock.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Micro-pores in shales have the ability to imbibe more than large pores in certain conditions. ([[2017-al-arfaj|Al-Arfaj, 2017, p. 39]])


- Six pore types were identified: microporosity, intraparticle, interparticle, vuggy, moldic, and fracture. ([[2020-al-zoukani|Al-Zoukani, 2020, p. 137]])
