# Ground Uplift

**Summary**: The upward movement of the ground surface due to subsurface pressure changes.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-khan|Khan, 2017]] measured ground uplift to assess the impact of CO2 injection on the surface.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-khan|Khan, 2017]] Used to monitor the impact of injection on the surface and detect caprock leaks.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-khan|Khan, 2017]] Simulated using CMG-GEM software.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Ground uplift was maximum just above the injection point and extended for several kilometers. ([[2017-khan|Khan, 2017, p. 48]])
- [[2017-khan|Khan, 2017]] Values up to 26.1 mm were observed in simulation.
