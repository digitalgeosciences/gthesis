# First Arrival

**Summary**: The first point in time where a seismic wave is detected at a receiver.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-edigbue|Edigbue, 2016]] focused on enhancing the first arrival of noisy seismic data to improve automatic picking.

- [[2021-almashhor|AlMashhor, 2021]] evaluated the accuracy of different methods for identifying the first-arrival.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Synthetic data showed accurate first arrivals after applying 3D SVI. ([[2016-edigbue|Edigbue, 2016, p. 13]])

- First arrival traveltime is essential to be picked accurately to enhance the efficiency of the static correction methods. ([[2021-almashhor|AlMashhor, 2021, p. 14]])

- Accurate picking of first arrivals is critical for determining velocity analysis, static corrections, and migration. ([[2023-bargees|Bargees, 2023, p. 17]])
