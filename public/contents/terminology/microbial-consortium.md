# Microbial Consortium

**Summary**: A group of different microbial strains working together to degrade complex pollutants.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-malik|Malik, 2015]] compared the degradation performance of several microbial consortia.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-malik|Malik, 2015]] consortia are often more effective than single strains for complex mixtures.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-malik|Malik, 2015]] compared across three different consortia (C2, C4, C7).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Consortia C4 and C7 showed higher degradation rates for pyrene than individual isolates. ([[2015-malik|Malik, 2015, p. 106]])
