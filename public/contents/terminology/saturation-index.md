# Saturation Index

**Summary**: A measure of the thermodynamic equilibrium between a mineral and its surrounding aqueous solution.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1992-hassan|Hassan, 1992]] calculated saturation indices for approximately 200 mineral phases using PCWATEQ.

## Scientific Context

- [[1992-hassan|Hassan, 1992]] Used to identify the primary mineral phases controlling water chemistry and potential for mineral precipitation or dissolution.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1992-hassan|Hassan, 1992]] calculated using PCWATEQ software based on chemical concentration data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Both aquifers are oversaturated with calcite and dolomite, and undersaturated with gypsum. ([[1992-hassan|Hassan, 1992, p. 19]])
