# Specific Surface Area

**Summary**: The total surface area of a material per unit of bulk volume.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-alabbad|Alabbad, 2024]] used specific-surface-area to characterize the interaction between pore fluids and matrix minerals.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-alabbad|Alabbad, 2024]] Indicator of pore shape and potential for chemical reactions.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-alabbad|Alabbad, 2024]] Calculated from segmented 3D FIB-SEM images.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- A higher specific surface area indicates more crack-shaped pores or connected kerogen bodies, potentially increasing chemical reaction potential. ([[2024-alabbad|Alabbad, 2024, p. 136]])
