# Poisson Ratio

**Summary**: The ratio of transverse strain to axial strain in a material.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-tariq|Tariq, 2016]] measured and predicted Poisson's ratio for limestone formations.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-tariq|Tariq, 2016]] Used to characterize rock deformability and stiffness.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-tariq|Tariq, 2016]] Measured via triaxial tests and predicted from well logs.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- ANN models showed high accuracy (R² = 0.96) in predicting Poisson's ratio from log data. ([[2016-tariq|Tariq, 2016, p. 109]])
- [[2016-tariq|Tariq, 2016]] Values ranged between 0.1 and 0.5.
