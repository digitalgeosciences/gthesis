# Filter Cake

**Summary**: A layer of solids deposited on the borehole wall during drilling.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024]] investigated filter cake formation and its impact on NMR signal interpretation.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024]] Filter cakes can block pores and alter local fluid properties, affecting log interpretation.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024]] Observed via NMR T2 distribution changes after addition of solids.

## Key Findings Across Sources
