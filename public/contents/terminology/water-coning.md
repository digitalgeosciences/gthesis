# Water Coning

**Summary**: The upward movement of water toward a well due to pressure drawdown, forming a cone.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1994-hakim|Hakim, 1994]] studied the impact of horizontal well geometry on water coning.

## Scientific Context

- [[1994-hakim|Hakim, 1994]] A major challenge in producing oil from reservoirs with underlying aquifers.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1994-hakim|Hakim, 1994]] Monitored via saturation distribution and production rates.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Coning is mitigated in horizontal wells by reducing the pressure drawdown per unit length. ([[1994-hakim|Hakim, 1994, p. 32]])
