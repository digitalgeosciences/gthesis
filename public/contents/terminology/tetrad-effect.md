# Tetrad Effect

**Summary**: A change in REE behavior as a function of atomic number due to non-Rayleigh fractionation.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] calculated the tetrad effects (TE1,3) for the Al-Ghurayyah pluton.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] Used to distinguish between magmatic and metasomatic processes.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] Calculated from multi-element ratios like Y/Ho and Zr/Hf.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Calculated values ranged from 1.25 to 1.46, indicating high differentiation and potential fluid interaction. ([[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024, p. 90]])
