# Plastic Viscosity

**Summary**: A measure of the resistance of a fluid to flow under shear stress.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Increasing the concentration of nanosilica was decreasing the plastic viscosity of the drilling fluid. ([[2017-alakbari|Alakbari, 2017, p. 82]])


- A decrease in plastic viscosity should be a signal corresponding decrease in the viscosity at the bit, leading to higher penetration rates. ([[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017, p. 45]])

- The PV ANFIS model achieved an R of 0.96 and AAPE of 4.59% for training data. ([[2021-alsabaa|Alsabaa, 2021, p. 76]])

- The addition of 4% BWOC perlite reduced the plastic viscosity of ilmenite-weighted cement by 29.6%. ([[2023-ahmed-2|AHMED, 2023, p. 180]])
