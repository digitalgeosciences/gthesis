# Dewpoint Pressure

**Summary**: The pressure at which the first drop of liquid forms in a gas mixture.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2001-humoud|Humoud, 2001]] investigated dewpoint pressure as the primary dependent variable for characterizing gas-condensate fluids.

## Scientific Context

- [[2001-humoud|Humoud, 2001]] Critical for determining reservoir performance and designing production systems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2001-humoud|Humoud, 2001]] Experimental determination via Constant Mass Expansion (CME) tests.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Dewpoint pressures in the study ranged from 2685 psia to 7465 psia. ([[2001-humoud|Humoud, 2001, p. 60]])
