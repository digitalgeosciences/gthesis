# Optimal Salinity

**Summary**: The salt concentration at which the largest volume of microemulsion is formed.

---

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Optimal salinity was identified in the range of 11522 to 17283 ppm for several surfactant formulations. ([[2023-hajiyev|Hajiyev, 2023, p. 115]])


- A secondary optimal salinity was identified at higher concentrations than the primary optimal salinity. ([[1987-fuseni|Fuseni, 1987, p. 23]])
