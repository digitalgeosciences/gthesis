# Free Water Level

**Summary**: The depth at which the pressure of the water phase equals the pressure of the oil/gas phase.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010]] determined free-water-level to establish the base for saturation height models.

## Scientific Context

- [[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010]] Defines the bottom of the hydrocarbon column.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010]] Determined from pressure-depth plots.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- A field-wide average free water level was identified at -4937 feet. ([[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010, p. 25]])
