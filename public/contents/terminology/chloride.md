# Chloride

**Summary**: An ion that serves as a conservative indicator of salinity and pollution.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2011-alsadeq|Alsadeq, 2011]] monitored chloride to assess salinity and potential septic system contamination.

## Scientific Context

- [[2011-alsadeq|Alsadeq, 2011]] Chlorides are used to track salinity and pollution from waste systems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods
