# Root Zone

**Summary**: The upper part of the soil profile where most plant roots are concentrated.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-prasetyo|Prasetyo, 2024]] investigated the root zone to understand its role in water and nutrient cycling.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-prasetyo|Prasetyo, 2024]] Crucial for understanding water uptake and nutrient cycling in arid environments.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-prasetyo|Prasetyo, 2024]] Identified via GPR and ERT resistivity anomalies.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- A resistive zone was identified below 3 cm, corresponding to the expected spatial distribution of the root zone. ([[2024-prasetyo|Prasetyo, 2024, p. 42]])
