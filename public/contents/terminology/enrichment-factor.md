# Enrichment Factor

**Summary**: A ratio used to determine if a substance is enriched in an environment relative to its natural background.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024]] calculated the enrichment-factor (EF) for various HMs.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024]] Identifies the extent of human activity in metal distribution.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024]] calculated as (Concentration / Crustal Background).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- HMs such as Zn, Pb, Ni, Cu, Cr, Cd, and As showed extreme enrichment, indicating heavy influence from anthropogenic sources. ([[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024, p. 141]])
