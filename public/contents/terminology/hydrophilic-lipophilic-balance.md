# Hydrophilic Lipophilic Balance

**Summary**: A measure of the degree to which a surfactant is hydrophilic or lipophilic.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-mohamed|MOHAMED, 2014]] developed a screening approach based on the HLB of surfactants for W/O emulsions.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-mohamed|MOHAMED, 2014]] Used to select appropriate surfactants for emulsifying water-soluble materials in oil.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-mohamed|MOHAMED, 2014]] Calculated based on the chemical structure of the surfactant.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Surfactants with higher HLB values within the recommended range (3.5-6) resulted in more stable W/O emulsions. ([[2014-mohamed|MOHAMED, 2014, p. 48]])
