# Transient Productivity Index

**Summary**: A measure of the well's ability to produce fluids from the reservoir over time.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 2014]] calculated the specific transient productivity index for U-shaped wells.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 2014]] Used to compare different production scenarios and well designs.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 2014]] derived from integrating point-source functions.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The values were identical for equal rates but doubled at initial times for double flow rates before converging as reservoir response dominated. ([[2014-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 2014, p. 116]])
