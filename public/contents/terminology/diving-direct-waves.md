# Diving Direct Waves

**Summary**: Seismic waves that travel along a curved path due to increasing velocity with depth.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-alsahwan|AlSahwan, 2023]] used diving-direct-waves to estimate water saturation in sand dunes.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-alsahwan|AlSahwan, 2023]] Useful for mapping large areas in unconsolidated sediments.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-alsahwan|AlSahwan, 2023]] Analyzed via travel time (T) and offset (X).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Diving direct waves are used because they provide information on the average saturation along the raypath. ([[2023-alsahwan|AlSahwan, 2023, p. 33]])
