# Diving Direct Waves

**Summary**: Seismic waves that curve downward in a medium where velocity increases with depth.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2025-oyekan|Oyekan, 2025]] utilized diving-direct-waves to link source-receiver offsets to the underlying P-wave velocity profile.

## Scientific Context

- [[2025-oyekan|Oyekan, 2025]] Used as a diagnostic tool for determining the depth of the first high-velocity layer or estimating saturation.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2025-oyekan|Oyekan, 2025]] Calculated from observed source-receiver offsets and velocity models.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The offset and two-way travel time of the diving direct wave can be calculated using parametric equations based on the P-wave velocity function. ([[2025-oyekan|Oyekan, 2025, p. 48]])
