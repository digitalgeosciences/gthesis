# Surfactant Polymer

**Summary**: A combined EOR method using surfactants to reduce interfacial tension and polymers for mobility control.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-ahmed|AHMED, 2013]] investigated surfactant-polymer (SP) flooding as the primary chemical EOR process.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-ahmed|AHMED, 2013]] Used to improve both sweep efficiency and displacement efficiency.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2013-ahmed|AHMED, 2013]] Simulated in a heterogeneous reservoir.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- SP flooding combines the advantage of mobility control by polymer and interfacial reduction by surfactant. ([[2013-ahmed|AHMED, 2013, p. 25]])
