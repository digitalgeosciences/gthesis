# Edge Detection

**Summary**: A process to identify and highlight boundaries or discontinuities in an image.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-adetokunbo|Adetokunbo, 2015]] evaluated edge-detection as a primary tool for delineating fault blocks and channels.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-adetokunbo|Adetokunbo, 2015]] Used to identify structural and stratigraphic discontinuities like faults, fractures, and channels.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-adetokunbo|Adetokunbo, 2015]] Implemented via convolution of 3x3 or 5x5 magic square/cube kernels.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The 5x5 magic cube operators provided better delineation of the channel system than the 3x3x3 versions or the Sobel filter. ([[2015-adetokunbo|Adetokunbo, 2015, p. 58]])
