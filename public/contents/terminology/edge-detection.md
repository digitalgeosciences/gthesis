# Edge Detection

**Summary**: An image processing operation that finds and highlights boundaries or discontinuities in an image.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-adetokunbo|Adetokunbo, 2015]] applied edge-detection to identify structural and stratigraphic features like faults and channels.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-adetokunbo|Adetokunbo, 2015]] Used to identify structural and stratigraphic features such as faults, fractures, and channels in 3D seismic data.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-adetokunbo|Adetokunbo, 2015]] Implemented via discrete differentiation operators (convolution kernels).
