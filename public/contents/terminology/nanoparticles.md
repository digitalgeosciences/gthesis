# Nanoparticles

**Summary**: Particles with a diameter of less than 100 nm used to modify rock surface properties.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2022-xiao|Xiao, 2022]] evaluated the use of nanoparticles as an alternative or supplement to surfactants for wettibility modification.

## Scientific Context

- [[2022-xiao|Xiao, 2022]] Nanoparticles offer high surface area and can be tailored with coatings to improve stability in harsh environments.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2022-xiao|Xiao, 2022]] Concentration-dependent studies.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Nanoparticles can change the wettibility of carbonate rocks from strongly oil-wet to water-wet at relatively low concentrations. ([[2022-xiao|Xiao, 2022, p. 89]])
