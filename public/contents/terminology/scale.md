# Scale

**Summary**: The accumulation of solid minerals in the pores or around the wellbore.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-othman|Othman, 2023]] investigated scale formation caused by divalent ions in seawater-based fluids.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-othman|Othman, 2023]] Scale can plug pore spaces and reduce permeability, making it a critical concern for seawater usage.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-othman|Othman, 2023]] Evaluated using the Differential Scale Loop (DSL).

## Key Findings Across Sources
