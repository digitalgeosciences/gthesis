# Storativity Ratio

**Summary**: The ratio of the storage capacity of the fracture system to the total system's storage.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-al-abdulmohsin|Al-Abdulmohsin, 2012]] used storativity ratio (ω) to characterize the contribution of fractures to the overall reservoir volume.

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-al-abdulmohsin|Al-Abdulmohsin, 2012]] Determines how quickly the fracture network responds to pressure changes.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2012-al-abdulmohsin|Al-Abdulmohsin, 2012]] Calculated based on fracture and matrix porosity/compressibility.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Increasing ω values resulted in a shallower dip in the pressure derivative curve at late times, indicating more fracture storage. ([[2012-al-abdulmohsin|Al-Abdulmohsin, 2012, p. 39]])
