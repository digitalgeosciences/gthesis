# Acid Fracturing

**Summary**: A stimulation method where acid is used to etch the rock surface and create flow paths.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-mohammed-3|MOHAMMED, 2017]] developed a fluid system that could be used for both proppant and acid fracturing.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The new fluid system... can be either used for proppant fracturing (high pH) or acid fracturing (low pH). ([[2017-mohammed-3|MOHAMMED, 2017, p. 16]])


- The use of gel acid was found more effective than straight acid in dolomite due to its impact on the diffusion coefficient. ([[2022-alsulaiman|AlSulaiman, 2022, p. 54]])

- Foamed acid can reduce the consumption of spent acid and provide deeper penetration into low permeability zones. ([[2024-al-drwish|AL-DRWISH, 2024, p. 70]])
