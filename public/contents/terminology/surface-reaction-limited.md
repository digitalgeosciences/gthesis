# Surface Reaction Limited

**Summary**: A regime where the rate of dissolution is controlled by chemical kinetics at the surface.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014]] identified surface reaction limited regimes at high rotation speeds.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014]] Helps identify the point at which further rotation does not increase dissolution rate.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014]] Determined by comparing dissolution rates at different rotation speeds.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- At high rotational speeds, the acid dissolution rate was constant, indicating a surface reaction limited regime. ([[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014, p. 15]])
