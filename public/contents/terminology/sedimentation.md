# Sedimentation

**Summary**: The settling of solid particles in the cement slurry due to gravity.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-ahmed-2|AHMED, 2023]] evaluated sedimentation to ensure a uniform density and consistent properties along the cement column.

- [[2023-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2023]] addressed sedimentation issues in high-density systems using additives like Micromax and Perlite.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-ahmed-2|AHMED, 2023]] Sedimentation leads to non-uniform cement columns and potential failure of the well's integrity.

- [[2023-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2023]] addressed sedimentation issues in high-density systems using additives like Micromax and Perlite.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-ahmed-2|AHMED, 2023]] Assessed via visual observation, rheology, and density gradient measurements.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Micromax showed less settling than hematite because of its smaller particle size distribution. ([[2023-ahmed-2|AHMED, 2023, p. 66]])


- Micromax was successfully used as a solution to mitigate the settling of heavy weighting agents like hematite. ([[2023-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2023, p. 33]])
