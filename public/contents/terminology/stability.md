# Stability

**Summary**: The ability of a substance to remain in its original state or form.

---

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Nanoparticles showed good stability in deionized water but were unstable in seawater and high-concentration NaCl. ([[2016-alhamad|AlHamad, 2016, p. 54]])
- [[2016-alhamad|AlHamad, 2016]] Stability was confirmed up to 3 days in deionized water but less than 1 hour in seawater.

- Stability is a function of both pore pressure and stress in coupled geomechanical models. ([[2017-khan|Khan, 2017, p. 36]])
