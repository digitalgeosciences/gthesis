# Solubilization Ratio

**Summary**: The ratio of the volume of oil or water dissolved in the microemulsion phase to the volume of surfactant in that phase.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-hajiyev|Hajiyev, 2023]] used solubilization ratios to characterize the effectiveness of the surfactant formulation.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-hajiyev|Hajiyev, 2023]] Solubilization ratios are used as a proxy for IFT when direct measurement is difficult at high temperatures.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-hajiyev|Hajiyev, 2023]] calculated from the volumes of the three phases in the system.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- An SR of 10 or more is used as a target for identifying promising formulations. ([[2023-hajiyev|Hajiyev, 2023, p. 32]])
