# Free Water Separation

**Summary**: The separation of water from the cement slurry, which can lead to gas migration.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-murtaza|Murtaza, 2013]] evaluated free water separation to ensure a stable hydrostatic pressure in the well.

- [[2017-ahmed|Ahmed, 2017]] measured free water separation of the cement slurries.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-murtaza|Murtaza, 2013]] Prevents settling of particles and maintains hydrostatic pressure.

- [[2017-ahmed|Ahmed, 2017]] measured free water separation of the cement slurries.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2013-murtaza|Murtaza, 2013]] Measured by aging the slurry for 2 hours.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Both Nano silica and Nano clay were found very effective in reducing free water contents. ([[2013-murtaza|Murtaza, 2013, p. 97]])


- Determined that fiber addition reduces the amount of free water content. ([[2017-ahmed|Ahmed, 2017, p. 17]])
