# Thermally Activated Polymers

**Summary**: Polymers that exhibit a significant increase in viscosity when exposed to high temperatures.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014]] evaluated the thermo-thickening behavior of TVPs under different salinity conditions.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014]] Used for deep and hot wells where high-temperature stability is required.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014]] Measured via flow temperature ramp tests.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The thermo-thickening property would come into effect only at highly saline conditions and moderately high polymer concentrations. ([[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014, p. 80]])
