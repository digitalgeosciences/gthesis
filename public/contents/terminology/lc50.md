# LC50

**Summary**: The concentration of a substance that is lethal to 50% of the test population within a specified time period.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2018-amiruddin|Amiruddin, 2018]] determined LC50 values for both Arabian Killifish and Brine Shrimp across various oil-related toxicants.

## Scientific Context

- [[2018-amiruddin|Amiruddin, 2018]] Used as a primary measure of acute toxicity in aquatic organisms.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2018-amiruddin|Amiruddin, 2018]] Calculated using SPSS by fitting log-logistic functions.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The LC50 of Arabian Killifish exposed to oil plus dispersant was 164.2 mgL-1. ([[2018-amiruddin|Amiruddin, 2018, p. 53]])
