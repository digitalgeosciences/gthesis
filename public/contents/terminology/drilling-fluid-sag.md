# Drilling Fluid Sag

**Summary**: The settling of weighting agents like barite or calcite in the drilling fluid column.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-badhafere|Badhafere, 2023]] monitored the sag of barite and calcite in drilling fluids using SIP.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-badhafere|Badhafere, 2023]] Sag affects the density and stability of the well control barrier.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-badhafere|Badhafere, 2023]] Monitored via changes in real conductivity and impedance over time.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Sag was successfully detected in less than 5 hours with high sensitivity for real conductivity and impedance. ([[2023-badhafere|Badhafere, 2023, p. 103]])
