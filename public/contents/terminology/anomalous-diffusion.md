# Anomalous Diffusion

**Summary**: Diffusion that does not follow the standard laws of Brownian motion, often due to complex pore structures.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-khan-2|Khan, 2013]] developed solutions for anomalous diffusion of slightly compressible fluids.

- [[2015-alloush|Alloush, 2015]] modeled anomalous diffusion using fractional derivatives in the Forchheimer equation.

## Scientific Context
