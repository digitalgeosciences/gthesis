# Upper Thermal Limit

**Summary**: The maximum temperature at which an organism can survive or remain active.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-prayudi|Prayudi, 2023]] investigated the upper thermal limit of biocalcifiers to assess resilience against climate change.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-prayudi|Prayudi, 2023]] Crucial for predicting the impact of rising sea temperatures on coastal biodiversity.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-prayudi|Prayudi, 2023]] Controlled heating in a petri dish across multiple temperature levels (40-60°C).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Both gastropods and ostracods exhibited elevated upper thermal tolerance, particularly when immersed in water. ([[2023-prayudi|Prayudi, 2023, p. 31]])
