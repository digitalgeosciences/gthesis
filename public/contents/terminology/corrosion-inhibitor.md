# Corrosion Inhibitor

**Summary**: Chemical additives used to protect metal surfaces from corrosion in acidic environments.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-al-drwish|AL-DRWISH, 2024]] investigated the impact of CI on foam viscosity and stability.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-al-drwish|AL-DRWISH, 2024]] Necessary for industrial application but often detrimental to foam stability.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-al-drwish|AL-DRWISH, 2024]] Evaluated by comparing viscosity of solutions with and without CI.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The addition of CI caused a sharp drop in foam viscosity (55% for Duomeen TTM) due to hindered micelle formation. ([[2024-al-drwish|AL-DRWISH, 2024, p. 186]])
