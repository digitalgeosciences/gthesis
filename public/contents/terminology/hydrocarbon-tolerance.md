# Hydrocarbon Tolerance

**Summary**: The ability of a foam to maintain its properties in the presence of oil.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-gowida|Gowida, 2024]] assessed hydrocarbon tolerance by measuring drainage rates in the presence of oil.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-gowida|Gowida, 2024]] Essential for UBD where oil may enter the wellbore from the formation.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-gowida|Gowida, 2024]] Measured by monitoring foam drainage rates in systems containing varying volumes of oil.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Formulations containing both PAC and bentonite showed superior hydrocarbon tolerance, with drainage rates lower than the commercial blend. ([[2024-gowida|Gowida, 2024, p. 101]])
