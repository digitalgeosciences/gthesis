# Subsidence

**Summary**: The sinking of the earth's crust, creating accommodation space for sediment.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-fahmi|Fahmi, 2024]] evaluated subsidence to determine its role in generating accommodation space.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-fahmi|Fahmi, 2024]] Crucial for determining total accommodation space in a basin.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-fahmi|Fahmi, 2024]] Modeled using differentiated rates across different members.

## Key Findings Across Sources
