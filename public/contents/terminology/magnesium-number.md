# Magnesium Number

**Summary**: The ratio of magnesium to total iron in a mineral, used as an indicator of mantle melting.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023]] calculated Mg# values for olivine and pyroxene to assess the degree of partial melting.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023]] Used to determine the extent of partial melting and mantle source characteristics.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023]] calculated from major element concentrations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The Mg# range of Harrat Uwayrid peridotite xenoliths (88.2 – 90.5), is similar to-western Arabian peridotite xenoliths average (Mg# of 88.8). ([[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023, p. 66]])
