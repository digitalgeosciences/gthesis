# Productivity

**Summary**: A measure of the flow capacity of a well, typically expressed as the ratio of production rate to pressure drawdown.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1999-fawumi|Fawumi, 1999]] measured productivity index (PI) to quantify the impact of liquid dropout.

## Scientific Context

- [[1999-fawumi|Fawumi, 1999]] Used to evaluate well performance and the impact of near-wellbore damage or phase changes.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1999-fawumi|Fawumi, 1999]] Calculated from simulation results.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The PI declined from 23.53 MSCF/D/psia to 2.11 MSCF/D/psia as flow rates increased to 60 MMSCF/D. ([[1999-fawumi|Fawumi, 1999, p. 160]])
