# Sabkha Sedimentology

**Summary**: The study of salt flats (sabkhas) and their associated sedimentary processes.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Sediment is one of the main components of the Sabkha and essential to the functioning of aquatic ecosystems. ([[2015-jawad|Jawad, 2015, p. 14]])


- Sabkha Jayb Uwayyid is characterized by a high concentration of salts (TDS up to 414,515 ppm) and poor sorting. ([[2016-armoh|Armoh, 2016, p. 56]])

- Sedimentary facies are characterized by a central halite crust, gypsum mush, and brown mud. ([[1994-al-guwaizani|Al-Guwaizani, 1994, p. 12]])
