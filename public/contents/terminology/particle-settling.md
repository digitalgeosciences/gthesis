# Particle Settling

**Summary**: The tendency of solid particles to settle out of the fluid medium.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-amer|Amer, 2012]] tested particle settling to assess suspension capability.

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-amer|Amer, 2012]] Prevents density differences across the cement column.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2012-amer|Amer, 2012]] Measured by comparing densities at top, middle, and bottom of the sample.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Samples with 1.0% and 2.0% Nano silica exhibited good suspension capability, preventing density segregation. ([[2012-amer|Amer, 2012, p. 123]])
