# Geosteering

**Summary**: The process of adjusting the trajectory of a horizontal well to stay within the target reservoir zone.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2011-al-dhufairi|AL-DHUFAIRI, 2011]] evaluated geosteering effectiveness using mobility profile data.

- [[2011-al-amri|Al-Amri, 2011]] utilized geosteering with live monitoring to optimize the exploitation of remaining producible oil in the mother-Field.
