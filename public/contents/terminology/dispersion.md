# Dispersion

**Summary**: The breakdown of clay particles into smaller pieces when exposed to fluids.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-al-arfaj|Al-Arfaj, 2017]] evaluated the dispersion characteristics of shale samples in different drilling fluids.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-al-arfaj|Al-Arfaj, 2017]] Dispersion causes wellbore instability and complicates drilling operations.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-al-arfaj|Al-Arfaj, 2017]] assessed through physical observation and mineralogical analysis.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Dispersion leads to increased solids loading and reduced hole cleaning efficiency. ([[2017-al-arfaj|Al-Arfaj, 2017, p. 13]])
