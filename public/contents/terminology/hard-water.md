# Hard Water

**Summary**: Water containing high concentrations of dissolved minerals, primarily calcium and magnesium.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2011-alsadeq|Alsadeq, 2011]] characterized groundwater as hard-water based on calcium and magnesium content.

## Scientific Context

- [[2011-alsadeq|Alsadeq, 2011]] Hardness affects water usage and is linked to local lithology.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2011-alsadeq|Alsadeq, 2011]] Calculation based on Calcium and Magnesium concentrations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The water in Tarout Island can be classified as very hard water (over 300 ppm). ([[2011-alsadeq|Alsadeq, 2011, p. 111]])
