# Groundwater Table

**Summary**: The upper surface of the zone of saturation in an aquifer.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017]] identified via low resistivity values in the ERT tomograms.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017]] Groundwater presence affects rock stability and electrical resistivity.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017]] identified via ERT inversion and confirmed by well-sounder data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The water table was clearly defined by a low-resistivity layer (< 3 Ohm.m) starting at approximately 10 m depth. ([[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017, p. 73]])
