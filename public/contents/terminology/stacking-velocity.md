# Stacking Velocity

**Summary**: The velocity used to align and sum seismic traces during stacking.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2020-alzahrani|Alzahrani, 2020]] evaluated stacking velocity using both conventional and squaring techniques.

## Scientific Context

- [[2020-alzahrani|Alzahrani, 2020]] Crucial for retrieving enhanced seismic data stacks and improving image quality.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2020-alzahrani|Alzahrani, 2020]] Determined from the slope of the curve in the t2-x2 plane.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Stacking velocities picked using the squaring technique are similar or better than those selected conventionally. ([[2020-alzahrani|Alzahrani, 2020, p. 17]])
