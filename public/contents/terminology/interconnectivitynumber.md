# Interconnectivitynumber

**Summary**: A quantitative measure of how well different pore systems are connected.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2017]] used ICN to quantify the connectivity between micro, meso, and macro pores.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2017]] Used to assess the effectiveness of acid treatment in connecting the reservoir.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2017]] Calculated from the ratio of diffusion coupling intensity to maximum intensity.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- HCl in seawater resulted in an ICN of 0 for some connections, indicating significant formation damage. ([[2017-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2017, p. 81]])
