# Rock Physics Model

**Summary**: A mathematical transform that relates elastic rock properties to volumetric ones.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2020-almumtin|Almumtin, 2020]] developed a Rock Physics Model (RPM) to link seismic data to reservoir properties.

- [[2024-alabbad|Alabbad, 2024]] developed rock-physics-model for both carbonate and mudrock systems.

## Scientific Context

- [[2020-almumtin|Almumtin, 2020]] It is the primary tool for translating seismic data into reservoir parameters.

- [[2024-alabbad|Alabbad, 2024]] developed rock-physics-model for both carbonate and mudrock systems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2020-almumtin|Almumtin, 2020]] Fitting theoretical curves (e.g., stiff-sand, Raymer) to cross-plots of elastic vs. volumetric properties.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The stiff-sand model was selected as the primary RPM due to its rigor and adjustability. ([[2020-almumtin|Almumtin, 2020, p. 14]])

- The inclusion of kerogen in the model was essential for accurate mudrock characterization. ([[2024-alabbad|Alabbad, 2024, p. 123]])
