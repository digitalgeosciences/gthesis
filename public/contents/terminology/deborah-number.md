# Deborah Number

**Summary**: The ratio of the relaxation time of a fluid to its residence time.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-dafaalla|Dafaalla, 2023]] used the Deborah number to quantify the extent of viscoelastic behavior in polymer solutions.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-dafaalla|Dafaalla, 2023]] Indicates whether a fluid behaves more like a solid or a liquid during flow.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-dafaalla|Dafaalla, 2023]] Calculated from rheological and flow parameters.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- A Deborah number around 0.05 is where significant impacts on pressure drop are first felt. ([[2023-dafaalla|Dafaalla, 2023, p. 34]])
- [[2023-dafaalla|Dafaalla, 2023]] Critical values vary between 0.05 and 1.0.
