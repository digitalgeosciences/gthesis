# Gelstrength

**Summary**: The ability of a fluid to suspend solids when circulation is stopped.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014]] measured gel strength to evaluate the suspension capabilities of the drilling muds.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014]] Crucial for ensuring that solids remain suspended in the wellbore during static periods.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014]] Measured at 3rpm after 10 seconds and 10 minutes of rest.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- For the SoluSeal system, 1wt% gave better results for gel strength than 8wt%. ([[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014, p. 125]])
