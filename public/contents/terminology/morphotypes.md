# Morphotypes

**Summary**: Classification of foraminifera based on shape and size to interpret environmental conditions.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-septriandi|Septriandi, 2016]] analyzed foraminiferal morphotypes to determine water depth and salinity.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-septriandi|Septriandi, 2016]] Used to interpret environmental parameters like depth and salinity.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-septriandi|Septriandi, 2016]] Categorization based on shape (e.g., planispiral, milioline).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Morphotype C1 (Miliolid-Quinqueloculina-Triloculina) was the most dominant, accounting for 40%. ([[2016-septriandi|Septriandi, 2016, p. 107]])
