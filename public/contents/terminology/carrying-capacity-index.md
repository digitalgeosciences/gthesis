# Carrying Capacity Index

**Summary**: An empirical index used to predict hole cleaning efficiency in vertical well sections.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017]] utilized the carrying capacity index (CCI) to evaluate the effectiveness of drilling mud.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017]] Used to assess the impact of mud rheology on cuttings transport.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017]] Calculated from Mud Weight, Annular Velocity, and Consistency Index.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- A CCI value greater than 1 indicates good hole cleaning; values below 0.5 indicate poor transport and potentially rounded cuttings. ([[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017, p. 110]])
