# Retrograde Gas Condensate

**Summary**: A reservoir containing a single-phase fluid that drops liquid (condensate) when pressure falls below the dew point.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-baarimah|BAARIMAH, 2012]] studied retrograde gas-condensate reservoirs to evaluate well performance under varying parameters.

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-baarimah|BAARIMAH, 2012]] Requires specific modeling to account for the complex thermodynamics of multi-component fluids.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2012-baarimah|BAARIMAH, 2012]] Characterized via PVT analysis and compositional simulation.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Retrograde systems exhibit three distinct flow regions based on local pressure relative to the dew point. ([[2012-baarimah|BAARIMAH, 2012, p. 21]])
