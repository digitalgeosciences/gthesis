# Vitrinite Reflectance

**Summary**: A measure of the thermal maturity of organic matter based on the reflectivity of vitrinite or other macerals.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-mohammed|Mohammed, 2017]] calculated Vitrinite Reflectance Equivalent (VRe) to determine the maturation stage of the source rocks.

- [[2020-usman|Usman, 2020]] evaluated thermal maturity using Tmax and biomarker ratios.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-mohammed|Mohammed, 2017]] VRo is the standard metric for determining if a rock is in the oil or gas window.

- [[2020-usman|Usman, 2020]] evaluated thermal maturity using Tmax and biomarker ratios.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-mohammed|Mohammed, 2017]] Measured via microscopy on core samples.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Tuwaiq Mountain VRe values ranged from 1.0% in Well-A to 1.55% in Well-E. ([[2017-mohammed|Mohammed, 2017, p. 64]])


- Samples with TOC > 2% showed VRo values between 0.9 and 1.2%, indicating a mature oil to wet-gas window. ([[2020-usman|Usman, 2020, p. 54]])
