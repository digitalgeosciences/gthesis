# Saturation Exponent

**Summary**: A parameter in Archie's equation describing the change in electrical tortuosity with changes in water content.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-adebayo|Adebayo, 2015]] analyzed how saturation exponent changed significantly with time during CO2 storage.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-adebayo|Adebayo, 2015]] Used to evaluate how fluid saturation affects rock resistivity; its variation can indicate chemical or physical changes in the rock matrix.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-adebayo|Adebayo, 2015]] Derived from plotting resistivity index against water saturation.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Saturation exponent changed significantly with time during CO2 storage and that the extent of change is dependent on the extent of CO2/brine/rock interaction (CBRI). ([[2015-adebayo|Adebayo, 2015, p. 19]])
