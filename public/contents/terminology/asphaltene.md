## Key Findings Across Sources

- Asphaltene content is a significant crude oil component causing wettibility alteration to intermediate or oil-wet. ([[2019-al-garadi|AL-GARADI, 2019, p. 31]])


- Asphaltenes were found to have condensed aromatic cores of 8-9 rings with varying sulfur content. ([[2021-alqam|ALQAM, 2021, p. 101]])

- Asphaltene was more prone to elongate in the direction perpendicular to the force, resulting in a higher Poisson's ratio (0.47) compared to kerogen (0.42). ([[2021-aslanov|Aslanov, 2021, p. 56]])
