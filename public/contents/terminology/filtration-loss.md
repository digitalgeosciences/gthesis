# Filtration Loss

**Summary**: The loss of drilling fluid into the surrounding formation during drilling operations.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-wajheeuddin|Wajheeuddin, 2014]] measured filtration loss to determine the effectiveness of date seeds and grass ash as additives.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-wajheeuddin|Wajheeuddin, 2014]] Critical for preventing formation damage and ensuring borehole stability.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-wajheeuddin|Wajheeuddin, 2014]] measured using an API filter press at 100 psi.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The selection of optimal concentration was based exclusively on filtration characteristics (least filtrate loss). ([[2014-wajheeuddin|Wajheeuddin, 2014, p. 78]])
