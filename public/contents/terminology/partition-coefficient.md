# Partition Coefficient

**Summary**: The ratio of the concentration of a substance in one phase to its concentration in another.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1987-fuseni|Fuseni, 1987]] measured partition coefficients to determine the distribution of surfactants between oil and brine.

## Scientific Context

- [[1987-fuseni|Fuseni, 1987]] Partition coefficients indicate the degree of surfactant's interaction with different phases.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1987-fuseni|Fuseni, 1987]] determined via two-phase titration.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- A unit partition coefficient corresponds to an optimal salinity where the aqueous phase is nearly devoid of surfactant. ([[1987-fuseni|Fuseni, 1987, p. 58]])
