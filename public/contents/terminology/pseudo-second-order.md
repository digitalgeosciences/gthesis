# Pseudo Second Order

**Summary**: A kinetic model used to describe the rate of adsorption, often indicating a chemically-mediated process.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-musa|Musa, 2015]] applied the pseudo-second order model to characterize the kinetics of metal ion adsorption.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-musa|Musa, 2015]] Used to determine if the adsorption process is governed by chemical bonding or physical forces.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods
