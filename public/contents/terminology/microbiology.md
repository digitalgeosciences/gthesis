# Microbiology

**Summary**: The study of microscopic organisms, such as bacteria, in the environment.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-bello|Bello, 2015]] conducted a microbiological investigation to understand the roles played by bacteria in the growing media and within plant tissues.

- [[2015-yusuf|Yusuf, 2015]] applied microbiology to assess total coliforms and heterotrophic plate counts in coastal waters.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-bello|Bello, 2015]] Microorganisms can play a significant role in the degradation of organic pollutants and the cycling of nutrients.

- [[2015-yusuf|Yusuf, 2015]] applied microbiology to assess total coliforms and heterotrophic plate counts in coastal waters.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods
