# Multiples

**Summary**: Secondary reflections that can obscure primary seismic signals.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2020-alzahrani|Alzahrani, 2020]] identified multiples in the Mobil dataset.

## Scientific Context

- [[2020-alzahrani|Alzahrani, 2020]] Common in marine environments, they can complicate velocity analysis.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2020-alzahrani|Alzahrani, 2020]] Identified as repeating patterns in the seismic trace.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The Mobil data were contaminated with water-bottom multiples; however, we still observed the change in the hyperbolic events to linear events after squaring. ([[2020-alzahrani|Alzahrani, 2020, p. 44]])
