# Pressure Drop

**Summary**: The loss of pressure in a fluid as it flows through a pipe or other system.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2018-al-dogail|Al-Dogail, 2018]] evaluated the impact of density, viscosity, and surface tension on pressure drop.

## Scientific Context

- [[2018-al-dogail|Al-Dogail, 2018]] Accurate prediction of pressure drop is essential for designing production systems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2018-al-dogail|Al-Dogail, 2018]] Measured using differential pressure gauges.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Density had the most significant effect on pressure drop (up to 5.2 psi), while surface tension had no measurable effect on pressure drop. ([[2018-al-dogail|Al-Dogail, 2018, p. 64]])
