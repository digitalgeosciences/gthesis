# Total Inorganic Carbon

**Summary**: The sum of all inorganic carbon species in a water sample.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2025-alghawi|Alghawi, 2025]] measured TIC to evaluate the amount of dissolved CO2.

## Scientific Context

- [[2025-alghawi|Alghawi, 2025]] TIC is used as an indicator for CO2 solubility and uptake.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

## Key Findings Across Sources
