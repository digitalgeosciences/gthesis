# Visco-Acoustic

**Summary**: A model of the earth that accounts for both elasticity and viscosity, leading to wave attenuation and dispersion.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-mohammed|Mohammed, 2015]] modeled the subsurface as a visco-acoustic medium to account for realistic energy losses.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-mohammed|Mohammed, 2015]] Essential for accurate imaging in deep or complex geological settings where high frequencies are attenuated.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-mohammed|Mohammed, 2015]] Represented via a complex velocity term and the quality factor (Q).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Incorporating the Q-factor into the velocity model allows for the compensation of absorption in the PSPI algorithm. ([[2015-mohammed|Mohammed, 2015, p. 62]])
