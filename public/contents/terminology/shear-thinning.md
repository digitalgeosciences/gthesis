# Shear Thinning

**Summary**: A decrease in viscosity with an increase in shear rate.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-omer|Omer, 2012]] observed shear-thinning behavior in all tested PAM concentrations and counterion mixtures.

- [[2014-azad|Azad, 2014]] evaluated the shear-thinning behavior of VES and polymer to assess their suitability for improving injectivity.

- [[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014]] characterized the shear-thinning behavior of HPAM and Alcomer130 to ensure flowability through drill bit nozzles.

- [[2016-sidaoui|Sidaoui, 2016]] characterized both diesel and waste oil emulsified acids as shear-thinning fluids.

- [[2023-al-dogail|AL-DOGAIL, 2023]] identified the non-Newtonian, shear-thinning behavior of both 15% and 20% HCl-PEAS.

- [[2023-dafaalla|Dafaalla, 2023]] analyzed the shear-thinning behavior of xanthan gum and AN-125 SH.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The reduction of dynamic viscosity was attributed to the role of decreasing PAM concentration, molecular structure, and network behavior. ([[2012-omer|Omer, 2012, p. 59]])


- The study found that VES exhibits a higher shear thinning potential than polymer, which is critical for mobility control in tight carbonate reservoirs. ([[2014-azad|Azad, 2014, p. 161]])

- Both HPAM and Alcomer130 were confirmed to be shear thinning, with significant viscosity drops observed at high shear rates. ([[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014, p. 48]])

- The flow behavior index (n) was less than 1 for both systems, confirming their classification as shear-thinning. ([[2016-sidaoui|Sidaoui, 2016, p. 39]])

- The reduction in pressure drop with increasing flow rates confirmed the shear-thinning nature of the system. ([[2023-al-dogail|AL-DOGAIL, 2023, p. 183]])

- Xanthan gum showed higher recovery at 4 ft/day, which is within the shear thinning regime. ([[2023-dafaalla|Dafaalla, 2023, p. 115]])

- All emulsion systems exhibited pseudoplastic fluid characteristics, with a significant reduction in viscosity at 70 and 90°C. ([[2024-abdallah|Abdallah, 2024, p. 66]])

- The formulation showed a shear-thinning behavior, where the viscosity decreased as the shear rate increased. ([[2023-othman|Othman, 2023, p. 80]])
