# Rock Dissolution

**Summary**: The chemical process where minerals in the rock matrix dissolve into the surrounding fluid.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2013]] evaluated rock-dissolution as a mechanism for increasing oil recovery by altering surface wettability.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2013]] A key mechanism for improving oil mobility by altering surface properties.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2013-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2013]] Determined by comparing ion concentrations in injected vs. produced fluids.

## Key Findings Across Sources
