# Wellbore Erosion

**Summary**: The physical degradation of the wellbore wall due to high-velocity fluid and solid particle interaction.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] studied the impact of wellbore erosion on production in horizontal wells.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] Critical for understanding long-term production performance in weakly consolidated formations.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] Modeled as a function of wall shear stress vs. critical shear stress.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Wellbores are susceptible to erosion from hydro-mechanical forces causing surface erosion, leading to variation of borehole diameter. ([[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019, p. 12]])
