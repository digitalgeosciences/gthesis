# Local Optimization

**Summary**: An optimization approach that finds a solution by moving iteratively from a starting point in a limited search space.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-edigbue|Edigbue, 2023]] compared local optimization to global methods for joint inversion of DCR and SR data.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-edigbue|Edigbue, 2023]] Local methods are efficient but can get stuck in local minima, which is problematic for non-linear geophysical problems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-edigbue|Edigbue, 2023]] Iteratively adjust parameters until the best fit between modeled and observed data is achieved.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The combined optimization approach outperforms both the local and just global optimization methods in terms of percentage of anomaly reconstruction. ([[2023-edigbue|Edigbue, 2023, p. 16]])
