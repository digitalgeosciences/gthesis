# Toc

**Summary**: The mass percentage of carbon atoms in organic compounds within a rock.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2022-septriandi|Septriandi, 2022]] measured TOC to evaluate the source rock potential of the Jubaila Formation.

- [[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023]] applied TOC analysis using LECO instruments for unpreserved sample comparison.

- [[2024-almutawa|AlMutawa, 2024]] used TOC analysis to quantify the degradation of total organic carbon (TOC) in contaminated soil samples.

- [[2025-alhubail|Alhubail, 2025]] performed TOC analysis to quantify the amount of organic matter in the carbonate mudrocks.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- TOC values in the Lower Jubaila (J1) range from 0.5% to 5%, with an average of 1.5%. ([[2022-septriandi|Septriandi, 2022, p. 51]])

- TOC values showed minor changes (0.13-0.94%) over time cycles of up to 5 months in Core 3. ([[2023-al-eid|Al-Eid, 2023, p. 74]])

- The study achieved an average efficiency of 38% for the reduction of TOC using indigenous bacterial strains. ([[2024-almutawa|AlMutawa, 2024, p. 12]])

- Established the baseline organic richness for each sample type. ([[2025-alhubail|Alhubail, 2025, p. 43]])
