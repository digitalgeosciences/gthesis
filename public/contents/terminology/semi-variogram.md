# Semi Variogram

**Summary**: A tool to describe the spatial or temporal correlation between nearby data samples as a function of distance.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2002-al-ali|Al-Ali, 2002]] used semi-variogram modeling to determine parameters like nugget, sill, and range.

## Scientific Context

- [[2002-al-ali|Al-Ali, 2002]] Essential for determining spatial correlation in geostatistical modeling.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2002-al-ali|Al-Ali, 2002]] Quantified as a function of the separation vector.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The semi-variogram typically increases as distance increases until it reaches a plateau (sill). ([[2002-al-ali|Al-Ali, 2002, p. 51]])
