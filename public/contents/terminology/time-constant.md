# Time Constant

**Summary**: A parameter representing the time delay of the injection rate affecting the producer.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-sadeed|Sadeed, 2023]] calculated the time constant for each producer in the CRM model.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-sadeed|Sadeed, 2023]] The time constant is a key CRM parameter reflecting reservoir storage and flow capacity.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-sadeed|Sadeed, 2023]] Derived from history matching of production data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The time constant provides the response time from injectors toward the producer, and it is generally affected by the inter-well distance, fluid properties and the producer productivity index. ([[2023-sadeed|Sadeed, 2023, p. 23]])
