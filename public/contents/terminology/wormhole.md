# Wormhole

**Summary**: Channels created in carbonate rocks by acid treatment to improve permeability.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-al-garni|Al-Garni, 2023]] investigated the sustainability of wormholes under high stress in carbonate samples.

- [[2023-aldhafeeri|Aldhafeeri, 2023]] investigated wormhole generation and its impact on mechanical properties.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-al-garni|Al-Garni, 2023]] Wormholes are a primary mechanism for enhancing flow in low-permeability carbonates.

- [[2023-aldhafeeri|Aldhafeeri, 2023]] investigated wormhole generation and its impact on mechanical properties.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-al-garni|Al-Garni, 2023]] Visualized via CT scanning and modeled using FEM.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Smaller, branched wormholes were found to be more vulnerable to strain than larger ones. ([[2023-al-garni|Al-Garni, 2023, p. 47]])

- Live brine injection generated wormholes in all samples, increasing porosity and permeability. ([[2023-aldhafeeri|Aldhafeeri, 2023, p. 14]])
