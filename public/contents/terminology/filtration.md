# Filtration

**Summary**: The process of liquid passing through a porous medium or filter cake.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The filtered volume was measured to ensure it did not exceed 15 ml as per API standards. ([[2014-elhag|ELHAG, 2014, p. 49]])


- The addition of 300 micron date seeds resulted in a 20% reduction in water loss at 1.5 ppb. ([[2014-wajheeuddin|Wajheeuddin, 2014, p. 81]])

- Combining different sizes of calcium carbonate as bridging agents reduced the difference in filtrate between raw and micronized barite to ~1 cc. ([[2017-mohamed|MOHAMED, 2017, p. 76]])

- Nanosilica was found to be more effective than calcium carbonate for surface plugging in certain contexts. ([[2017-alakbari|Alakbari, 2017, p. 20]])

- Two types of filtration were identified: static (when circulation is stopped) and dynamic (during active circulation). ([[2017-jafarov|Jafarov, 2017, p. 28]])

- The addition of OCs resulted in improved filtration properties of the drilling fluid. ([[2024-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2024, p. 16]])
