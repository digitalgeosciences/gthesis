# Pseudocritical Properties

**Summary**: Properties of a mixture at the pseudocritical point, used to characterize gas-condensate behavior.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2001-humoud|Humoud, 2001]] developed new correlations for pseudocritical-properties when fluid composition was unknown.

## Scientific Context

- [[2001-humoud|Humoud, 2001]] Used to estimate dewpoint pressure when full fluid composition is not available.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2001-humoud|Humoud, 2001]] Calculated via Kay's method or empirical correlations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- New correlation equations (5.8 and 5.9) reduced average absolute errors in predicting pseudocritical temperature and pressure to 1.8% and 1% respectively. ([[2001-humoud|Humoud, 2001, p. 85]])
