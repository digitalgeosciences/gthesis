# Multiphase Sinusoidal Oscillator

**Summary**: An electronic circuit that generates multiple sinusoidal signals with specific phase offsets.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1997-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 1997]] developed a new active-C MPSO using CCII to produce even or odd numbers of phases.

## Scientific Context

- [[1997-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 1997]] Used in power electronics and signal processing to generate balanced three-phase or multi-phase systems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1997-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 1997]] Analyzed via transfer functions and Pspice simulation.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The proposed MPSO circuits can produce both even and odd numbers of phases (e.g., 3-phase and 6-phase) using fewer active and passive elements than previously reported designs. ([[1997-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 1997, p. 80]])
