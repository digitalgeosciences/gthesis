# Pore Size Distribution

**Summary**: The distribution of pore sizes within a rock, often determined by NMR or MICP.

---

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Pores were classified into D1 (>100ms), D2 (50-100ms), and D3 (<50ms) based on T2 relaxation times. ([[2020-islam|Islam, 2020, p. 111]])

- Bulk relaxation effects in permeable formations led to an apparent increase in the signal from micropores due to overlapping relaxation contributions. ([[2024-akomolafe|Akomolafe, 2024, p. 82]])

- Pore sizes were found to range between 14 and 30 µm. ([[2025-almousa|Almousa, 2025, p. 41]])
