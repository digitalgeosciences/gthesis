## Key Findings Across Sources

- SEM imaging at 1 µm resolution allowed for clear identification of grain and pore sizes in both treated and untreated zones. ([[2013-khan|Khan, 2013, p. 107]])


- NMR was able to accurately analyze, in terms of pore size distribution, different characteristics of the wormholing process. ([[2014-al-duailej|Al-Duailej, 2014, p. 13]])

- Firing at 1000°C caused a reduction in the space between clay particles, altering the pore geometry. ([[2015-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2015, p. 43]])

- Particles larger than 100 nm could result in plugging of some pore channels. ([[2016-alhamad|AlHamad, 2016, p. 51]])

- Average pore size in Pink Desert limestone was 50-300 microns (up to 700-micron pores). ([[2016-elnaeem|Elnaeem, 2016, p. 44]])

- Both CCB_20 and FA_20 systems exhibited similar pore size distributions, with only a 3.51% difference in total porosity. ([[2022-adjei|Adjei, 2022, p. 137]])

- Optimum interstitial velocity decreases as pore radius size increases; larger pores result in thicker, less branched wormholes. ([[2022-alkathim|Alkathim, 2022, p. 61]])

- Micropores in kerogen showed a unimodal distribution with a lognormal profile, with most frequent radii between 2.7 and 3.5 Angstroms. ([[2023-afagwu|AFAGWU, 2023, p. 176]])

- T2 measurement is directly proportional to surface relaxation decay and can be linked to the size of the pores. ([[2023-al-boghail|AL-BOGHAIL, 2023, p. 40]])

- Carbonate samples were characterized by two distinct peaks representing macro- and micro-pore systems. ([[2024-suleiman-2|Suleiman, 2024, p. 60]])

- Before treatment, the core manifested a bi-modal pore size distribution. ([[2024-suleiman-4|Suleiman, 2024, p. 78]])

- T2 relaxation data provided insights into the pore size distribution and brine mobility. ([[2024-jameel|Jameel, 2024, p. 84]])

- Pore size distribution was analyzed for each rock type using segmented images from the watershed algorithm. ([[2024-jibrin|Jibrin, 2024, p. 39]])

- Approximately 80% of pore throats in Berea and Bentheimer samples were between 10 and 60 $\mu$m. ([[1991-ali|Ali, 1991, p. 53]])

- The pore size distribution index (A) was calculated as 0.73. ([[1999-fawumi|Fawumi, 1999, p. 56]])
