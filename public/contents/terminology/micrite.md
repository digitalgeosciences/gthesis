# Micrite

**Summary**: Fine-grained carbonate mud (less than 4 microns).

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1992-abdulghani|Abdulghani, 1992]] identified micrite in the Pseudocyclammina/Kurnubia microfacies.

## Scientific Context

- [[1992-abdulghani|Abdulghani, 1992]] Micrite content affects the primary porosity of the rock.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1992-abdulghani|Abdulghani, 1992]] measured via SEM and thin section.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Micrite crystals ranged between 1 to 4 microns, and were anhedral to subhedral in shape. ([[1992-abdulghani|Abdulghani, 1992, p. 106]])
