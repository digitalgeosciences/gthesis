# Ph Zero Point Of Charge

**Summary**: The pH value at which the net surface charge of an adsorbent is zero.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2018-adeyemi|Adeyemi, 2018]] determined the pHzpc to understand the influence of solution pH on adsorption.

## Scientific Context

- [[2018-adeyemi|Adeyemi, 2018]] Used to determine the optimal pH for adsorption based on electrostatic interactions.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2018-adeyemi|Adeyemi, 2018]] Batch equilibrium technique using a known volume of 0.1mol/m3 KNO3.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The pHzpc for the SDAC was found to be 7.16. ([[2018-adeyemi|Adeyemi, 2018, p. 82]])
