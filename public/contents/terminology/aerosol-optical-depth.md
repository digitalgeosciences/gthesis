# Aerosol Optical Depth

**Summary**: A measure of how much light is blocked by aerosols in the atmosphere.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019]] used aerosol optical depth to quantify atmospheric particulate matter concentration.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019]] AOD is an effective tool to estimate quantities of pollution in the atmosphere.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019]] measured via ground-based sun-photometry and satellite systems.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- AOD values greater than one indicate high aerosol loadings and severe pollution. ([[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019, p. 38]])
