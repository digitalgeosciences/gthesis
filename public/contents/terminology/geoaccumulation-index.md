# Geoaccumulation Index

**Summary**: An index used to evaluate the degree of soil contamination by heavy metals.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2022-al-harbi|Al-Harbi, 2022]] calculated the Geoaccumulation Index (Igeo) to distinguish between natural background levels and anthropogenic pollution.

## Scientific Context

- [[2022-al-harbi|Al-Harbi, 2022]] used to determine if the site required remediation.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2022-al-harbi|Al-Harbi, 2022]] calculated using mean and maximum concentrations from ICP-MS data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Most metals at both sites were classified as unpolluted, with some exceptions for Cu and Cd in Hafer Al-Batin. ([[2022-al-harbi|Al-Harbi, 2022, p. 55]])
