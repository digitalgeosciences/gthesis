# Mechanical Parameters

**Summary**: Physical measurements taken at the surface during drilling operations.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-ahmed|Ahmed, 2019]] used mechanical parameters (ROP, RPM, SPP, Torque, WOB) as inputs for AI models.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-ahmed|Ahmed, 2019]] Used as real-time indicators for identifying hazardous zones.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-ahmed|Ahmed, 2019]] Measured via sensors on the drill string and surface equipment.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Six mechanical parameters were sufficient to predict loss zones with high accuracy. ([[2019-ahmed|Ahmed, 2019, p. 20]])
