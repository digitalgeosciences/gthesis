# Paleoproductivity

**Summary**: The rate at which organic matter is produced by primary producers in a marine environment.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018]] evaluated paleoproductivity using P/Ti, Ba/Al, Ni/Al, and Zn/Al ratios.

## Scientific Context

- [[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018]] Used to identify periods of high nutrient availability and planktonic blooms.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018]] Analysis of elements like Phosphorus (P) and Barium (Ba) normalized against Titanium (Ti) or Aluminum (Al).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Highest paleoproductivity occurred during the deposition of the Mdg lithofacies and upper Mzr lithofacies. ([[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018, p. 100]])
