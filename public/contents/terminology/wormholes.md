# Wormholes

**Summary**: Channels created by acid dissolution in carbonate rocks, improving permeability.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-alawi|ALAWI, 2019]] monitored the growth of wormholes during core flood experiments.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-alawi|ALAWI, 2019]] The primary goal of matrix acidizing in carbonate reservoirs.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-alawi|ALAWI, 2019]] Observed via CT scan and calculated through pore volume tracking.

## Key Findings Across Sources
