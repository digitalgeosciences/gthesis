# Wormholes

**Summary**: Small, continuous tubes formed in carbonate rocks by acid dissolution.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-al-garni|Al-Garni, 2023]] investigated the sustainability of wormholes under high confining stress.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-al-garni|Al-Garni, 2023]] Wormholes are a primary mechanism for enhancing permeability during matrix acidizing.
