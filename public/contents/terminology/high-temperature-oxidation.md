# High Temperature Oxidation

**Summary**: Oxidation of crude oil at high temperatures, producing carbon oxides.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1999-iddris|Iddris, 1999]] distinguished HTO from LTO to understand different oxidation regimes.

## Scientific Context

- [[1999-iddris|Iddris, 1999]] Produces carbon oxides and no residue; occurs at higher temperatures than LTO.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1999-iddris|Iddris, 1999]] Measured via TGA/DTA analysis.

## Key Findings Across Sources
