# Oolitic Shoal

**Summary**: A carbonate deposit characterized by ooids formed in high-energy environments.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2021-alabyadh|Alabyadh, 2021]] studied oolitic shoal deposits to determine their depositional setting and diagenetic history.

## Scientific Context

- [[2021-alabyadh|Alabyadh, 2021]] Oolitic shoals are key targets for reservoir characterization due to their high energy and potential porosity.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2021-alabyadh|Alabyadh, 2021]] Identified by the presence of ooids and associated sedimentary structures.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Oolitic grainstones and packstones indicate high energy shoals, with some being storm or washover fan deposits. ([[2021-alabyadh|Alabyadh, 2021, p. 100]])
