# Provenance

**Summary**: The origin or source area of sedimentary deposits.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1999-babalola|Babalola, 1999]] investigated provenance using heavy mineral and trace element chemistry.

## Scientific Context

- [[1999-babalola|Babalola, 1999]] Determining the source area helps reconstruct paleogeography and tectonic settings.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1999-babalola|Babalola, 1999]] Analyzed via heavy mineral suites and trace element ratios.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Heavy minerals indicate an acidic to intermediate source terrain; specifically, high SiO2, Al2O3, and Fe203/TiO2 combined with low P2O5, CaO, MgO, TiO2, Cr, Co, Ni, and V suggest a dominant intermediate to felsic source. ([[1999-babalola|Babalola, 1999, p. 18]])
