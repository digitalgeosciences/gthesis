# Short Offset

**Summary**: The assumption that seismic waves travel over a short distance relative to the depth of investigation, simplifying velocity calculations.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2018-abdulrahman|Abdulrahman, 2018]] evaluated the impact of the short-offset assumption on velocity determination.

## Scientific Context

- [[2018-abdulrahman|Abdulrahman, 2018]] Used to simplify NMO and RMS velocity calculations in seismic processing.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods
