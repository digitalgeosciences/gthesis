# Nugget Effect

**Summary**: The portion of the variogram that is attributed to random noise or micro-scale variability.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1987-abdul-latif|Abdul-Latif, 1987]] determined nugget effects in the variograms for various deposit parameters.

## Scientific Context

- [[1987-abdul-latif|Abdul-Latif, 1987]] Indicates the level of random noise or small-scale variability.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1987-abdul-latif|Abdul-Latif, 1987]] Determined by fitting mathematical models to experimental data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- All fitted models showed zero nugget variances, indicating high continuity near the origin. ([[1987-abdul-latif|Abdul-Latif, 1987, p. 85]])
