# Reservoir Rock Types

**Summary**: A grouping of lithofacies with similar petrophysical properties.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2020-al-zoukani|Al-Zoukani, 2020]] defined 14 reservoir rock types (RRTs) by integrating geological and petrophysical data.

## Scientific Context

- [[2020-al-zoukani|Al-Zoukani, 2020]] RRTs are essential for modeling and simulation to identify flow units within a reservoir.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2020-al-zoukani|Al-Zoukani, 2020]] Grouped based on shared petrophysical properties like porosity, permeability, and pore throat size.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Integration of texture-based classification with MICP data resulted in the identification of 14 Reservoir Rock Types (RRTs). ([[2020-al-zoukani|Al-Zoukani, 2020, p. 20]])
