# Reservoir Rock Types

**Summary**: A classification of rocks based on similar petrophysical properties like porosity, permeability, and pore geometry.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2020-al-zoukani|Al-Zoukani, 2020]] identified Reservoir Rock Types (RRTs) by grouping D5-D6 samples with similar characteristics.

## Scientific Context

- [[2020-al-zoukani|Al-Zoukani, 2020]] RRTs are used to bridge the gap between geological description and reservoir performance.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2020-al-zoukani|Al-Zoukani, 2020]] Grouping based on both depositional texture and diagenetic features.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- RRTs are essential for identifying flow units within a reservoir to aid in modeling and simulation. ([[2020-al-zoukani|Al-Zoukani, 2020, p. 38]])
