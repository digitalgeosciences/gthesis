# Thalassinoides

**Summary**: A type of burrowing trace fossil characterized by large, branching tubes.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-saraih|Saraih, 2024]] identified Thalassinoides burrows as the primary source of super-permeability in the study area.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-saraih|Saraih, 2024]] Thalassinoides burrows can create three-dimensional permeability pathways.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-saraih|Saraih, 2024]] identified via petrography and CT scanning.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The Khanasir Member contains burrowed strata that serve as an analog to bioturbated carbonate reservoirs with large-size burrows (Thalassinoides). ([[2024-saraih|Saraih, 2024, p. 38]])
