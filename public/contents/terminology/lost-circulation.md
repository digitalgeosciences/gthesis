# Lost Circulation

**Summary**: The loss of drilling or cementing fluids into permeable formations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Fiber systems were shown to plug fractures and reduce non-productive time (NPT). ([[2017-ahmed|Ahmed, 2017, p. 53]])


- Lost circulation accounts for 25-40% of total drilling costs. ([[2019-ahmed|Ahmed, 2019, p. 19]])

- Classified into seepage, partial, severe, and complete based on flow rates (e.g., 50-150 bbl/hr for severe). ([[2021-alsaihati|Alsaihati, 2021, p. 20]])

- Lost circulation can cost the industry approximately $2 to $4 billion annually. ([[2022-adjei|Adjei, 2022, p. 37]])
