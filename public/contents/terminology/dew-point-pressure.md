# Dew Point Pressure

**Summary**: The pressure at which the first drop of liquid condenses from a gas.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2010-al-dhamen|Al-Dhamen, 2010]] estimated the dew-point pressure (Pd) as a function of GOR, temperature, and specific gravities.

## Scientific Context

- [[2010-al-dhamen|Al-Dhamen, 2010]] Critical for determining the flow behavior and phase behavior of gas condensate fluids in reservoirs.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

## Key Findings Across Sources
