## Key Findings Across Sources

- Reaction rates were found to be mass transfer limited at low rotation speeds and surface reaction limited at high rotation speeds for Silurian dolomite. ([[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014, p. 123]])


- Toluene removal by UV/H2O2 followed first-order kinetics with a rate constant k = 0.3593. ([[2016-sameh|Sameh, 2016, p. 92]])

- Determined a specific rate equation for the NH4Cl/NaNO2 system: dc/dt = -7.66 x 10^11 Co^2.45 e^(-91.44 kJ/mol)/RT. ([[2024-abdallah|Abdallah, 2024, p. 50]])

- Growth rates were found to be influenced by concentration, temperature, and pressure. ([[2024-habiburrahman|HABIBURRAHMAN, 2024, p. 36]])

- Temperature is the most important factor influencing reaction kinetics. ([[2004-ahmed|Ahmed, 2004, p. 18]])
