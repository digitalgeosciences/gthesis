# Gas Oil Transition Zone

**Summary**: The zone where both gas and oil are present.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010]] calculated the Gas Oil Transition Zone (GOTZ) to determine the thickness of the gas cap.

## Scientific Context

- [[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010]] The vertical thickness between Free Oil Level and Gas Oil Contact.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010]] Determined from log analysis (resistivity, density, neutron).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- In low permeability zones, the GOTZ can be several tens or hundreds of feet thick. ([[2010-alroudhan|AlRoudhan, 2010, p. 74]])
