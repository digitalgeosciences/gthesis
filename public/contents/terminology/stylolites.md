# Stylolites

**Summary**: Pressure solution features formed during burial and diagenesis.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-saleh|Saleh, 2016]] analyzed stylolites to determine their formation history and relationship with lithology.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-saleh|Saleh, 2016]] Stylolites indicate the intensity of compaction and diagenesis.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-saleh|Saleh, 2016]] identified via borehole imaging and gamma-ray logs.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Bedding-parallel stylolites were more common in the limestone and dolomitic limestone lithologies. ([[2016-saleh|Saleh, 2016, p. 123]])
