# Paleosols

**Summary**: Fossil soils that indicate periods of non-deposition or subaerial exposure.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-ali|Ali, 2015]] identified paleosols as key indicators for sequence boundaries in the Dam Formation.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-ali|Ali, 2015]] Paleosols mark periods of non-deposition and are used to identify sequence boundaries.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-ali|Ali, 2015]] Identification of rootlets, mudcracks, and specific mineralogy (palygorskite).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Paleosols are used to identify unconformities within the Dam Formation. ([[2015-ali|Ali, 2015, p. 87]])
