**Summary**: A mathematical method to calculate interval velocity from stacking velocities.

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

## Scientific Context

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2018-abdulrahman|Abdulrahman, 2018]] Applied to stacking velocities from both long and short offset cases.

## Key Findings Across Sources
