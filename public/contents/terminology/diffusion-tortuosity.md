# Diffusion Tortuosity

**Summary**: A measure of the complexity of the path taken by a molecule as it moves through a porous medium.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-afagwu|AFAGWU, 2023]] evaluated the diffusion tortuosity to separate structural effects from adsorption effects.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-afagwu|AFAGWU, 2023]] Determines the effective permeability and transport rates in tight pores.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-afagwu|AFAGWU, 2023]] Calculated from the ratio of transition diffusion to actual diffusion.
