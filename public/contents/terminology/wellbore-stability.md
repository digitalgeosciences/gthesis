# Wellbore Stability

**Summary**: The ability of a wellbore to remain open during drilling.

---

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Wellbore instability manifests as hole pack off, overpull, tight spot, stuck pipe, and lost circulation. ([[2014-al-haidary|Al-Haidary, 2014, p. 19]])


- Depletion causes changes in mud weight windows, potentially leading to wellbore collapse or lost circulation. ([[2018-aldossary|AlDossary, 2018, p. 16]])

- Wellbore instability was primarily manifested as severe loss circulation in the carbonate reservoir. ([[2019-al-nutaifi|Al-Nutaifi, 2019, p. 59]])

- Losses can lead to wellbore instability, increasing time and cost. ([[2021-alsaihati|Alsaihati, 2021, p. 14]])

- Wellbore instability issues cause extra drilling costs about 10 to 15%. ([[2021-serag|SERAG, 2021, p. 17]])
