# Oil Swelling

**Summary**: The increase in volume of crude oil due to the dissolution of gas (e.g., CO2).

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-shakeel|SHAKEEL, 2014]] evaluated oil-swelling as a key mechanism for improving mobility and recovery.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-shakeel|SHAKEEL, 2014]] Swelling reduces the viscosity of the oil and improves its mobility in the reservoir.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-shakeel|SHAKEEL, 2014]] calculated based on CO2 solubility data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Oil swelling is significant even at low pressures, especially with heavy crude oils. ([[2014-shakeel|SHAKEEL, 2014, p. 69]])
