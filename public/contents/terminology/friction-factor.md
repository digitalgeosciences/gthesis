# Friction Factor

**Summary**: A dimensionless number used to describe the resistance of a pipe or wellbore to fluid flow.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] monitored friction factor reduction due to sand surface roughness changes.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] It determines the pressure drop and velocity within the wellbore.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] Calculated using Darcy-Weisbach or similar formulas.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The friction factor shows a continuous reduction as sand is eroded and the surface becomes smoother. ([[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019, p. 65]])
- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] Not specified as a range, but shown to decrease over time.
