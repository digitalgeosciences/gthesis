# Dissolution Rate

**Summary**: The rate at which a solid mineral is dissolved by an acid solution.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014]] calculated dissolution rates to understand the chemical reaction between HCl and dolomite.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014]] Quantifies the chemical reaction speed between acid and rock.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014]] Derived from weight loss measurements over time.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Dissolution rates were found to be higher in samples with higher porosity (17%) compared to those with lower porosity (10%). ([[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014, p. 153]])
