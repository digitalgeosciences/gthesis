# Tilted Transverse Isotropy

**Summary**: A type of anisotropy where the symmetry axis is tilted relative to the vertical.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-makki|Makki, 2024]] modeled the sand dune as a TTI medium due to its dipping cross-strata.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-makki|Makki, 2024]] TTI models are necessary for dunes with dipping internal structures to correctly interpret seismic data.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-makki|Makki, 2024]] Determined by fitting observed velocities to a TTI model.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The best-fit values for epsilon and delta were 0.035 and 0.005, confirming TTI characteristics. ([[2024-makki|Makki, 2024, p. 13]])
