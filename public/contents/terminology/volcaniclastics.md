# Volcaniclastics

**Summary**: Sedimentary rocks or sediments composed of fragments of volcanic origin.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2022-argadestya|Argadestya, 2022]] studied the mineralogical and elemental variability of Merapi volcaniclastics.

## Scientific Context

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

## Key Findings Across Sources
