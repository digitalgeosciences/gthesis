# Hydroponic System

**Summary**: A method of growing plants in nutrient-rich water instead of soil.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-bello|Bello, 2015]] used a hydroponic system to evaluate the phytoremediation potential of selected plants.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-bello|Bello, 2015]] Hydroponics allows for the isolation of plant performance from soil chemistry variables.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-bello|Bello, 2015]] Utilized a Deep Water Culture (DWC) setup with air pumps and nutrient solutions.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The use of a hydroponic system allowed for controlled assessment of plant performance on specific contaminants. ([[2015-bello|Bello, 2015, p. 45]])
