# Disinfection By-Products

**Summary**: Chemical compounds formed when water is treated with disinfectants like chlorine or ozone.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2011-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2011]] investigated the formation of DBPs in blended drinking water.

## Scientific Context

- [[2011-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2011]] DBPs are a major concern for human health in drinking water systems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2011-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2011]] Quantified via GC-MS and Ion Chromatography.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Brominated DBPs are more toxic than their chlorinated analogs. ([[2011-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2011, p. 25]])
