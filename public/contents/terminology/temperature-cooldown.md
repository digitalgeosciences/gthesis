# Temperature Cooldown

**Summary**: The reduction in temperature of the injected fluid and surrounding rock during treatment.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-alabdrabalnabi|Alabdrabalnabi, 2024]] evaluated how temperature cooldown affects the gelation time of the nanosilica system.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-alabdrabalnabi|Alabdrabalnabi, 2024]] Critical for determining if the gel will set before or after reaching its target.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-alabdrabalnabi|Alabdrabalnabi, 2024]] Calculated using heat transfer equations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- A drop from 210°F to 160°F can increase gelation time by over 100 minutes at high activator concentrations. ([[2024-alabdrabalnabi|Alabdrabalnabi, 2024, p. 93]])
