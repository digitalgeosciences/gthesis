# Clogging

**Summary**: The reduction of permeability due to the accumulation of particles or mineral precipitation in pore spaces.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2026-fatima|Fatima, 2026]] investigated clogging as a primary hurdle for managed aquifer recharge (MAR) systems.

## Scientific Context

- [[2026-fatima|Fatima, 2026]] Clogging is a critical technical challenge for the sustainability of MAR systems using treated wastewater.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2026-fatima|Fatima, 2026]] Measured via decline in outflow rate and hydraulic conductivity in column experiments.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Calcite precipitation and redissolution were identified as major contributors to clogging. ([[2026-fatima|Fatima, 2026, p. 17]])
