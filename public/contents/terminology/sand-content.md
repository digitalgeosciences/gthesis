# Sand Content

**Summary**: The amount of sand particles within the drilling fluid or filter cake.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-geri|Geri, 2012]] measured the sand content in the filter cake to predict its thickness.

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-geri|Geri, 2012]] High sand content degrades drilling fluid performance and complicates filter cake removal.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2012-geri|Geri, 2012]] Determined via XRD and SEM-EDS analysis.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Sand content reached more than 40% by weight at the last part of the horizontal section. ([[2012-geri|Geri, 2012, p. 125]])
