# Supercritical Fluid

**Summary**: A state of matter where a substance has properties between those of a gas and a liquid.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2019]] analyzed the behavior of CO2 in its supercritical state for EOR.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2019]] Standard condition for CO2 in many deep sandstone reservoirs.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2019]] Evaluated via pressure and temperature monitoring.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- CO2 at supercritical conditions (above 1071.8 psi and 31.1°C) produces weak foam. ([[2019-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2019, p. 29]])
