# Well Spacing

**Summary**: The distance between two adjacent wells in a reservoir.

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-khan|KHAN, 2015]] Well spacing is critical to avoid well collisions and ensure efficient drainage areas.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-khan|KHAN, 2015]] Evaluated using an ellipse equation for horizontal wells.

## Key Findings Across Sources
