# Surfactant Adsorption

**Summary**: The process by which surfactant molecules adhere to the rock surface.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1994-eme|Eme, 1994]] measured surfactant adsorption on limestone to evaluate chemical loss.

## Scientific Context

- [[1994-eme|Eme, 1994]] High adsorption reduces the amount of surfactant available to lower IFT.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1994-eme|Eme, 1994]] Determined by comparing initial and residual concentrations via ICP.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The adsorption of surfactant in the presence of alkali was lower than that without alkali. ([[1994-eme|Eme, 1994, p. 24]])
