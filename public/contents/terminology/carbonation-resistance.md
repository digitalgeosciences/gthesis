# Carbonation Resistance

**Summary**: The ability of a material to resist chemical changes and structural degradation when exposed to CO2.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2020-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2020]] evaluated the carbonation resistance of various cement additives in high-temperature conditions.

## Scientific Context

- [[2020-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2020]] Critical for the integrity of wellbores in carbon capture and storage projects.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2020-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2020]] Measured by tracking carbonation depth (μm) and carbonate formation volume.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Adding 0.3% BWOC of laponite improved the carbonation resistance by reducing both the carbonation depth and the rate of acid invasion. ([[2020-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2020, p. 81]])
