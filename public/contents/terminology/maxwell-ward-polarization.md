# Maxwell Ward Polarization

**Summary**: A type of polarization that occurs at the interface between two fluids with different conductivities.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-abdulwahab|Abdulwahab, 2023]] used Maxwell-Wagner polarization to identify non-wetting phase inclusions.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-abdulwahab|Abdulwahab, 2023]] It allows for the identification of oil inclusions within water-wet systems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-abdulwahab|Abdulwahab, 2023]] Analyzed via multi-frequency dielectric measurements.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Maxwell-Wagner polarization is a significant contributor to dielectric dispersion in the medium to high frequency range. ([[2023-abdulwahab|Abdulwahab, 2023, p. 35]])
