# Super Permeability

**Summary**: Zones of exceptionally high flow capacity in a reservoir.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-saraih|Saraih, 2024]] investigated the potential for super-permeability zones created by large burrows.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-saraih|Saraih, 2024]] Super-permeability is a key target in carbonate reservoir development.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-saraih|Saraih, 2024]] Inferred from burrow size, connectivity, and filling type.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Large, open, or partially filled burrows can create super-permeability zones with high production rates (e.g., >500 bbl/day per foot). ([[2024-saraih|Saraih, 2024, p. 36]])
