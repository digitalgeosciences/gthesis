# Super Permeability

**Summary**: Zones of exceptionally high flow capacity within a reservoir.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-saraih|Saraih, 2024]] investigated how burrows create super-permeability zones in the Aruma Formation.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-saraih|Saraih, 2024]] Super-permeability is often associated with large, interconnected burrow systems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

## Key Findings Across Sources
