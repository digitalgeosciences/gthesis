# Loss Of Circulation

**Summary**: The loss of drilling fluid into the formation during drilling operations.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2021-alsaihati|Alsaihati, 2021]] addressed the problem of lost circulation in real-time.

## Scientific Context

- [[2021-alsaihati|Alsaihati, 2021]] Losses can lead to significant economic impacts and safety risks at the rig site.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2021-alsaihati|Alsaihati, 2021]] Calculated as the difference between actual and theoretical reduction of active pit volume (APV).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Losses were identified when the difference between actual and theoretical reduction of APV exceeded a threshold of 0.5 bbl. ([[2021-alsaihati|Alsaihati, 2021, p. 59]])
