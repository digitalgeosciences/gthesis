# Evaporite Mineralogy

**Summary**: The study of minerals formed by the evaporation of aqueous solutions.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1994-al-guwaizani|Al-Guwaizani, 1994]] analyzed evaporite mineralogy to determine the distribution of gypsum, anhydrite, and halite.

## Scientific Context

- [[1994-al-guwaizani|Al-Guwaizani, 1994]] Evaporites are key indicators of the hydrological history of sabkha systems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1994-al-guwaizani|Al-Guwaizani, 1994]] Quantified via XRD and observed via SEM.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Gypsum is the most widely distributed evaporitic mineral at the sabkha surface, forming 30-100% of surface sediments. ([[1994-al-guwaizani|Al-Guwaizani, 1994, p. 70]])
