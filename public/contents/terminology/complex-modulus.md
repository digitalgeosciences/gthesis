# Complex Modulus

**Summary**: A measure of a material's viscoelasticity, where G' represents elasticity and G" represents viscosity.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014]] used complex moduli to evaluate the gel strength and stability of cross-linked systems.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014]] Used to determine if a gel is 'solid-like' enough to bridge fractures effectively.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014]] Determined via oscillatory frequency sweep tests.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The HPAM/PEI (3/1.2) system aged at 120°C for 1 day showed a storage modulus (G') of 3564 Pa, significantly higher than the base polymer. ([[2014-hashmat|Hashmat, 2014, p. 58]])
