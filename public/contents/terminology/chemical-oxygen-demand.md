# Chemical Oxygen Demand

**Summary**: A measure of the amount of oxygen required to chemically oxidize organic matter in water.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-al-aqeel|Al-Aqeel, 2023]] measured COD levels to evaluate the effectiveness of treatment methods.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-al-aqeel|Al-Aqeel, 2023]] High COD levels in spent caustic make it hazardous and difficult to treat biologically.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-al-aqeel|Al-Aqeel, 2023]] Chemical analysis of the waste stream.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The target for safe disposal is a COD level below 50 mg/L; the study achieved 57 mg/L under optimized conditions. ([[2023-al-aqeel|Al-Aqeel, 2023, p. 13]])
