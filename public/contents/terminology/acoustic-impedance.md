# Acoustic Impedance

**Summary**: A physical property of rock that is the product of density and velocity.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2002-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2002]] used acoustic-impedance as a common currency to link seismic data to petrophysical properties.

- [[2015-fuad|Fuad, 2015]] used acoustic impedance as an intermediate property derived from seismic inversion to predict porosity.

- [[2016-ali|Ali, 2016]] used acoustic impedance (AI) as a primary seismic attribute for fluid identification.

- [[2018-mohamed|Mohamed, 2018]] used to describe the impact of geological features on seismic waves.
