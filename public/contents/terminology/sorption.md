# Sorption

**Summary**: The uptake of molecules or atoms by a solid surface, including both physical and chemical processes.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2021-lawal|LAWAL, 2021]] investigated the sorption capacity of methane in a carbonate source rock under non-isothermal conditions.

## Scientific Context

- [[2021-lawal|LAWAL, 2021]] Crucial for evaluating gas storage in source rocks where pore structures are often small and complex.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2021-lawal|LAWAL, 2021]] measured via gravimetric methods under non-isothermal heating.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Non-isothermal methane sorption capacity was found to increase with temperature from 40°C to 300°C due to the coupling of maturation and sorption. ([[2021-lawal|LAWAL, 2021, p. 84]])
