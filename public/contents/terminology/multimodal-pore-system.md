# Multimodal Pore System

**Summary**: A pore system containing multiple distinct sizes (macropores, mesopores, and micropores).

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2020-isah|Isah, 2020]] studied the drainage mechanism in a multimodal pore system.

## Scientific Context

- [[2020-isah|Isah, 2020]] Impacts the reliability of standard petrophysical models like Archie's equation.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2020-isah|Isah, 2020]] Characterized via NMR T2 distribution and core analysis.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The complexity of carbonate reservoirs is largely due to these systems, making standard equations difficult to apply. ([[2020-isah|Isah, 2020, p. 18]])
