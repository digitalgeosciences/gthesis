# Weighting Material Sag

**Summary**: The settling of heavy minerals like barite and calcite in the liquid phase of drilling fluids.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-badhafere|Badhafere, 2023]] monitored weighting material sag using time-elapsed SIP measurements at 10 kHz.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-badhafere|Badhafere, 2023]] A critical issue in drilling operations that leads to non-uniform pressure distribution.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-badhafere|Badhafere, 2023]] Measured by comparing signals across different segments and over time.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- A significant change in real conductivity (9.5 umS/cm) and impedance (3.9 Ohms) was observed for bar1ite, indicating settlement. ([[2023-badhafere|Badhafere, 2023, p. 80]])
