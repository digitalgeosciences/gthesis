# Durability

**Summary**: The ability of a material to withstand wear, pressure, or environmental decay.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2023]] evaluated the long-term durability of geopolymer systems in acidic and saline environments.

- [[2024-suleiman-4|Suleiman, 2024]] evaluated the durability of nano-silica gel over a 6-month period in formation water.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2023]] Durability is critical for ensuring the integrity of well cement over many years.

- [[2024-suleiman-4|Suleiman, 2024]] evaluated the durability of nano-silica gel over a 6-month period in formation water.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2023]] Exposure of samples to acid, brine, and CO2 for extended periods.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Geopolymers showed significantly less strength loss (1.76%) compared to OPC (28%) when exposed to acid. ([[2023-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2023, p. 40]])


- The nano-silica gel maintained plugging efficiency above 99.98% after 6 months. ([[2024-suleiman-4|Suleiman, 2024, p. 66]])
