# Groundwater Salinity

**Summary**: The concentration of dissolved salts in groundwater, which affects water quality and suitability for use.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2022-pambudi|Pambudi, 2022]] assessed groundwater salinity as a key factor for ASR site selection, prioritizing fresh to slightly brackish zones.

## Scientific Context

- [[2022-pambudi|Pambudi, 2022]] Determines the suitability of an aquifer for storage and subsequent use without excessive contamination.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2022-pambudi|Pambudi, 2022]] Categorized into six classes based on concentration levels.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Salinity was categorized into six levels; values <1 g/L were assigned the highest rank (5) for ASR suitability. ([[2022-pambudi|Pambudi, 2022, p. 64]])
