# Bulk Density

**Summary**: The mass per unit volume of a rock.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-tariq|Tariq, 2016]] used bulk density as an input for ANN and ANFIS models.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-tariq|Tariq, 2016]] Used to determine porosity and lithology.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-tariq|Tariq, 2016]] Measured via wireline logs.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Bulk density was one of the primary inputs used to predict both Poisson's ratio and UCS. ([[2016-tariq|Tariq, 2016, p. 130]])
- [[2016-tariq|Tariq, 2016]] Values ranged from 2.0 to 3.0 g/cc.
