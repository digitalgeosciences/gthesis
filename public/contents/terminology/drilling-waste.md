# Drilling Waste

**Summary**: The materials (fluids and solid cuttings) produced during the drilling of oil and gas wells.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-attyah|Attyah, 2012]] analyzed the volume and composition of drilling waste to assess environmental impact.

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-attyah|Attyah, 2012]] Management of these wastes is critical due to their potential toxicity and environmental impact in the Eastern Province.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2012-attyah|Attyah, 2012]] Surveying companies on production volumes.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Waste volumes reported by companies ranged between 500-4000 tons for fluids and 300-3000 tons for solid cuttings. ([[2012-attyah|Attyah, 2012, p. 9]])
