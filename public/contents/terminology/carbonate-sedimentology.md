## Key Findings Across Sources

- The study established that the electrical behavior of carbonate rocks under long term CO2 storage is dynamic under a static condition. ([[2015-adebayo|Adebayo, 2015, p. 17]])


- Two distinct platform geometries were identified: a homoclinal ramp (Musayr) and fault-bounded platforms (Wadi Waqb). ([[2015-koeshidayatullah|Koeshidayatullah, 2015, p. 122]])

- Facies analysis documents low- to high-energy environments, including deep lagoonal, lagoonal shoal flank, and barrier sand shoal. ([[2015-elzain|Elzain, 2015, p. 13]])

- High Ca values (average 34% in CG1) were indicative of high carbonate productivity. ([[2022-septriandi|Septriandi, 2022, p. 142]])

- Host rock was deposited in a low energy - quit water environment (middle ramp), while infills were high-energy tempestites. ([[2022-syahputra|Syahputra, 2022, p. 81]])

- The reservoir shows a shoaling trend from wackestone to grainstone. ([[2024-yusmananto|Yusmananto, 2024, p. 99]])

- The presence of some microfossils within shallow water carbonates might point towards a marine connection. ([[2024-malik|Malik, 2024, p. 105]])

- The carbonates are found in all groups except the Shammar group. ([[1985-ghaled|Ghaled, 1985, p. 37]])
