# Deformities

**Summary**: Morphological abnormalities in foraminiferal tests caused by environmental stress or biological interactions.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-amao|Amao, 2017]] analyzed test deformities to assess the impact of high temperature and salinity.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-amao|Amao, 2017]] Used as indicators of environmental stress or biological interactions like parasitism.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-amao|Amao, 2017]] Quantified as a percentage of total population for specific taxa.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Peneroplis (58.4%) and Coscinospira (17.1%) accounted for 75.5% of all observed test deformities in Salwa Bay. ([[2017-amao|Amao, 2017, p. 20]])
