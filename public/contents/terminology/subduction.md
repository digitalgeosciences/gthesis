# Subduction

**Summary**: The process where one tectonic plate sinks beneath another into the mantle.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2025-kesumastuti|Kesumastuti, 2025]] investigated the geometry and depth extent of subducting slabs in northern Sulawesi.

## Scientific Context

- [[2025-kesumastuti|Kesumastuti, 2025]] Subduction is a primary driver of crustal formation and volcanic activity in Indonesia.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2025-kesumastuti|Kesumastuti, 2025]] Identified via high-velocity anomalies in P-wave tomograms.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- High-velocity perturbations in northern Sulawesi are interpreted as multiple subducting slabs. ([[2025-kesumastuti|Kesumastuti, 2025, p. 14]])
