# Storage Modulus

**Summary**: A measure of the elastic energy stored in a material.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-omer|Omer, 2012]] measured storage modulus (G’) to determine the gel strength and structural information of the polymer.

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-omer|Omer, 2012]] Indicates the ability of the LCM to form a stable gel structure.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2012-omer|Omer, 2012]] Measured during frequency sweep tests.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- For 1 wt% PAM, G’ values were observed at 0.68, 1.24, 8.41, and 26.52 Pa across different concentrations (noted as a typo in text for specific samples). ([[2012-omer|Omer, 2012, p. 56]])
