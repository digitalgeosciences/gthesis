# Time To Depth Conversion

**Summary**: The process of converting seismic travel times into depth measurements using a velocity model.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-felemban|Felemban, 2024]] evaluated the impact of offset limits on time-to-depth conversion accuracy for three formations.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-felemban|Felemban, 2024]] Crucial for determining the location of target formations and estimating reserves.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-felemban|Felemban, 2024]] Calculated by multiplying TWT by RMS velocity divided by 2000.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Errors in T2Z increase when large offsets are included because phase changes beyond critical angles weaken semblance energy. ([[2024-felemban|Felemban, 2024, p. 11]])
