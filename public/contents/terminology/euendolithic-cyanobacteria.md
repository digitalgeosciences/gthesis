# Euendolithic Cyanobacteria

**Summary**: Cyanobacteria that live inside rocks, specifically within carbonate substrates.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-amao|Amao, 2012]] studied the diversity and morphology of euendolithic cyanobacteria in extreme environments.

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-amao|Amao, 2012]] These organisms are significant for their role in biogeochemical cycles and adaptation to extreme environments.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2012-amao|Amao, 2012]] Identified via SEM and LM analysis of calcareous sediments.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Identified several species including Hyella and Solentia from both Arabian Gulf and Costa Rican samples. ([[2012-amao|Amao, 2012, p. 126]])
