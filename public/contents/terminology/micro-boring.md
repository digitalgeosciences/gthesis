# Micro Boring

**Summary**: The process by which microorganisms create tunnels or cavities in carbonate rocks.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-amao|Amao, 2012]] studied the mechanisms of micro-boring, including acid production and calcium ion sequestration.

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-amao|Amao, 2012]] Micro-boring is a key process for carbonate rock dissolution and biological corrosion.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2012-amao|Amao, 2012]] Observation of tunnel architecture and cell morphology.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Three main theories for boring include: (I) Rock dissolution by acid production, (II) Sequestration of Ca2+ using a pump, and (III) P-type ATPase-mediated transcellular Ca2+ transport. ([[2012-amao|Amao, 2012, p. 21]])
