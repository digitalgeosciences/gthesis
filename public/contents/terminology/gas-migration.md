# Gas Migration

**Summary**: The movement of gas through the cement slurry or set cement into the wellbore.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-ahmed-2|AHMED, 2023]] evaluated gas migration to ensure proper zonal isolation in high-pressure zones.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-ahmed-2|AHMED, 2023]] Gas migration can lead to poor zonal isolation and potential blowouts in oil wells.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-ahmed-2|AHMED, 2023]] Tested using a specialized gas migration apparatus at 1000 psi and 250°F.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Mixing hematite with Micromax was found to minimize gas migration compared to using them individually. ([[2023-ahmed-2|AHMED, 2023, p. 76]])
