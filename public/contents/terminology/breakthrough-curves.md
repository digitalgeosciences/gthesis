# Breakthrough Curves

**Summary**: A plot of the concentration of a solute in the effluent of a fixed-bed column as a function of time or volume.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2025-almousa|Almousa, 2025]] analyzed breakthrough curves to determine saturation times and adsorption capacities.

## Scientific Context

- [[2025-almousa|Almousa, 2025]] Breakthrough curves are essential for designing and sizing fixed-bed adsorption systems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2025-almousa|Almousa, 2025]] Calculated by plotting C/Co against time (t) and applying non-linear regression.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Breakthrough curves were used to calculate the total amount of MTBE and BTEX adsorbed by the CAC. ([[2025-almousa|Almousa, 2025, p. 37]])
