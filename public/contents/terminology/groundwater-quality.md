**Summary**: The chemical composition of groundwater, specifically its salinity and mineral content.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Taleh aquifer water in the southeast is poor quality due to high chloride and sulfate concentrations from anhydrite dissolution. ([[1993-moallim|Moallim, 1993, p. 180]])

- Deep groundwater is not suitable for drinking due to high concentrations of certain elements. ([[2011-alsadeq|Alsadeq, 2011, p. 12]])

- Average TDS values were 4200 mg/L in 2010, with a range of 3900 to 4700 mg/L. ([[2012-iwalewa|Iwalewa, 2012, p. 129]])
