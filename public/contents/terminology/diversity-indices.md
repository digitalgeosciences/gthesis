# Diversity Indices

**Summary**: Statistical measures used to quantify the variety and abundance of species in a community.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018]] calculated diversity-indices (Shannon-H, Fisher alpha) for foraminiferal assemblages.

## Scientific Context

- [[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018]] Used to identify environmental stress or high-energy zones.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018]] Calculation of Shannon-H, Fisher alpha, and Chao-1.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The highest diversity is found in lithofacies Mlg, while low diversity indicates high energy or dysoxic conditions. ([[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018, p. 60]])
