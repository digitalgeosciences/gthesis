# Nitrate

**Summary**: Nitrogen compounds often used in fertilizers and indicators of sewage contamination.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The majority of the deep ground water samples were above the MCL of 10 ppm, with a maximum of 43 ppm in DGW 7. ([[2011-alsadeq|Alsadeq, 2011, p. 128]])

- Atmospheric deposition and fertilizers are the potential sources of nitrate in the north and eastern sections, while manure and wastewater contribute in the west. ([[2019-ahmed|Ahmed, 2019, p. 13]])

- Nitrate concentration reached 73.3 mg/L at the end of the 3-hour run under optimal conditions. ([[2020-ghazwani|Ghazwani, 2020, p. 65]])
