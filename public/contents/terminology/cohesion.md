# Cohesion

**Summary**: The internal strength of a rock or soil that resists separation along a plane.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-tariq|Tariq, 2016]] developed models to predict cohesion using ANFIS, ANN, and SVM techniques.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-tariq|Tariq, 2016]] Cohesion is a key parameter in the Mohr-Coulomb failure criterion for rock mechanics.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-tariq|Tariq, 2016]] measured via laboratory tests and predicted from well logs.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- ANFIS was the optimum best model for predicting cohesion with an R2 of 0.94. ([[2016-tariq|Tariq, 2016, p. 154]])
