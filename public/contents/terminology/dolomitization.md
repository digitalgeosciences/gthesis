# Dolomitization

**Summary**: The chemical alteration of limestone into dolomite.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2002-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2002]] investigated the role of dolomitization in the development of porosity and permeability.

- [[1988-darwish|Darwish, 1988]] analyzed the extent of dolomitization in the Gulf of Salwa sediments.

- [[2017-elyamani|ElYamani, 2017]] investigated the dolomitization of the TMF by hypersaline fluids.

## Scientific Context

- [[2002-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2002]] Dolomitization is a key diagenetic process that enhances reservoir quality in carbonates.

- [[1988-darwish|Darwish, 1988]] analyzed the extent of dolomitization in the Gulf of Salwa sediments.

- [[2017-elyamani|ElYamani, 2017]] investigated the dolomitization of the TMF by hypersaline fluids.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2002-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2002]] Petrographic analysis and core description.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Dolomitizing fluids likely entered via fractures, creating high-permeability zones. ([[2002-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2002, p. 22]])

- Identified a lower dolomite interval up to 40 meters thick. ([[1988-darwish|Darwish, 1988, p. 183]])

- Dolomite crystals are zoned idiotopic rhombs with a cloudy core and clear rim (CCCR) and have Sr concentrations from 800 ppm to 1200 ppm. ([[2017-elyamani|ElYamani, 2017, p. 145]])
