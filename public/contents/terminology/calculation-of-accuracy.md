# Calculation Of Accuracy

**Summary**: A metric used to determine the concentration of cuttings in the annulus.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017]] used CCA to ensure that cutting concentrations did not exceed 0.05.
