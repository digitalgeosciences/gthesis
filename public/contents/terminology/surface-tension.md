# Surface Tension

**Summary**: The property of a liquid's surface that makes it behave like a stretched elastic membrane.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-mohamed|Mohamed, 2024]] measured surface tension to determine the Critical Micelle Concentration (CMC) of various ionic liquids.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-mohamed|Mohamed, 2024]] Lower surface tension is essential for reducing capillary pressure and improving fluid mobility in tight pores.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-mohamed|Mohamed, 2024]] measured using the KRUSS K100 tensiometer.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Ionic liquid X3 achieved the lowest surface tension values, reaching 39 mN/m in both 5 wt% KCl and fracturing fluid bases. ([[2024-mohamed|Mohamed, 2024, p. 33]])

## Contrasting Values and Interpretations

- [[2024-mohamed|Mohamed, 2024]] 39 mN/m to 47 mN/m depending on concentration and salt type.
