# Polyelectrolytes

**Summary**: Polymers that contain ionizable groups and can be used to modify the surface properties of materials.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-olabemiwo|Olabemiwo, 2017]] used polyelectrolytes (PDADMAC and PSS) to coat fly ash for improved cadmium removal.

## Scientific Context
