# Biofacies

**Summary**: A group of organisms that live together in a specific environment, used to interpret paleoenvironments.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2006-al-enezi|Al-Enezi, 2006]] used foraminiferal biofacies to characterize the environmental parameters recorded at each sample locality.

## Scientific Context

- [[2006-al-enezi|Al-Enezi, 2006]] used to determine environmental parameters like salinity and depth.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The dead assemblages (thanatocoenosis) were similar to living assemblages (biocoenosis), allowing them to be used as indicators of environment. ([[2006-al-enezi|Al-Enezi, 2006, p. 99]])
