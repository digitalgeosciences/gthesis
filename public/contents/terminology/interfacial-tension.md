# Interfacial Tension

**Summary**: The force per unit length existing at the interface between two immiscible fluids.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-ahmed|Ahmed, 2023]] IFT reduction is a primary mechanism for improving oil mobility and recovery in chemical EOR.

- [[2024-mohamed|Mohamed, 2024]] measured interfacial tension (IFT) to evaluate the effectiveness of surfactants in reducing capillary pressure.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-ahmed|Ahmed, 2023]] measured via spinning drop and calculated from microfluidic observations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The lowest IFT value of 3 mN/m was achieved with formulation x3. ([[2024-mohamed|Mohamed, 2024, p. 14]])
