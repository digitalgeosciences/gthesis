# Quartz Crystallinity

**Summary**: A measure of the crystalline state of quartz, often used to determine provenance and maturity.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2022-radwan|Radwan, 2022]] measured QCI values in sandstones and sands to investigate provenance and the role of chert.

## Scientific Context

- [[2022-radwan|Radwan, 2022]] Used to determine the geological source and transport history of sedimentary deposits.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2022-radwan|Radwan, 2022]] X-ray diffraction analysis of sand samples.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- QCI data confirmed that early Paleozoic sandstones are the main source for Arabian sands. ([[2022-radwan|Radwan, 2022, p. 82]])
