# Mass Transfer

**Summary**: A reaction state where the rate is limited by the movement of reactants or products to/from the surface.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2017]] determined if the dissolution process was mass transfer or surface reaction limited using RDA data.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2017]] Distinguishing between these regimes allows for the calculation of accurate diffusion coefficients.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2017]] Analysis of dissolution rate as a function of rotation speed.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The 20% GLDA/DI system became mass transfer limited at temperatures above 150°F. ([[2017-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2017, p. 20]])
