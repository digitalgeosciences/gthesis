# Plastogamy

**Summary**: A form of sexual reproduction in foraminifera where two individuals join to exchange gametes.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-arslan|Arslan, 2015]] observed plastogamy in Glabratellina margaritaceus during laboratory experiments.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-arslan|Arslan, 2015]] A successful reproductive strategy for certain species.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-arslan|Arslan, 2015]] Observation under light microscopy.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Glabratellina margaritaceus is involved in plastogamy, which provides protection and a higher survival rate for gametes. ([[2015-arslan|Arslan, 2015, p. 59]])
