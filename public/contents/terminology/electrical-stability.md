# Electrical Stability

**Summary**: A measure of the stability of an oil-water emulsion.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2011-sanmi|Sanmi, 2011]] measured electrical-stability to determine how well the water phase was held in the oil phase.

## Scientific Context

- [[2011-sanmi|Sanmi, 2011]] High electrical stability indicates a stable invert emulsion that will not separate under conditions of stress.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2011-sanmi|Sanmi, 2011]] Performed using the OFITE ESM-30A.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The final formulation achieved an electrical stability of 625 Volts. ([[2011-sanmi|Sanmi, 2011, p. 81]])
