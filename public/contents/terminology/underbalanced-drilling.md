# Underbalanced Drilling

**Summary**: A drilling technique where the hydrostatic pressure of the drilling fluid is maintained below the formation pore pressure.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-gowida|Gowida, 2024]] UBD is used in depleted and fractured formations to improve well productivity.
