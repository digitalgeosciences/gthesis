# Foam Strength

**Summary**: The ability of a foam to resist flow or deformation in a porous medium.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2016]] measured foam strength via pressure drop (ΔP) and bubble circularity.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2016]] Stronger foam is characterized by finer bubbles and higher pressure drops.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2016]] measured via steady-state pressure drop (ΔP) across the core.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Addition of N2 made the foam stronger for FS-51 and AOS at all qualities except 0.95. ([[2016-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2016, p. 80]])
