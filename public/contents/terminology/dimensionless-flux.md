# Dimensionless Flux

**Summary**: A normalized measure of fluid flow rate per unit length along the wellbore.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 2014]] calculated dimensionless flux to determine how flow is distributed between the two wings.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 2014]] Used to analyze how wellbore geometry and hydraulics affect production distribution.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 2014]] calculated using a multi-segment approach.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Dimensionless flux (Qd) was shown to be symmetrical for equal flow rates but asymmetrical when flow rates differed. ([[2014-al-gahtani|Al-Gahtani, 2014, p. 108]])
