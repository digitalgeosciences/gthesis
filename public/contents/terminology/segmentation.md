# Segmentation

**Summary**: The process of partitioning a digital image into multiple segments (sets of pixels) to simplify or change the representation of an object.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2025-ferreira|Ferreira, 2025]] applied semantic segmentation to identify ore minerals and microfossils in thin section images.

## Scientific Context

- [[2025-ferreira|Ferreira, 2025]] Essential for identifying mineral boundaries and quantifying mineral proportions in petrography.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2025-ferreira|Ferreira, 2025]] evaluated using mean Intersection over Union (mIoU) scores.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- STEGO achieved a mean IoU score of 0.8 for pyrite segmentation, indicating strong correlation with ground truth masks. ([[2025-ferreira|Ferreira, 2025, p. 30]])
