# Bottom Hole Pressure

**Summary**: The pressure of the fluid in the wellbore at the bottom of the well.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] evaluated impact of erosion on flowing bottom hole pressure (BHP).

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] BHP is critical for determining deliverability and flow rates.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] Calculated from reservoir and well equations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Wellbore erosion leads to a slight increase in BHP, which can affect near-wellbore condensate dropout. ([[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019, p. 69]])
- [[2019-suleiman|Suleiman, 2019]] Increased due to larger wellbore radius.
