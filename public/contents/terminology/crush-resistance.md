# Crush Resistance

**Summary**: The ability of a proppant to withstand high closure stress without breaking into fines.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-suleiman-5|Suleiman, 2024]] evaluated the crush resistance of 40/70 sand, RCS, and ceramic.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-suleiman-5|Suleiman, 2024]] Crucial for maintaining fracture conductivity by preventing the formation of fines that plug pore throats.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-suleiman-5|Suleiman, 2024]] Measured using a proppant crush tester (PCT) at increasing stress levels.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- RCS 40/70 showed only 4.7% crushed material at 10,000 psi compared to 46.8% for raw 40/70 sand. ([[2024-suleiman-5|Suleiman, 2024, p. 42]])
