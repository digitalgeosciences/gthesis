# Th U Ratio

**Summary**: The ratio of Thorium to Uranium, used as an indicator of terrestrial input and oxidation state.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-abdalla|Abdalla, 2013]] used Th/U ratios to identify reservoir facies and determine depositional environment.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-abdalla|Abdalla, 2013]] High ratios indicate detrital input and well-oxygenated water; low ratios suggest uranium mobility in reducing conditions.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2013-abdalla|Abdalla, 2013]] Calculated from SGR log counts and ICP-MS concentrations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- High Th/U ratios (>1) were associated with reservoir-facies (e.g., stromatoporoid wackestone, packstone). ([[2013-abdalla|Abdalla, 2013, p. 205]])
