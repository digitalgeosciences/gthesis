# Detrital Flux

**Summary**: The amount of sediment transported from land into a marine basin.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018]] used detrital-flux proxies (Ti/Al, Zr/Al) to determine the influence of terrestrial input.

## Scientific Context

- [[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018]] Used to understand the influence of terrestrial input on the sedimentary system.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018]] Calculated using Ti/Al and Zr/Al ratios from XRF data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Detrital proxies showed declining trends toward the facies Mdg, coinciding with lower foraminiferal abundance. ([[2018-perdana|Perdana, 2018, p. 97]])
