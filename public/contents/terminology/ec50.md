# EC50

**Summary**: The concentration of a substance that causes a specific effect in 50% of the test population within a specified time period.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2018-amiruddin|Amiruddin, 2018]] determined EC50 values for Brine Shrimp cysts to assess hatching efficiency under toxic stress.

## Scientific Context

- [[2018-amiruddin|Amiruddin, 2018]] Used to assess sublethal effects, specifically on the development of brine shrimp cysts.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

## Key Findings Across Sources
