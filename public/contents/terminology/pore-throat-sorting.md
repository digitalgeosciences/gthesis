# Pore Throat Sorting

**Summary**: A measure of the uniformity of the pore throat size distribution.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1991-ali|Ali, 1991]] calculated pore-throat-sorting to evaluate the homogeneity of the rock's internal structure.

## Scientific Context

- [[1991-ali|Ali, 1991]] Used to characterize the distribution of pore throats in carbonate and sandstone rocks.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1991-ali|Ali, 1991]] calculated from the ratio of pressures at 25% and 75% mercury saturation.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Berea and Bentheimer samples were found to be well sorted (PTS between 1.2 and 1.6). ([[1991-ali|Ali, 1991, p. 130]])
