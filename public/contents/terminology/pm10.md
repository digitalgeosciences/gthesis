# Pm10

**Summary**: Particulate matter with an aerodynamic diameter of less than or equal to 10 micrometers.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-lawal-2|Lawal, 2016]] measured PM10 concentrations in three cities to assess air quality.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-lawal-2|Lawal, 2016]] PM10 is a key indicator of air quality and its concentration is linked to human respiratory problems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-lawal-2|Lawal, 2016]] Gravimetric analysis using an Ecotech HiVol-3000 sampler.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Concentrations were compared against USEPA, WHO, and PME standards to determine if they exceeded safety limits. ([[2016-lawal-2|Lawal, 2016, p. 46]])
