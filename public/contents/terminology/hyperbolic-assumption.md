# Hyperbolic Assumption

**Summary**: The assumption that seismic travel times follow a hyperbolic curve in the t-x domain.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2020-alzahrani|Alzahrani, 2020]] compared the hyperbola-based approach with the linear-based squaring technique.

## Scientific Context

- [[2020-alzahrani|Alzahrani, 2020]] Standard method for determining velocity in seismic processing.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2020-alzahrani|Alzahrani, 2020]] Used to calculate NMO and stacking velocities.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- In the time–offset (t–x) plane, reflections appear as hyperbolas, while in the t2–x2 plane, they appear linearly. ([[2020-alzahrani|Alzahrani, 2020, p. 20]])
