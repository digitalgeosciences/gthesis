# Kerogen Maturation

**Summary**: The chemical transformation of organic matter into hydrocarbons through heat and pressure.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-moh-ali|Moh-Ali, 2024]] evaluated the impact of TCF on kerogen maturation in organic-rich shale.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-moh-ali|Moh-Ali, 2024]] Maturation is critical for determining the hydrocarbon potential of source rocks.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-moh-ali|Moh-Ali, 2024]] measured via gas chromatography and comparison with pre-treatment samples.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- TCF accelerated kerogen maturation by 18-42% through the generation of in-situ heat and pressure. ([[2024-moh-ali|Moh-Ali, 2024, p. 99]])
