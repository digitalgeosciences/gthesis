# Tic

**Summary**: The amount of inorganic carbon in a sample, often used as a proxy for dissolved CO2.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2025-alghawi|Alghawi, 2025]] measured TIC to evaluate the effect of adding CO2 into the collected sample.

## Scientific Context

- [[2025-alghawi|Alghawi, 2025]] Used to quantify the amount of CO2 sequestered or dissolved in the system.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2025-alghawi|Alghawi, 2025]] Measured using an aj-Analyzer multi-N/C 3100.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- TIC levels were higher at 1000 psi compared to lower pressures (e.g., 472.9 mg/l at 200 psi). ([[2025-alghawi|Alghawi, 2025, p. 53]])
