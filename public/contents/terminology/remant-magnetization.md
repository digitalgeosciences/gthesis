# Remant Magnetization

**Summary**: The magnetic state of a rock as it exists in nature.

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1993-kaka|Kaka, 1993]] analyzed remanent_magnetization to distinguish between primary and secondary components.

## Scientific Context

- [[1993-kaka|Kaka, 1993]] Essential for identifying the primary signal from secondary noise in paleomagnetic studies.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1993-kaka|Kaka, 1993]] Measured using a Digital Spinner Magnetometer (DSM-1).

## Key Findings Across Sources
