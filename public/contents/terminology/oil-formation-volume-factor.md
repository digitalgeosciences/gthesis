# Oil Formation Volume Factor

**Summary**: The volume of oil and its dissolved gas at reservoir conditions compared to the volume of oil at surface.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2005-ademola|ADEMOLA, 2005]] evaluated several correlations for predicting oil FVF at bubblepoint.

## Scientific Context

- [[2005-ademola|ADEMOLA, 2005]] Used to estimate the shrinkage of oil liquid volume during production.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2005-ademola|ADEMOLA, 2005]] Empirical correlation modeling.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The Al-Marhoun (1992) correlation showed the most outstanding performance for oil FVF at bubblepoint with an average relative error of 0.59%. ([[2005-ademola|ADEMOLA, 2005, p. 72]])
