# Fines Migration

**Summary**: The movement of small particles (fines) through the pore system, potentially causing plugging.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2015]] investigated fines-migration as a mechanism for both oil recovery and potential formation damage.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2015]] Fines migration can significantly impact permeability and oil recovery.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2015]] Monitored via pressure drop measurements during core flooding.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Fines migration was identified as a possible cause for increased oil recovery in some cases but also as a source of permeability reduction in others. ([[2015-mahmoud|Mahmoud, 2015, p. 53]])
