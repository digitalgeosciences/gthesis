# Paleobathymetry

**Summary**: The reconstruction of past water depths in a sedimentary basin.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-jalloh|Jalloh, 2024]] developed a conceptual paleobathymetric model for the D7 Unit to define paleoenvironmental conditions.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-jalloh|Jalloh, 2024]] Used to interpret the transition from lagoonal to intra-shelf environments during transgression.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-jalloh|Jalloh, 2024]] derived from foraminiferal morphogroup analysis and abundance of planktic/agglutinated species.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The model highlights the influence of eustatic sea-level variations on depositional systems, showing transitions from shallow lagoonal to deeper intra-shelf basin settings. ([[2024-jalloh|Jalloh, 2024, p. 16]])
