# Paleobathymetry

**Summary**: The study of the depth of water in the past.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-septriandi|Septriandi, 2016]] used morphogroup analysis to interpret paleobathymetric trends during the deposition of the Dam Formation.

- [[2024-jalloh|Jalloh, 2024]] developed a paleobathymetric model for the D7 unit.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-septriandi|Septriandi, 2016]] Determining the depth of the sea during deposition to understand environmental changes.

- [[2024-jalloh|Jalloh, 2024]] developed a paleobathymetric model for the D7 unit.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-septriandi|Septriandi, 2016]] Semi-quantitative assessment based on morphogroup proportions.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Changes in the proportions of the morphogroups are used to semi-quantitatively assess paleobathymetric trends. ([[2016-septriandi|Septriandi, 2016, p. 38]])

- Highlighted influence of eustatic sea-level variations on depositional systems. ([[2024-jalloh|Jalloh, 2024, p. 16]])
