# Thin Beds

**Summary**: Geological layers that are thinner than the vertical resolution of conventional logging tools.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-alqunais|Alqunais, 2019]] analyzed thin-beds to identify bypassed pay zones in complex formations.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-alqunais|Alqunais, 2019]] A major cause of LRP, where thin sand and shale layers average out to a low resistivity value.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-alqunais|Alqunais, 2019]] Identified using high-resolution imaging tools.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Thin beds can be as small as 0.24 inches, which are often missed by standard logs due to lack of vertical resolution. ([[2019-alqunais|Alqunais, 2019, p. 15]])
