# Ground Roll

**Summary**: Low-frequency, high-amplitude surface waves that travel horizontally.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

## Scientific Context

- [[2018-putra|Putra, 2018]] Ground roll is a primary noise source that masks deeper seismic reflections.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods
