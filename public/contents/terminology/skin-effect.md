# Skin Effect

**Summary**: The additional pressure drop caused by damage or stimulation near the wellbore.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-khan-2|Khan, 2013]] included skin effects in the analytical solutions for both standard and fractional models.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-khan-2|Khan, 2013]] Critical for determining the actual productivity of a well.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2013-khan-2|Khan, 2013]] Determined via pressure transient analysis.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Skin values were accurately matched when using the fractional diffusion model (e.g., 1.21 vs 1.51). ([[2013-khan-2|Khan, 2013, p. 97]])
