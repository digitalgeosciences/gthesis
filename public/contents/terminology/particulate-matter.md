# Particulate Matter

**Summary**: A mixture of solid and liquid particles suspended in the atmosphere.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-lawal-2|Lawal, 2016]] studied the concentration and characteristics of particulate matter (PM) in three cities.

- [[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019]] studied particulate matter (PM10) as a primary indicator of air quality.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-lawal-2|Lawal, 2016]] PM is a significant environmental concern due to its impact on human respiratory health.

- [[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019]] studied particulate matter (PM10) as a primary indicator of air quality.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods
