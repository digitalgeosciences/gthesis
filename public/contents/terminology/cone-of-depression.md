# Cone Of Depression

**Summary**: The lowering of the water table around a pumping well due to extraction.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-iwalewa|Iwalewa, 2012]] analyzed cone-of-depression impacts to determine safe zones for new well construction.

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-iwalewa|Iwalewa, 2012]] Critical for determining the spatial extent of impact from groundwater extraction.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

## Key Findings Across Sources
