# Ångström Exponent

**Summary**: A measure of the size distribution of particles in the atmosphere based on their wavelength dependency.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019]] used Angstrom Exponent to distinguish between fine and coarse mode aerosols.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019]] used to determine if particles are fine or coarse based on their source.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019]] calculated from multi-wavelength AOD data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- An Angstrom Exponent of 1 or greater indicates fine mode aerosols, while less than one indicates coarse mode. ([[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019, p. 40]])
