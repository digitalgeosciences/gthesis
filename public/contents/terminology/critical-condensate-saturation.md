# Critical Condensate Saturation

**Summary**: The minimum amount of condensate that must be present for it to become mobile.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1999-fawumi|Fawumi, 1999]] evaluated critical-condensate-saturation to determine its impact on well productivity.

## Scientific Context

- [[1999-fawumi|Fawumi, 1999]] Determines how much liquid is 'trapped' versus available for flow.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1999-fawumi|Fawumi, 1999]] Simulated and varied from 0% to 40%.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Higher critical condensate saturation leads to lower well productivity; a 10% value was used as the standard for the study's primary model. ([[1999-fawumi|Fawumi, 1999, p. 89]])
