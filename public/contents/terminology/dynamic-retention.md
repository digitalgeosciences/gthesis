# Dynamic Retention

**Summary**: The amount of chemical substance retained by the rock during a flow process.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-al-azani|Al-Azani, 2024]] evaluated dynamic retention to balance oil recovery with surfactant loss.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-al-azani|Al-Azani, 2024]] High dynamic retention can lead to high chemical costs and reduced effectiveness in the reservoir.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods
