# Well Stimulation

**Summary**: Techniques used to improve the flow of fluids in a well.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Acidizing sandstone results primarily in dissolution of minerals rather than creation of new flow paths. ([[1999-al-muhareb|Al-Muhareb, 1999, p. 17]])


- Optimized injection rates for HEDTA were found between 1.5 and 2.0 cc/m to balance time and volume requirements. ([[2015-barri|Barri, 2015, p. 190]])

- Chelating agents like DTPA provided significant permeability improvements in both Berea and Bandera cores. ([[2016-hamza|HAMZA, 2016, p. 19]])

- The combination of in-situ HF and thermochemicals provided a 485x permeability increase in Scioto core. ([[2019-gomaa|Gomaa, 2019, p. 138]])

- Foamed acid significantly improved fracture conductivity compared to conventional acid treatments. ([[2024-al-drwish|AL-DRWISH, 2024, p. 30]])
