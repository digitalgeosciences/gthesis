## Key Findings Across Sources

- All three systems met the minimum requirement of 2000 psi for durability against perforation shots. ([[2012-amer|Amer, 2012, p. 110]])


- 1% NC system provided high compressive strength (7190 psi) after 48 hours. ([[2013-murtaza|Murtaza, 2013, p. 114]])

- Adding 5% Nano-clay resulted in an increase in the elastic modulus by 143% at 28 days and improved compressive strength. ([[2015-thalab|Thalab, 2015, p. 61]])

- 0.1% CNT provided high compressive strength after 48 hours and the shortest transition period. ([[2015-khan-2|Khan, 2015, p. 106]])

- G2 slurry showed significant improvement in early strength, reaching 1983 psi at 6 hours. ([[2017-baig|Baig, 2017, p. 104]])

- Consolidated sand packs reached strengths exceeding 1800 psi and 1490 psi respectively. ([[2020-gowida|Gowida, 2020, p. 17]])

- A 12 ppg non-foamed cement had a compressive strength of 254 psi, while the 11.8 ppg foamed cement reached 1050 psi. ([[2022-adjei|Adjei, 2022, p. 43]])
