# Curie Point

**Summary**: The temperature at which a material loses its permanent magnetic properties.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-chavanidis|Chavanidis, 2024]] used the Curie point to estimate the geotherm of the area.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-chavanidis|Chavanidis, 2024]] Used to determine the depth of high-temperature zones in geothermal systems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-chavanidis|Chavanidis, 2024]] Analysis of aeromagnetic data to find the depth where magnetism is lost.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- For magnetite, this depth is encountered at the temperature of approximately 580°C. ([[2024-chavanidis|Chavanidis, 2024, p. 42]])
