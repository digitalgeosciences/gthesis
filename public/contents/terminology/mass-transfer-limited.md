# Mass Transfer Limited

**Summary**: A regime where the rate of dissolution is controlled by the transport of reactants to the surface.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014]] identified mass transfer limited regimes at low rotation speeds.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014]] Helps distinguish between physical and chemical limitations in acidizing.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014]] Determined by comparing dissolution rates at different rotation speeds.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- At low rotational speeds, the acid dissolution rate increased with speed, indicating a mass transfer limited regime. ([[2014-khalid|Khalid, 2014, p. 15]])
