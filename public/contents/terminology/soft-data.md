# Soft Data

**Summary**: Data that is more extensively sampled than well data, such as seismic.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2001-al-khalifa|Al-Khalifa, 2001]] integrated soft-data (seismic impedance) to improve the spatial resolution of porosity models.

## Scientific Context

- [[2001-al-khalifa|Al-Khalifa, 2001]] Soft data provides spatial continuity that is missing in sparse well data.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2001-al-khalifa|Al-Khalifa, 2001]] analyzed via correlation with hard data (well logs).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Seismic impedance was identified as a high-quality soft data source for porosity estimation due to its strong correlation with log porosity. ([[2001-al-khalifa|Al-Khalifa, 2001, p. 48]])
