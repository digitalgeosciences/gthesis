# Geopolymer

**Summary**: An inorganic polymeric cementitious binder formed from aluminosilicate substances and alkali activators.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2023]] Geopolymers are being explored as eco-friendly alternatives to Portland cement in the oil industry due to their superior performance in harsh environments.
