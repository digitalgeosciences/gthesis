# Two Phase Flow

**Summary**: The simultaneous flow of two fluids (typically oil and water) through a porous medium.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2018-salih|Salih, 2018]] evaluated the proxy model's ability to simulate two-phase flow in an 'Egg Model'.

## Scientific Context

- [[2018-salih|Salih, 2018]] Essential for modeling waterflooding and other EOR methods.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2018-salih|Salih, 2018]] Simulated using the Egg Model with specific relative permeability values.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The proxy model accurately predicted oil and water production rates with low error margins compared to numerical simulation. ([[2018-salih|Salih, 2018, p. 67]])
