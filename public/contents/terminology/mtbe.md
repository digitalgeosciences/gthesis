# MTBE

**Summary**: A fuel additive used to increase octane rating, known for being persistent in groundwater.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- TiO2 showed higher removal rates compared to the other materials at a dosage of 10 mg under 0.98 mW/cm^2 UV light. ([[2013-muhyedeen|Muhyedeen, 2013, p. 52]])


- MTBE has high solubility (50,000 mg/L) and low soil adsorption, making it highly mobile in groundwater. ([[2014-adebayo|Adebayo, 2014, p. 17]])

- MTBE was found to be highly soluble in water and potentially carcinogenic, requiring effective treatment methods like adsorption. ([[2018-adeyemi|Adeyemi, 2018, p. 23]])

- Optimal conditions for MTBE were found at a dosage of 2g, concentration of 5000 μg/L, and flow rate of 1 mL/min. ([[2025-almousa|Almousa, 2025, p. 13]])
