# Potentiometric Surface

**Summary**: The height of the water table or pressure head in an aquifer.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2012-iwalewa|Iwalewa, 2012]] monitored the potentiometric-surface to evaluate drawdown from pumping.

## Scientific Context

- [[2012-iwalewa|Iwalewa, 2012]] Used to determine the impact of pumping on aquifer levels.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2012-iwalewa|Iwalewa, 2012]] Measured via observation wells and simulated using finite difference modeling.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Average drawdown of 8.5 m was recorded between 1967 and year 2010. ([[2012-iwalewa|Iwalewa, 2012, p. 88]])
