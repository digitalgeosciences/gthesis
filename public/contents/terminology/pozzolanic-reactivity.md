# Pozzolanic Reactivity

**Summary**: The chemical reaction between pozzolans and calcium hydroxide to produce additional cementitious gel (C-S-H).

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2022-adjei|Adjei, 2022]] evaluated the pozzolanic reactivity of various bentonite types.

## Scientific Context

- [[2022-adjei|Adjei, 2022]] Crucial for determining the suitability of pozzolanic additives in improving the strength and durability of cement systems.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2022-adjei|Adjei, 2022]] Measured via TGA weight loss and XRD peak analysis.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Both calcined calcium bentonite and fly ash exhibited similar pozzolanic reactivity, as evidenced by comparable weight loss in TGA analysis. ([[2022-adjei|Adjei, 2022, p. 131]])
