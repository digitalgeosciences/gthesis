# Gravity Override

**Summary**: A phenomenon where gas rises to the top of a reservoir due to lower density compared to oil and water, leading to poor sweep efficiency.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2019-al-azani|AL-AZANI, 2019]] investigated gravity-override as the primary challenge in CO2-EOR for thick reservoirs.

- [[2021-al-shuqayyiqi|Al-Shuqayyiqi, 2021]] investigated gravity-override using different foam qualities and SAG.

## Scientific Context

- [[2019-al-azani|AL-AZANI, 2019]] Gravity override is the main factor resulting in poor sweep efficiency and early breakthrough in CO2-EOR.
