# Fire Retardancy

**Summary**: The ability of a material to resist ignition or slow the spread of flame.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-khalid|Khalid, 2023]] evaluated fire retardancy using TGA and LOI analysis.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-khalid|Khalid, 2023]] Fire safety is critical for gas transport infrastructure.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-khalid|Khalid, 2023]] measured via char residue percentage and Limited Oxygen Index (LOI)

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The inclusion of MOF increased char residue by 63% and LOI by 24%, significantly improving fire-retardancy. ([[2023-khalid|Khalid, 2023, p. 91]])
