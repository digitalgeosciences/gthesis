# Epar

**Summary**: A parameter in DEM modeling that represents the average shape of pores as an aspect ratio.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2022-salih|Salih, 2022]] used EPAR to quantify the impact of pore geometry on P- and S-wave velocity.

## Scientific Context

- [[2022-salih|Salih, 2022]] EPAR allows for a more quantitative comparison of different pore geometries' effects on seismic velocities.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2022-salih|Salih, 2022]] Calculated from DEM modeling results and compared to experimental data.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Samples with high EPAR values (around 0.4) corresponding to oomoldic and framework pores showed higher velocities than those with lower EPARs. ([[2022-salih|Salih, 2022, p. 68]])
