# Resistivity Inversion

**Summary**: The mathematical process of converting apparent resistivity data into a model of true subsurface resistivity.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017]] performed resistivity inversion to derive 3D models from the collected field data.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017]] Essential for converting raw geophysical measurements into interpretable geological models.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017]] Iterative optimization using least-squares methods.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Modern inversion techniques can deal with arbitrary resistivity distributions and are fully automated, often using non-linear optimization methods. ([[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017, p. 48]])
