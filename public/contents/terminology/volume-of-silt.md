# Volume of Silt

**Summary**: The volume of silt within the reservoir, which impacts permeability and flow.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] estimated volume_of_silt to characterize the lithology and quality of the rock.

## Scientific Context

- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] Used to identify and map lithological changes like silt bodies.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] Derived from well logs and predicted via multi-attribute regression.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Volume of silt was successfully predicted using a multi-attribute approach with good correlation. ([[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997, p. 47]])
- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] Values ranged across various wells (e.g., 0.07 to 0.66).
