# Volume Of Silt

**Summary**: The volume of silt particles within the rock matrix, affecting permeability.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] mapped the volume of silt using multi-attribute analysis to identify reservoir quality zones.

## Scientific Context

- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] Used to identify and delineate zones of poor reservoir quality due to fines content.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997]] calculated from well logs and predicted via multivariate regression.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Volume of silt values across the study wells ranged from 0.07 to 0.66. ([[1997-al-marhoun|Al-Marhoun, 1997, p. 47]])
