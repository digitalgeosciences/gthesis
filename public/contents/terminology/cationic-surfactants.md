# Cationic Surfactants

**Summary**: Surfactants with a positively charged head group.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2022-xiao|Xiao, 2022]] evaluated cationic surfactants as the most effective class for altering carbonate rock wettability.

## Scientific Context

- [[2022-xiao|Xiao, 2022]] Preferred in carbonate systems due to their ability to form ion pairs with carboxylates.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2022-xiao|Xiao, 2022]] Characterized by CMC, Krafft temperature, and HLB values.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Cationic surfactants have been proved to be more effective than anionic, nonionic, and zwitterionic surfactants in wettibility modification of carbonate surface. ([[2022-xiao|Xiao, 2022, p. 47]])
