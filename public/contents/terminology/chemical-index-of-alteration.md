# Chemical Index of Alteration

**Summary**: A geochemical index used to quantify the extent of chemical weathering.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[1999-babalola|Babalola, 1999]] used CIA values to assess the impact of chemical weathering on the Wajid Sandstone.

## Scientific Context

- [[1999-babalola|Babalola, 1999]] CIA helps distinguish between primary mineralogy and secondary alteration.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[1999-babalola|Babalola, 1999]] calculated from major element concentrations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- CIA values ranged from 51 to 98, indicating extensive chemical weathering at the source. ([[1999-babalola|Babalola, 1999, p. 167]])
