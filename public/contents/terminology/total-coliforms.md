# Total Coliforms

**Summary**: A group of bacteria used as indicators for fecal contamination.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-yusuf|Yusuf, 2015]] measured total-coliforms to assess risk of pathogens.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-yusuf|Yusuf, 2015]] Used as a proxy measure for the presence of pathogens in water.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-yusuf|Yusuf, 2015]] Enumerated on Levine EMB agar using membrane filter technique.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Total Coliform counts in sampling stations ranged from 4000 to 310000 CFU/100ml. ([[2015-yusuf|Yusuf, 2015, p. 14]])
