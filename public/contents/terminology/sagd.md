# Sagd

**Summary**: A thermal EOR method where steam is injected into a horizontal well to reduce oil viscosity and allow gravity to move it to a lower producer.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-khan|KHAN, 2015]] evaluated the SAGD process as a primary heavy oil recovery technique.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-khan|KHAN, 2015]] SAGD is a standard for heavy oil, but requires high water/energy; VAPEX is an alternative with lower energy but lower rates.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-khan|KHAN, 2015]] Simulated via reservoir models.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- SAGD showed better performance than VAPEX in Case 2 and Case 3 of the optimization study. ([[2015-khan|KHAN, 2015, p. 17]])
- [[2015-khan|KHAN, 2015]] Superior performance in complex optimization cases (2 and 3).
