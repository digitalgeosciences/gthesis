# Drilling Specific Energy

**Summary**: A modified version of MSE that includes a bit hydraulic term to better account for the impact of hydraulics on drilling performance.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-khamis|Khamis, 2013]] utilized drilling-specific-energy (DSE) to identify inefficient drilling conditions in real-time.

- [[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017]] used DSE as an indicator for ROP optimization.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-khamis|Khamis, 2013]] Used to distinguish between mechanical and hydraulic influences on ROP.

- [[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017]] used DSE as an indicator for ROP optimization.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2013-khamis|Khamis, 2013]] Calculation using the formula including bit hydraulics.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The inclusion of the hydraulic term allows DSE to accurately reflect how much energy is required to drill faster when mechanical energy is increased. ([[2013-khamis|Khamis, 2013, p. 32]])


- The developed model can assist drilling engineers in selecting improved drilling parameters by optimizing the drilling specific energy. ([[2017-al-rubaii|AL-RUBAII, 2017, p. 22]])
