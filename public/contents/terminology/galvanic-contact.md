# Galvanic Contact

**Summary**: The electrical connection between the electrodes and the ground surface.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017]] addressed issues of poor galvanic contact to ensure high data quality in dry soil conditions.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017]] Critical for ensuring that measured resistivity reflects soil properties rather than contact issues.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017]] Measured as Contact Resistance (CRS) during pre-survey checks.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Poor galvanic contact leads to significant degradation of data quality when electrodes are on highly resistive surfaces like dry sands or gravel. ([[2017-tayyab|Tayyab, 2017, p. 49]])
