# Colloidal Stability Index

**Summary**: An index used to determine the stability of asphaltenes in crude oil based on their polarity.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-suleiman|Suleiman, 2023]] used CSI to assess the stability of asphaltene fractions, where a value > 0.95 indicates unstable crude.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-suleiman|Suleiman, 2023]] Used to predict if asphaltenes will remain in solution or precipitate under specific conditions.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-suleiman|Suleiman, 2023]] Calculated from SARA fraction data and dielectric constants.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- CSI > 0.95 depicts unstable crude while CSI < 0.95 represents stable crude. ([[2023-suleiman|Suleiman, 2023, p. 71]])
