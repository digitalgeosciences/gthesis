# In Situ Co2 Generation

**Summary**: The production of CO2 within the reservoir using chemical reactions.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2013]] evaluated in-situ CO2 generation using low pH chelating agents like HEDTA and BCA.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2013]] Used to improve oil mobility and provide gas drive in carbonate reservoirs.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2013-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2013]] Assessed by comparing recovery rates of different chelating agents.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- In-situ CO2 generated by low pH chelating agents increased recovery by 30% of the OOIP. ([[2013-ibrahim|Ibrahim, 2013, p. 98]])
