# Thickening Time

**Summary**: The period during which a cement slurry remains pumpable under specific conditions.

---

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Thickening time is typically controlled by using retarders; addition of carbohydrates like sucrose can extend it. ([[2013-murtaza|Murtaza, 2013, p. 53]])


- Addition of 3% untreated Saudi bentonite increased the thickening time from 3 hours to 11 hours. ([[2015-thalab|Thalab, 2015, p. 89]])

- 0.1% and 0.25% CNT concentrations increased thickening time compared to the base mix. ([[2015-khan-2|Khan, 2015, p. 91]])

- A mixture of retarder and superplasticizer increased thickening time from 80 to 580 min at 195°F. ([[2023-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2023, p. 87]])
