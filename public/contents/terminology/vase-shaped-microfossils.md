# Vase Shaped Microfossils

**Summary**: Small, vase-like fossils often found in carbonate rocks.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-malik|Malik, 2024]] identified vase-shaped-microfossils as the first of their kind in Saudi Arabia.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-malik|Malik, 2024]] Used as indicators of early microbial life and environmental conditions in the Neoproterozoic.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-malik|Malik, 2024]] identified via thin-section petrography.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The VSMs display various morphologies, are infilled with calcite, and may occur singularly or in clusters. ([[2024-malik|Malik, 2024, p. 83]])
