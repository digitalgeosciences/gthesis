# Geo Accumulation Index

**Summary**: An index used to evaluate the degree of metal accumulation in a sample relative to its background.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024]] calculated the geo-accumulation-index (Igeo) for HMs.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024]] Used to classify samples as unpolluted, moderately contaminated, or heavily contaminated.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024]] calculated using a formula involving concentration and background values.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- High Igeo values for Pb, Cu, Cd, and Zn resulted from increased outdoor emissions and indoor dust. ([[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024, p. 145]])
