# Clay Mineralogy

**Summary**: The study of the minerals that make up the clay fraction in a rock.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-abdullah|Abdullah, 2013]] calculated clay volume (VCL) to assess its impact on resistivity and porosity.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-abdullah|Abdullah, 2013]] Clay content affects both porosity and the ability of the rock to conduct electricity.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2013-abdullah|Abdullah, 2013]] Calculated from Gamma Ray and Density logs.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Clay volume was reduced from 0.65 to 0.45 when using LSA techniques in Well A's thin-bed zone. ([[2013-abdullah|Abdullah, 2013, p. 83]])

- Kaolinite is the dominant clay mineral, acting as a pore lining and reducing permeability. ([[2016-abbas|Abbas, 2016, p. 105]])
