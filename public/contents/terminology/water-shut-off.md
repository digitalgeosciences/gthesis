# Water Shut Off

**Summary**: A technique used to block water from entering a wellbore, typically in high permeability zones or fractures.

---

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-suleiman-4|Suleiman, 2024]] A critical operation for managing reservoir conformance and extending the life of producing wells.
