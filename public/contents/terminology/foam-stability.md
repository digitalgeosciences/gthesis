# Foam Stability

**Summary**: The ability of a foam to maintain its structure and integrity under specific conditions.

---

## Key Findings Across Sources

- N2 addition improved foam stability at supercritical conditions where pure CO2 was weak. ([[2019-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2019, p. 18]])


- At 1000 psi, the foam half-life increased from 97 minutes at 14.7 psi to over 350 minutes. ([[2024-gowida|Gowida, 2024, p. 63]])

- N2-foam was more stable than CO2-foam because the higher permeability of CO2 through the film led to faster bubble coalescence. ([[2024-al-drwish|AL-DRWISH, 2024, p. 54]])
