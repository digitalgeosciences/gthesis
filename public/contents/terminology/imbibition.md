# Imbibition

**Summary**: The process of a wetting fluid entering and spreading through a porous medium.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-al-arfaj|Al-Arfaj, 2017]] studied the imbibition of different fluids into the shale pore network.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-al-arfaj|Al-Arfaj, 2017]] Understanding imbibition is key to predicting how drilling fluids interact with the rock matrix.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-al-arfaj|Al-Arfaj, 2017]] measured using analytical balances and NMR T2 profiles.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Imbibition is driven by capillary pressure, osmosis, and water adsorption. ([[2017-al-arfaj|Al-Arfaj, 2017, p. 39]])
