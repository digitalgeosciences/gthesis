# Wormlike Micelles

**Summary**: Long, worm-like structures formed by surfactants that provide viscoelastic properties.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2014-azad|Azad, 2014]] investigated the formation of WLM to ensure mobility control in high salinity conditions.

- [[2024-al-drwish|AL-DRWISH, 2024]] identified wormlike-micelles as the primary reason for the superior performance of Duomeen TTM.

## Scientific Context

- [[2014-azad|Azad, 2014]] WLM provide the 'living polymer' behavior needed for both mobility control and displacement.

- [[2024-al-drwish|AL-DRWISH, 2024]] identified wormlike-micelles as the primary reason for the superior performance of Duomeen TTM.
