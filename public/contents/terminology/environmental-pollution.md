## Key Findings Across Sources

- Soil contaminated with crude oil was treated using a consortium of two bacterial strains. ([[2011-salamah|Salamah, 2011, p. 67]])


- Hydrocarbons like Benzene and Toluene were detected in shallow groundwater near industrial sites. ([[2011-alsadeq|Alsadeq, 2011, p. 92]])

- Tarut Bay showed significantly higher contamination than Jubail due to agricultural runoff. ([[2013-mohammed|Mohammed, 2013, p. 14]])

- Fly ash is a waste material that can be repurposed for environmental remediation. ([[2014-adebayo|Adebayo, 2014, p. 97]])

- Heavy metals like lead, cadmium, and mercury are major concerns for human health. ([[2015-bello|Bello, 2015, p. 19]])

- Jubail harbour showed significantly higher concentrations compared to other sites due to industrial and agricultural discharges. ([[2015-akere|Akere, 2015, p. 42]])

- All three outfall locations were found to serve as important sources of inorganic nutrient into the coastal waters. ([[2015-yusuf|Yusuf, 2015, p. 14]])

- Pollution can cause deformities to rise from less than 2% in unpolluted areas to up to 50% in heavily contaminated ones. ([[2015-arslan|Arslan, 2015, p. 41]])

- Several heavy metals (Cr, Zn) were found at levels exceeding safety standards in some areas. ([[2016-lawal-2|Lawal, 2016, p. 104]])

- Pyrene is a persistent pollutant that poses risks to human health and the environment. ([[2017-budiyanto|Budiyanto, 2017, p. 21]])

- Cadmium is a highly toxic and potentially carcinogenic heavy metal found in industrial waste. ([[2017-olabemiwo|Olabemiwo, 2017, p. 20]])

- Oil-based mud (OBM) has higher concentrations of PAHs than water-based muds. ([[2018-amiruddin|Amiruddin, 2018, p. 26]])

- MTBE is classified by the USEPA as a possible human carcinogen in groundwater. ([[2018-adeyemi|Adeyemi, 2018, p. 25]])

- Both materials showed significant potential for removing hazardous contaminants from water. ([[2019-salawudeen|Salawudeen, 2019, p. 93]])

- Particulate matter levels are influenced by industrial activity, coastal proximity, and seasonal weather events like sandstorms. ([[2019-alotaibi|Alotaibi, 2019, p. 26]])

- The overall assessment showed that the sediment collected from the mangrove zone of the Western Arabian gulf has low ecological risk. ([[2019-alamran|AlAmran, 2019, p. 115]])

- PAHs are considered priority pollutants due to their toxic, carcinogenic, and mutagenic properties. ([[2019-alhaiqi|Alhaiqi, 2019, p. 22]])

- Microplastics were found at all sites, with fibers being the most common shape (96%). ([[2022-alshaikhali|Alshaikhali, 2022, p. 38]])

- Heavy metal concentrations in dust were significantly influenced by industrial activities and urban growth. ([[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024, p. 32]])
