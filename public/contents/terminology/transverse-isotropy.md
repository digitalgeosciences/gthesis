# Transverse Isotropy

**Summary**: A type of seismic anisotropy where properties vary with direction but are symmetric about a vertical axis.

---

- [[2015-own|Own, 2015]] evaluated transverse-isotropy to correct for its effects on hypocenter location accuracy.
