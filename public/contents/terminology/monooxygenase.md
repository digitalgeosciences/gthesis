# Monooxygenase

**Summary**: Enzymes that incorporate one oxygen atom from O2 into a substrate while reducing the other to water.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-almutawa|AlMutawa, 2024]] investigated monooxygenases as key enzymes for initiating hydrocarbon breakdown.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-almutawa|AlMutawa, 2024]] Crucial for the first step of hydrocarbon degradation by making them more soluble.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-almutawa|AlMutawa, 2024]] Screened using fluorescence assays.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Monooxygenases convert hydrophobic compounds into more hydrophilic forms like alcohols or ketones. ([[2024-almutawa|AlMutawa, 2024, p. 40]])
