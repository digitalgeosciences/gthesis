# Air Quality Index

**Summary**: A numerical scale used to communicate how polluted the air currently is and what associated
health risks it poses.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2025-al-afif|Al-Afif, 2025]] calculated AQI values for several pollutants using US EPA formulas.

## Scientific Context

- [[2025-al-afif|Al-Afif, 2025]] Used as a primary metric to assess and communicate environmental risk from industrial emissions.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2025-al-afif|Al-Afif, 2025]] Calculated using concentration breakpoints provided by the US EPA.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The study utilized an index between 0-500 (or 0-400 in some contexts) to categorize air quality from 'Good' to 'Hazardous'. ([[2025-al-afif|Al-Afif, 2025, p. 37]])
