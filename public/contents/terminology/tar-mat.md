# Tar Mat

**Summary**: A layer of heavy, viscous oil that can act as a barrier to water movement in a reservoir.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2011-al-dhufairi|AL-DHUFAIRI, 2011]] addressed the challenge of navigating tar mats using mobility profiles.

## Scientific Context

- [[2011-al-dhufairi|AL-DHUFAIRI, 2011]] Common in heavy oil fields, requiring specific geosteering strategies.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2011-al-dhufairi|AL-DHUFAIRI, 2011]] Identified via mobility profile differences.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Tar mats complicate geosteering because MWD tools cannot identify them; formation testing is required but expensive. ([[2011-al-dhufairi|AL-DHUFAIRI, 2011, p. 14]])
