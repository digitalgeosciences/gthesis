# Klinkenberg Effect

**Summary**: The phenomenon where gas molecules slip along the pore walls, increasing apparent permeability at low pressures.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2000-khan|Khan, 2000]] accounted for the Klinkenberg effect to correct for gas slippage in tight rocks.

## Scientific Context

- [[2000-khan|Khan, 2000]] Crucial for accurate permeability measurement in tight formations.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2000-khan|Khan, 2000]] Corrected using the Klinkenberg equation: $K_g = K_i(1 + b/P)$.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Neglecting this effect leads to significant errors in determining true permeability. ([[2000-khan|Khan, 2000, p. 23]])
