# Chromium Number

**Summary**: The ratio of chromium to (chromium plus aluminum) in a mineral, used as an indicator of melting.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023]] calculated Cr# values for clinopyroxene to determine the degree of partial melting.

## Scientific Context

- [[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023]] Used as an indicator for the extent of fractional melting.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023]] calculated from major element concentrations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Cr# ranges from 2.60 – 16.90 and have an average of 11.63 [for clinopyroxene]. ([[2023-iskandar|Iskandar, 2023, p. 75]])
