# Pollution Load Index

**Summary**: An index used to evaluate the cumulative impact of heavy metal contamination in a specific area.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024]] calculated the pollution-load-index (PLI) for HMs.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024]] Used to compare contamination levels across different geographic regions.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024]] calculated as the product of individual concentration factors (Cf).

## Key Findings Across Sources

- The highest PLI values were found in Jeddah, Dammam, and Khobar, indicating high accumulation from traffic emissions. ([[2024-alenazi|Alenazi, 2024, p. 139]])
