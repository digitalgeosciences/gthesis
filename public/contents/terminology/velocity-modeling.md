# Velocity Modeling

**Summary**: The process of creating a model of the earth's seismic velocity to improve imaging.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2018-mohamed|Mohamed, 2018]] performed velocity modeling using FDelmodc and SWI to refine the starting model for FWI.

## Scientific Context

- [[2018-mohamed|Mohamed, 2018]] Accurate velocity models are critical for overcoming signal degradation caused by complex near-surface geology.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2018-mohamed|Mohamed, 2018]] Iterative inversion and manual picking.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- SWI provided more accurate velocity at top 200m, leading to improved RTM images. ([[2018-mohamed|Mohamed, 2018, p. 46]])
