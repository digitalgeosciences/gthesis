# Foam Quality

**Summary**: The volume fraction of the foam that contains gas.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2016]] evaluated the impact of foam quality on mobility and stability in porous media.

- [[2019-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2019]] evaluated foam quality to find an optimal balance between stability and recovery.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2016]] Determines the transition between different flow regimes and the effectiveness of mobility reduction.

- [[2019-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2019]] evaluated foam quality to find an optimal balance between stability and recovery.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2016]] Calculated as (gas volume / (gas volume + liquid volume)) * 100%.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- A certain range of foam quality exists (40 to 95%) within which foam is able to reduce mobility. ([[2016-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2016, p. 25]])
- [[2016-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2016]] 0.70, 0.80, 0.90, 0.95

- A foam quality of 0.8 was found to be the optimum, while 0.9 led to 'dry foam' with poor stability. ([[2019-abdelaal|Abdelaal, 2019, p. 105]])
