# Pvbt

**Summary**: The amount of fluid required to reach the first point of pressure drop reduction due to wormhole growth.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2020-aly|Aly, 2020]] calculated PVBT values for both radial and linear acidizing experiments.

## Scientific Context

- [[2020-aly|Aly, 2020]] PVBT is used to determine the optimal injection rate for acid treatment.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2020-aly|Aly, 2020]] calculated from pressure drop data using the Buijse & Glasbergen (2005) model.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Block ASW-001 was assumed to be at the optimum injection rate with a PVBT of 0.232. ([[2020-aly|Aly, 2020, p. 125]])
