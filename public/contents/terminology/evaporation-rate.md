# Evaporation Rate

**Summary**: The loss of water from a surface due to phase change into vapor.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

## Scientific Context

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-armoh|Armoh, 2016]] Direct measurement using a portable chamber.

## Key Findings Across Sources
