# Evaporation Rate

**Summary**: The rate at which water is lost from a surface into the atmosphere.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2016-armoh|Armoh, 2016]] measured evaporation rate to determine the impact of environmental factors on water loss.

## Scientific Context

- [[2016-armoh|Armoh, 2016]] Critical for predicting water yields and managing salt production viability.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2016-armoh|Armoh, 2016]] Measured using a portable chamber and compared with pan evaporation.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Average annual evaporation was 145 mm, significantly lower than pan evaporation (3590 mm/year) due to salt crust formation and soil texture. ([[2016-armoh|Armoh, 2016, p. 47]])
