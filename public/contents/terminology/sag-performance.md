# Sag Performance

**Summary**: The tendency of weighting agents to settle out of suspension in a fluid.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2017-mohamed|MOHAMED, 2017]] evaluated sag performance across a range of temperatures up to 312°F.

## Scientific Context

- [[2017-mohamed|MOHAMED, 2017]] Critical for ensuring that weighting agents remain evenly distributed in completion fluids under high temperature conditions.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2017-mohamed|MOHAMED, 2017]] Comparing density measurements at the top and bottom of a sample over time.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Sag performance was acceptable until ~212°F, after which Xanthan polymer degradation caused significant settling. ([[2017-mohamed|MOHAMED, 2017, p. 64]])
- [[2017-mohamed|MOHAMED, 2017]] Acceptable range defined as 0.5 to 0.53.
