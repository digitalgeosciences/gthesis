# Quality Assessment

**Summary**: The evaluation of the clarity and accuracy of an image, often compared to a reference.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-mohammed|Mohammed, 2015]] developed non-reference methods for assessing seismic image quality.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-mohammed|Mohammed, 2015]] Crucial for determining the best imaging algorithm when no ground truth is available.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-mohammed|Mohammed, 2015]] Using entropy-based metrics (SENSE) or curvelet features.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- SENSE (spatial/spectral entropy) and curvelet features provide objective metrics for assessment. ([[2015-mohammed|Mohammed, 2015, p. 14]])
