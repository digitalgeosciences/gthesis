# Well Placement

**Summary**: The strategic positioning of injection and production wells within a reservoir.

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-sibaweihi|Sibaweihi, 2013]] optimized well placement using CMA-ES and DE algorithms to maximize NPV.

- [[2013-ahmed|AHMED, 2013]] addressed well placement as a critical step in field development plans.

- [[2014-al-ismael|AL-ISMAEL, 2014]] optimized well placement using a multi-objective approach to balance NPV and pressure.

- [[2015-khan|KHAN, 2015]] optimized well placement (WPO) by incorporating minimum spacing constraints into the PSO algorithm.

## Scientific Context

- [[2013-sibaweihi|Sibaweihi, 2013]] Optimal well placement ensures maximum contact with the reservoir and minimizes early breakthrough.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Well placement optimization was performed for both Case I (mature) and Case II (newly discovered) fields. ([[2013-sibaweihi|Sibaweihi, 2013, p. 40]])


- Well placement is one of the most important steps; it can be optimized using stochastic algorithms rather than manual methods. ([[2013-ahmed|AHMED, 2013, p. 31]])

- Optimized placements were compared against standard patterns (Pattern 1) to evaluate performance. ([[2014-al-ismael|AL-ISMAEL, 2014, p. 40]])

- Well placement is critical because errors in horizontal well selection are costly to correct during field development. ([[2015-khan|KHAN, 2015, p. 51]])
