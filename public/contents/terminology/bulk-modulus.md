# Bulk Modulus

**Summary**: A measure of a material's resistance to uniform compression.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2015-barri|Barri, 2015]] monitored bulk modulus changes during the stimulation process.

## Scientific Context

- [[2015-barri|Barri, 2015]] Indicates the rock's ability to resist compression without deformation.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2015-barri|Barri, 2015]] Derived from P-wave and S-wave velocities.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Austin Chalk samples showed significant increases in bulk modulus, indicating they became less capable of resisting deformation. ([[2015-barri|Barri, 2015, p. 182]])
