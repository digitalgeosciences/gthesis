# Liquid Loading

**Summary**: The accumulation of liquids in a gas well, which increases backpressure and reduces production.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-moh-ali|Moh-Ali, 2024]] addressed the issue of liquid loading by using TCF to generate heat and pressure to lift fluids.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-moh-ali|Moh-Ali, 2024]] Liquid loading is a common problem in aging gas wells that limits production rates.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-moh-ali|Moh-Ali, 2024]] Analyzed via flow loop experiments and PIPESIM simulations.

## Key Findings Across Sources

- Injection of TCF in gas wells suffering from liquid loading showed promising results by removing more than 90% of the liquid holdup. ([[2024-moh-ali|Moh-Ali, 2024, p. 13]])
