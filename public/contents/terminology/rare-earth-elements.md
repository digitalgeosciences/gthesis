# Rare Earth Elements

**Summary**: A series of 17 chemical elements in the periodic table, from lanthanum to lutetium.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] analyzed REE enrichment within the Al-Ghurayyah pluton.

## Scientific Context

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] REE enrichment is a key indicator for economic potential in rare metal deposits.

## Measurement and Characterization Methods

- [[2024-sulistyohariyanto|Sulistyohariyanto, 2024]] measured via whole rock geochemistry and mineral chemistry analysis.
