# Porous Media

**Summary**: A material containing pores, such as rock or soil, used to model fluid flow in reservoirs.

---

## How It Appears in the Thesis Collection

- [[2013-al-mutairi|AL-MUTAIRI, 2013]] modeled the movement of fluids through a porous-medium.

- [[2013-khan-2|Khan, 2013]] studied the behavior of slightly compressible fluids in porous media using fractional diffusion.

- [[2014-jamil|JAMIL, 2014]] modeled the flow of CO2 through porous media to account for viscous and inertial resistances.

- [[2015-alloush|Alloush, 2015]] studied gas flow in porous media using modified diffusivity equations.

- [[2016-siddiqui|Siddiqui, 2016]] investigated foam behavior within a sandstone reservoir model.

- [[2017-olabemiwo|Olabemiwo, 2017]] used fly ash as a porous medium to act as an adsorbent for cadmium removal.

- [[2018-elsaieed|ElSaieed, 2018]] modeled flow through porous media using Darcy's law and Stokes-Brinkman equations.

- [[2018-jamal|Jamal, 2018]] modeled the interaction between free-flow zones and porous media using the Brinkman equation.

- [[1992-al-towailib-2|Al-Towailib, 1992]] analyzed two-phase flow through chokes as a porous-media problem.
